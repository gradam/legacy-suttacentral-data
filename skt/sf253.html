Saṅgītisūtra

a evaṃ mayā śrutaṃ |

ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavān malleṣu janapadeṣu caryāṃ caran pāpām anuprāptaḥ pāpāyāṃ viharati jalūkāvanaṣaṇḍe |

b tena khalu samayena pāpīyakair mallair antarā ca … pūrvām antarā ca paścimāṃ pāpām atrāntarodbhūtake caitye navasaṃsthāgāro ’cirakārito ’ciraniṣṭhito ’cirābhyuṣitaḥ śramaṇena vā brāhmaṇena vā mallena vā mallakumāreṇa vā kenacid vā loke manuṣyabhūtena |

c aśrauṣuḥ pāpīyakā mallā bhagavān malleṣu janapadeṣu caryāṃ caran pāpām anuprāptaḥ pāpāyāṃ viharati jalūkāvanaṣaṇḍe |

d atha ca sāmīcīpratipannānāṃ mallānām etad abhavat | asmākam udbhūtake caitye navasaṃsthāgāro ’cirakārito ’ciraniṣṭhito ’cirabhyuṣitaḥ śramaṇena vā brāhmaṇena vā mallena vā mallakumāreṇa vā kenacid vā loke manuṣyabhūtena | taṃ bhagavān prathamataḥ paribhuṃjīta bhikṣusaṃghaś ca | sa bhagavatā prathamataḥ paribhukto bhikṣusaṃghena ca te vayaṃ kṛtāgrāḥ kṛtākuśalā labdhodayā labdhalābhās tataḥ paścāt paribhokṣyāmahe | tad asmākam bhaviṣyati dīrgharātram arthāya hitāya sukhāya |

e atha pāpīyakā mallāḥ saṃghāt saṃghaṃ pūgāt pūgaṃ saṃgamya samāgamya pāpāyā niṣkramya yena bhagavāṃs tenopajagmuḥ | upetya bhagavatpādau śirasā vanditvā tripradakṣiṇīkṛtvaikānte nyaṣīdan |

f ekāntaniṣaṇṇān pāpīyakān mallān bhagavān dhārmyā kathayā sandarśayati samādāpayati samuttejayati saṃpraharṣayati | atha bhagavān pāpīyakān mallān anekaparyāyeṇa dhārmyā kathayā sandarśayitvā samādāpayitvā samuttejayitvā saṃpraharṣayitvā tūṣṇīm abhūt |

g atha pāpīyakā mallā bhagavato bhāṣitam abhinandyānumodyotthāyāsanād ekāṃsam uttarāsaṅgaṃ kṛtvā yena bhagavāṃs tenāñjaliṃ praṇamya bhagavantam idam avocan |

h asmākam antarā ca … pūrvām antarā ca paścimāṃ pāpām atrāntarodbhūtake caitye navasaṃsthāgāro ’cirakārito ’ciraniṣṭhito ’cirābhyuṣitaḥ śramaṇena vā brāhmaṇena vā mallena vā mallakumāreṇa vā kenacid vā loke manuṣyabhūtena | taṃ bhagavān prathamataḥ paribhuṃjīta bhikṣusaṃghaś ca | sa bhagavatā prathamataḥ paribhukto bhikṣusaṃghena ca te vayaṃ kṛtāgrāḥ kṛtakuśalā labdhodayā labdhalābhās tataḥ paścāt paribhokṣyāmahe | tad asmākaṃ bhaviṣyati dīrgharātram arthāya hitāya sukhāya |

i adhivāsayati bhagavān pāpīyakānāṃ mallānāṃ tūṣṇīṃbhāvena |

j atha pāpīyakā mallā bhagavatas tūṣṇīṃbhāvenādhivāsanāṃ viditvā bhagavatpādau śirasā vanditvā bhagavato ’ntikāt prakrāntāḥ |

k atha pāpīyakā mallās tenaiva divasāvaśeṣeṇa śakaṭaiḥ … saṃsthāgāraṃ s …tailapradīpān āropya tān ādīpya yena bhagavāṃs tenopajagmuḥ | upetya bhagavatpādau śirasā vanditvā tripradakṣaṇīkṛtvā yena bhagavāṃs tenāñjaliṃ praṇamya bhagavantam idam avocan |

l sarvākārasaṃskṛto bhadanta navasaṃsthāgāraudakamaṇayaḥ pratiṣṭhāpitās tailapradīpā āropitāḥ | yastyedānīṃ bhagavān kālaṃ manyate |

m atha bhagavān bhikṣusaṃghaparivṛto bhikṣusaṃghapuraskṛto yena pāpīyakānāṃ mallānāṃ saṃsthāgāraṃs tenopajagāma | upetya bahiḥ saṃsthāgārasya pādau prakṣālya saṃsthāgāraṃ praviśya sarvaṃ saṃsthāgāraṃ vyavalokya madhyamaṃ staṃbhaṃ niśṛtya niṣaṇṇaḥ pūrvāṃmukhaḥ |

bhikṣusaṃgho ’pi bahiḥ saṃsthāgārasya pādau prakṣālya saṃsthāgāraṃ praviśya sarvaṃ saṃsthāgāraṃ vyavalokya paścimāṃ bhittiṃ niśṛtya niṣaṇṇaḥ pūrvāṃmukho bhagavantaṃ saṃpuraskṛtya |

pāpīyakā mallā bahiḥ saṃsthāgārasya pādau prakṣālya saṃsthāgāraṃ praviśya sarvaṃ saṃsthāgāraṃ vyavalokya pūrvāṃ bhittiṃ niśṛtya niṣaṇṇāḥ paścānmukhā bhagavantaṃ puraskṛtya bhikṣusaṃghaṃ ca |

n atha bhagavān pāpīyakān mallān dhārmyākathayā sandarśayati samādāpayati samuttejayati saṃpraharṣayati | anekaparyāyeṇa dhārmyā kathayā saṃdarśayitvā samādāpayitvā samuttejayitvā saṃpraharṣayitvodyojayati |

o atikrānto rātryāḥ prathamo yāmaḥ | yasyedānīṃ kālaṃ manyadhve |

p evaṃ bhadanteti pāpīyakā mallā bhagavataḥ pratiśrutyotthāyāsanād bhagavatpādau śirasā vanditvā bhagavato ’ntikāt prakrāntāḥ |

q atha bhagavān aciraprakrāntān pāpīyakān mallān viditvāyuṣmantaṃ śāriputram āmantrayate |

r pratibhātu te śāriputra bhikṣūṇāṃ dhārmī kathā | pṛṣṭhī ma āvilāyati tāṃ tāvad āyamayiṣye |

s adhivāsayaty āyuṣmāñ śāriputras tūṣṇīṃbhāvena |

t atha bhagavāṃś caturguṇaṃ uttarāsaṅgaṃ prajñapya gaṇaguṇāṃ saṃghāṭiṃ śirasy upadhāya dakṣiṇena pārśvena śayyāṃ kalpayati pāde pādam ādhāyālokasaṃjñī smṛtaḥ saṃprajāna utthānasaṃjñāṃ manasi kurvāṇaḥ |

u tatrāyuṣmāṃś śāriputro bhikṣūn āmantrayate |

v nirgrantho jñātiputraḥ kālakṛtaḥ pāpāyāṃ | tasya kālakriyayā nirgranthā jñātiputrikā bhinnāḥ kalahakārakā viharanti bhaṇḍanakārakā vigṛhītā vivādam āpannāḥ |

w naitaṃ tvaṃ dharmavinayam ājānāsi | aham etaṃ dharmavinayam ājānāmi | kim etaṃ dharmavinayaṃ tvam ājānāsi | … sahitaṃ mama | asahitaṃ tava | pūrvaṃ vacanīyaṃ paścād avocaḥ | paścād vacanīyaṃ pūrvam avocaḥ | abhicīrṇaṃ te … apahara vādaṃ vāda vipramokṣāya …

x ity anyo ’nyaṃ mukhaśaktikayā vitudanto viharanti | …

ye ’pi nirgranthasya jñātiputrasya śrāvakā gṛhiṇo ’vadātavasanā pāpāyāṃ prativasaṃti te ’pi nirgrantheṣu jñātiputrikeṣu bhinneṣu vivādam āpanneṣu kalahajāteṣu bhaṇḍanajāteṣu vigṛhīteṣu nirviṇṇarūpāḥ prativāṇirūpā asamānarūpā apakrāntā yathā dharmavinayād durākhyātād duṣprativeditād aniryāṇikād asaṃbodhagāmino bhinnād asaṃstūpād apratiśaraṇāt |

y ayaṃ khalu tathāgato ’rhan samyaksaṃbuddho ’smākaṃ śāstā | svākhyātaś cāyaṃ dharma vinayaḥ supravedito niryāṇikaḥ saṃbodhagāmy abhinnaḥ saṃstūpaḥ sapratiśaraṇaḥ | śāstā cāsya tathāgato ’rhan samyaksaṃbuddhaḥ |

z te vayaṃ saṃhitāḥ samagrāḥ saṃmodamānā bhūtvā samśayāya na vivādāmahe yathedaṃ brahmacaryaṃ cirasthitikaṃ syāt | tad bhaviṣyati bahujanahitāya bahujanasukhāya lokānukaṃpāyārthāya hitāya sukhāya devamanuṣyāṇāṃ |

1.1 eka āyuṣmanto dharmo bhagavatā svayam abhijñāyābhisaṃbudhyākhyāto

yaduta sarvasatvā āhārasthitayaḥ | taṃ vayaṃ saṃhitāḥ samagrāḥ saṃmodamānā bhūtvā saṃśayāya na vivadāmahe | yathedaṃ brahmacaryaṃ cirasthitikaṃ syāt | tad bhaviṣyati bahujanahitāya bahujanasukhāya lokānukaṃpāyārthāya hitāya sukhāya devamanuṣyāṇām ||

1.2 yaduta sarvasatvāḥ saṃskārasthāyinaḥ |

1.3 apramādaḥ kuśaleṣu dharmeṣu |

antaroddānam ||
āhārasthitayaḥ satvāḥ atha saṃskārasthāyinaḥ |
apramādaḥ kuśaleṣu dharmeṣv ekakāḥ punar udditāḥ ||

2.1 dvāv āyuṣmanto dharmau bhagavatā svayam abhijñāyābhisaṃbudhyākhyātau

yaduta nāmaṃ ca rūpaṃ ca | tau vayaṃ saṃhitāḥ samagrāḥ saṃmodamānā bhūtvā pūrvavad yāvad devamanuṣyāṇām ||

2.22 dṛṣṭiviśuddhiś ca tathā dṛṣṭasya yoniśaḥ pradhānam |

2.23 saṃvejanīyeṣu dharmeṣu saṃvegaḥ tathāsaṃvignasya ca yoniśaḥ pradhānam |

2.24 asaṃtuṣṭitā ca kuśaleṣu dharmeṣv aprativāṇitā ca pradhāne |

2.25 śamathaś ca vipaśyanā ca |

2.26 vidyā ca vimuktiś ca |

2.27 kṣayajñānaṃ cānutpādajñānaṃ ca |

antaroddānam | … tiyaṃ padaṃ bhavatṛṣṇāhrīkye ca hrīś cā …

3.1 traya āyuṣmanto dharmā bhagavatā svayam abhijñāyābhisaṃbudhyākhyātāḥ |

yaduta trīṇi duścaritāni | kāyaduścaritaṃ vāgduścaritaṃ manoduścaritam |

tān vayaṃ saṃhitāḥ samagrāḥ saṃmodamānā bhūtvā pūrvavad yāvad devamanuṣyāṇāṃ ||

3.3 trīṇy akuśalamūlāni | lobho ’kuśalamūlaṃ dveṣo moho ’kuśalamūlaṃ |

3.6 trayaḥ kuśalavitarkāḥ | naiṣkramyavitarkaḥ avyāpādavitarkaḥ avihiṃsāvitarkaḥ |

3.30.1 pariśuddhakāyasamudācāras tathāgato nāsti tathāgatānām apariśuddhakāyasamudācāratā yāṃ tathāgataḥ praticchādayet kaccin me pare na vijānīyuḥ |

3.34 tisraḥ kāmopapattayaḥ | katamās tisraḥ |

3.34.1 santi satvāḥ pratyupasthitakāmā ye pratyupasthitaiḥ kāmair aiśvaryaṃ vaśe vartayanti tadyathā manuṣyā ekatyaś ca devāḥ | iyaṃ prathamā kāmopapattiḥ |

3.34.2 santi satvā nirmitakāmā ye nirmiya nirmiya kāmair aiśvaryaṃ vaśe vartayanti tadyathā devā nirmāṇaratayaḥ | iyaṃ dvitīyā kāmopapattiḥ |

3.34.3 santi satvāḥ parinirmitakāmā ye parinirmitaiḥ kāmaiḥ aiśvaryaṃ vaśe vartayanti tadyathā devāḥ parinirmitavaśavartinaḥ | iyaṃ tṛtīyā kāmopapattiḥ |

3.35 tisras sukhopapattayaḥ

3.35.1.a santi satvāḥ ya imam eva kāyaṃ vivekajena prītisukhenābhiṣyandayanti pariṣyandayanti pariprīṇayanti parispharanti | nāsty eṣāṃ kiñcit sarvataḥ kāyād asphuṭaṃ bhavati spharaṇīyaṃ yaduta vivekajena prītisukhena |

3.35.1.b te tena sukhena tena saumanasyena dīrghāyuṣo dīrgham adhvānaṃ tiṣṭhanti tadyathā devā brahmakāyikāḥ | iyaṃ prathamā sukhopapattiḥ |

3.35.2.a santi satvā ya imam eva kāyaṃ samādhijena prītisukhenābhiṣyandayanti pariṣyandayanti pariprīṇayanti parispharanti | nāsty eṣāṃ kiñcit sarvataḥ kāyād asphuṭaṃ bhavati spharaṇīyaṃ yaduta samādhijena prītisukhena |

3.35.2.b te tena sukhena tena saumanasyena dīrghāyuṣo dīrgham adhvānaṃ tiṣṭhanti tadyathā devā ābhāsvarāḥ | iyaṃ dvitiyā sukhopapattiḥ |

3.35.3.a santi satvā ya imam eva kāyaṃ niṣprītikena sukhenābhiṣyandayanti pariṣyandayanti pariprīṇayanti parispharanti | nāsty eṣāṃ kiñcit sarvataḥ kāyād asphuṭaṃ bhavati spharaṇīyaṃ yaduta niṣprītikena sukhena |

3.35.3.b te tena sukhena tena saumanasyena dīrghāyuṣo dīrgham adhvānaṃ tiṣṭhanti tadyathā devāḥ śubhakṛtsnāḥ | iyaṃ tṛitīyā sukhopapattiḥ |
