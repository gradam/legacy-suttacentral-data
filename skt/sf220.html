Catuṣpariṣatsūtra (1st)

1.1 atha dvayor brahmakāyikayor devatayor brahmaloke nivāsinor etad abhavat |

1.2 buddho bhagavān urubilvāyāṃ viharati nadyā nairañjanāyās tīre prathamābhisaṃbuddhas tejodhātusamādhiṃ samāpannaḥ | buddho bhagavān saptāham ekaparyaṅkeṇātinamayati sa na kenacit piṅḍkena pratipāditaḥ | yannv āvāṃ gatvā pratyedapratīkṣāṃ gāthāṃ bhāṣevahi |

1.3 atha dve brahmakāyike devate tadyathā balavān puruṣaḥ saṃmiñjitaṃ bāhuṃ prasārayet prasāritaṃ vā saṃmiñjayed evam eva dve brahmakāyike devate brahmaloke antarhite bhagavataḥ purataḥ pratyasthātām |

1.4 athaikā brahmakāyikā devatā gāthāṃ babhāṣe |

sārthavāhānigha vicara loke |

dharmaratnasyājñātāraḥ ||

2.1 vimuktiprītisukhapratisaṃvidī buddho bhagavān saptāham ekaparyaṅkeṇātināmayati sa na kenāpi piṇḍakena pratipāditaḥ |

2.2 tena khalu samayena tripusabhallikau vaṇijau pañcamātraiḥ śakaṭaśataiḥ sārdhaṃ tasminn eva mārge ’dhvapratipannau babhuvatuḥ |

2.3 atha tripusabhallikayor vaṇijoḥ pūrvamitrāmātyajñātisālo hitāyā devatāyā etad abhavat |

2.4 ayaṃbuddho bhagavān urubilvāyāṃ viharati nadyā nairañjanāyās tīre bodhimūle ’cirābhisaṃbuddho vimuktiprītisukhapratisaṃvedī | buddho bhagavān saptādam ekaparyaṅkeṇātināmayati sa na kenacit piṇḍakena pratipāditaḥ | tatra yat vaṇijau taṃ prathamataḥ piṇḍakena pratipādayato bhaviṣyati tat tripusabhallika you vaṇijor dīrgharātram arthāya hitāya sukhāya | yannv ahaṃ svayam evautsukyam āpadyeyaṃ piṇḍapātapratipadanāya |

2.5 atha sā devatā śakaṭavaṇija udāre ṇāvabhāsena spharitvā tripusabhallikau baṇijāv idam avocat |

2.6 vaṇijau vaṇijāv ayaṃ buddho bhagavān urubilvāyāṃ viharati nadyā nairañjanāyās tire bodhimūle ’cirābhisaṃbuddho vimuktiprītisukhapratisaṃvedī | buddho bhagavān saptāham ekaparyaṅkeṇātināmayati sa na dencit pi.ḍakena pratipāditaḥ | tatra yuvāṃ taṃ prathamataḥ piṇḍakena pratipādayataṃ tad yuṣmādaṃ bhaviṣyati dīrgharātram arthāya hitāya sukhāya |

2.7 atha tripusabhallikayor vaṇijor etad abhavat | na batāvaro buddho bhaviṣyati nāvaraṃ dharmākhyānaṃ yad idānīm īdṛśo devatā apy autsudyam āpadyante tasya tathāgatasyārahataḥ samyaksaṃbuddhasya piṇḍapātapratipādanāya |

2.8 atha tripusabhallikau vaṇijau prabhūtaṃ manthaṃ ca madhupiṇḍam ādāya yena bhagavāṃs tenopajagmutuḥ |

2.9 upetya bhagavatpādau śirasā vanditvaikānte ’sthātām | ekāntasthitau tripusabhallikau vaṇijau bhagavantam idam avocatām |

2.10 āvābhyāṃ bhadanta bhagavantam uddiśya piṇḍapāta ānītaḥ prabhūtaṃ madhupiṇḍaḥ | taṃ bhagavān pratigṛhṇātv anukṃpām upādāya |

2.11 atha bhagavata etad abhavat | saced ahaṃ svahastābhyāṃ piṇḍapātaṃ pratigrahīṣyāmi tīrthikavat tan na mama pratirūpaṃ syāt | yannv ahaṃ cintayeyaṃ yasmin pūrvakaiḥ samyaksaṃbuddhaiḥ piṇḍapātaḥ pratigṛhīto hitāya prāṇinām |

2.12 devatā bhagavata ārocayanti | pātre bhadanta pūrvakaiḥ samyaksaṃbuddhaiḥ piṇḍapātaḥ pratigṛhīto hitāya prāṇinām | bhagavato ’pi ..naṃ pravartate jñānadarśanaṃ pātre pūrvakaiḥ samyaksaṃbuddhaiḥ piṇḍapātaḥ pratigṛhīto hitāya prāṇinām iti |

3.2 upetya bhagavatpādau śirasā vanditvaikānti tasthuḥ |

3.5 atha bhagavata etad abhavat | saced aham ekasya mahārājñaḥ pātraṃ pratigrahīṣyāmi trayāṇāṃ bhaviṣyaty anyathātvam | saced dvayos trayāṇāṃ pratigrahīṣyaty anyathātvam | yannv ahaṃ caturṇāṃ mahārājñāṃ pātrāṇi pratigṛhyaikaṃ pātram adhimucyeyam |

3.6 atha bhagavāṃś caturṇāṃ mahārājñāṃ pātrāṇy pratigṛhyaikaṃ pātram adhimuktavān |

3.7 tatra bhagavatā tripusabhallikayor vaṇijoḥ piṇḍapātaḥ pratigṛhīto hitāya prāṇinām |

3.8 tatra abhagavāṃs tripusabhallikau vaṇijāv āmantrayate |

3.9 etāv āvāṃ bhadanta buddhaṃ śaraṇaṃ gacchāvo dharmaṃ śaraṇaṃ gacchāvo yo ’sau bhaviṣyaty anāgate ’dhvani saṃgho nāma tam apy āvāṃ śaraṇaṃ gacchāvaḥ |

3.10 atha bhagavāṃs tripusabhallikayor vaṇijos tad dānam anayābhyanumodanayābbyanumidate |

21.2 divyā ye ca mānuṣāḥ | carata bhikṣavaś caryāṃ bahujanahitāya bahujanasukhāya lokānukampāyārthāya hitāya sukhāya devamanuṣyāṇām | mā dvāv ekena gamiṣyatha | aham apy urubilvāyāṃ senāyanagrāmakaṃ gamiṣyāmīti | yannv ahaṃ tam upasaṅkrameyaṃ vicakṣuḥkarmane |

21.3 atha māraḥ pāpīyān māṇavakavarṇam ātmānam abhinirmiya yena bhagavāṃs tenopajagāma |

21.4 upetya tasyāṃ velāyāṃ gāthāṃ babhāṣe |

amukto manyase mukto
muktaḥ kiṃ tava cintitam |
mahābandhanabaddho ’si
na me śramaṇa mokṣyase ||

21.5 atha bhagavata etad abhavat | māro ’yaṃ pāpīyān mām upasaṅkrānto vicakṣuḥkarmane | iti viditvā bhagavān tasyāṃ velāyāṃ gāthāṃ bābhāṣe |

mukto ’haṃ sarvapāśdbhyo
ye divyā ye ca mānuṣāḥ |
jānīhī pāpīyann evaṃ
hato ’si paripanthaka ||

21.6 atha mārāsya pāpīyasyaitad abhavat | jānāti śramaṇo gautamaś cetasā cetaḥparyāyam | iti viditvā duḥkhī durmanā vipratisārī tatraivāntarhitaḥ |

21.7 punar api bhagavān bhikṣūn āmantrayate | mukto ’haṃ bhikṣavaḥ sarvapāśebhyo ye divyā ye ca mānuṣāḥ | yūyam api muktāḥ sarvapāśebbhyo ye divyā ye ca mānuṣāḥ | carata bhikṣavaś caryāṃ bahujanahitāya bahujanasukhāya likānukampāyārthāya hitāya sukhāya devmanuṣyāṇām | mā ddvāv ekena gamiṣyatha | aham apy urubilvāyāṃ senāyanagrāmakaṃ gamiṣyāmi |

21.8 evaṃ bhadanteti bhikṣavo bhagavataḥ pratiśrutya janapaduṣu caryāṃ prakrāntāḥ |
