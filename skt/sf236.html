Candrasūtra

1 evaṃ mayā śrutam | ekasamayaṃ bhagavāṃ caṃpāyāṃ viharati gargarapuṣkariṇyās tīre |

2 tena khalu samayena rāhuṇā asurendreṇa sarvaṃ candramaṇḍalam āvṛtaṃ |

3 atha yā devatā tasmiṃ candramaṇḍale sthitā bhītā trastā saṃvignā hṛṣṭaromakūpā yena bhagavāṃs tenopajagāma | upetya bhagavatpādau śirasā vanditvaikānte ’sthāt | ekāntasthitā bhagavaṃtaṃ gāthayā babhāṣe |

4 namas te buddha vīrāstu vipramuktāya savataḥ |
saṃbādhapratipanno ’smi tasya me śaraṇaṃ bhava ||

5 loke sugatam arhantaṃ candramāḥ śaraṇaṃ gataḥ |
rāhoś candramasaṃ muñca buddhā lokānukaṃpakāḥ ||

6 bhagavāṃ gāthayā pratyābhāṣīt |

yo hy andhakāre nabhasi
prabhākaro vairocano … |

rāho śaśāṅkaṃ grasa māntarīkṣe
prajāpradīpaṃ tvaritaṃ vimuñca ||

7 … | saṃsvinnagātro vyathitaḥ saṃbādhito yathā naraḥ | saṃtvaramāṇo ’sau rāhuś candramaṇḍalam utsṛṣṭaḥ ||

8 tena samayena baḍir virocanapautra āsīd asuraḥ | adrākṣīd baḍir virocanapautro rāhuṇā asurendreṇa tvaritatvaritaṃ candramaṇḍalam utsṛṣṭam dṛṣṭvā ca tasyāṃ velāyāṃ gāthāṃ babhāṣe ||

9 kiṃ nu saṃtvaramāṇas tvaṃ rāhuś candraṃ vimuñcasi | saṃsvinnagātro vyathitaḥ saṃbādhito yathā naraḥ ||

10 rāhur avocat || saptadhā me sphalen mūrdhā jīvan na sukham āpnuyāṃ tatra buddhābhigītena muñceyaṃ śaśinaṃ na cet ||

11 baḍir virocanapautra gāthayā babhāṣe ||

… madarśinaḥ saṃbuddhāḥ … |
teṣāṃ gāthābhigītena rāhuś candraṃ vimuñcati ||

12 candrasūtraṃ samāptam ||
