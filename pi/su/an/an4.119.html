Aṅguttara Nikāya 4

12. Kesivagga

119. Paṭhamabhayasutta

“Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, bhayāni. Katamāni cattāri? Jātibhayaṃ, jarābhayaṃ, byādhibhayaṃ, maraṇabhayaṃ–  imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri bhayānī”ti.

Navamaṃ.
