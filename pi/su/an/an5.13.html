Aṅguttara Nikāya 5

2. Balavagga

13. Saṅkhittasutta

“Pañcimāni, bhikkhave, balāni. Katamāni pañca? Saddhābalaṃ, vīriyabalaṃ, satibalaṃ, samādhibalaṃ, paññābalaṃ–  imāni kho, bhikkhave, pañca balānī”ti.

Tatiyaṃ.
