Saṃyutta Nikāya 22

15. Diṭṭhivagga

157. Abhinivesasutta

Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Kismiṃ nu kho, bhikkhave, sati, kiṃ upādāya, kiṃ abhinivissa uppajjanti saṃyo­janā­bhini­vesa­vini­bandhā”ti? Bhagavaṃmūlakā no, bhante, dhammā … pe … “rūpe kho, bhikkhave, sati, rūpaṃ upādāya, rūpaṃ abhinivissa uppajjanti saṃyo­janā­bhini­vesa­vini­bandhā. Vedanāya sati … saññāya sati … saṅkhāresu sati … viññāṇe sati, viññāṇaṃ upādāya, viññāṇaṃ abhinivissa uppajjanti saṃyo­janā­bhini­vesa­vini­bandhā. Taṃ kiṃ maññatha, bhikkhave, rūpaṃ niccaṃ vā aniccaṃ vā”ti? “Aniccaṃ, bhante”. “Yaṃ panāniccaṃ … pe … api nu taṃ anupādāya uppajjeyyuṃ saṃyo­janā­bhini­vesa­vini­bandhā”ti? “No hetaṃ, bhante” … pe … “evaṃ passaṃ … pe … nāparaṃ itthattāyāti pajānātī”ti.

Aṭṭhamaṃ.
