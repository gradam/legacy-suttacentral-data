Saṃyutta Nikāya 1

8. Chetvāvagga

73. Vittasutta

“Kiṃsūdha vittaṃ purisassa seṭṭhaṃ,
Kiṃsu suciṇṇo sukhamāvahati;
Kiṃsu have sādutaraṃ rasānaṃ,
Kathaṃjīviṃ jīvitamāhu seṭṭhan”ti.

“Saddhīdha vittaṃ purisassa seṭṭhaṃ,
Dhammo suciṇṇo sukhamāvahati;
Saccaṃ have sādutaraṃ rasānaṃ,
Paññājīviṃ jīvitamāhu seṭṭhan”ti.

Pali text from the Mahāsaṅgīti Tipiṭaka Buddhavasse 2500: World Tipiṭaka Edition in Roman Script. Edited and published by The M.L. Maniratana Bunnag Dhamma Society Fund, 2005.
