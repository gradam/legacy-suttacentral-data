Saṃyutta Nikāya 1

6. Jarāvagga

53. Mittasutta

“Kiṃsu pavasato mittaṃ,
kiṃsu mittaṃ sake ghare;
Kiṃ mittaṃ atthajātassa,
kiṃ mittaṃ samparāyikan”ti.

“Sattho pavasato mittaṃ,
mātā mittaṃ sake ghare;
Sahāyo atthajātassa,
hoti mittaṃ punappunaṃ;
Sayaṅkatāni puññāni,
taṃ mittaṃ samparāyikan”ti.

Pali text from the Mahāsaṅgīti Tipiṭaka Buddhavasse 2500: World Tipiṭaka Edition in Roman Script. Edited and published by The M.L. Maniratana Bunnag Dhamma Society Fund, 2005.
