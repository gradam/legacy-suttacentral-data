Saṃyutta Nikāya 52

2. Dutiyavagga

20. Indri­yapa­ropa­riyatta­sutta

“Imesañca panāhaṃ, āvuso, catunnaṃ satipaṭṭhānānaṃ bhāvitattā bahulīkatattā parasattānaṃ parapuggalānaṃ indriyaparopariyattaṃ yathābhūtaṃ pajānāmī”ti.

Dasamaṃ.
