Saṃyutta Nikāya 51

3. Ayoguḷavagga

25. Paṭhamaphalasutta

“Cattārome, bhikkhave, iddhipādā. Katame cattāro? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu chanda­samā­dhippa­dhāna­saṅkhā­rasa­mannā­gataṃ iddhipādaṃ bhāveti, vīriyasamādhi … pe … cittasamādhi … vīmaṃsā­samā­dhippa­dhāna­saṅkhā­rasa­mannā­gataṃ iddhipādaṃ bhāveti–  ime kho, bhikkhave, cattāro iddhipādā. Imesaṃ kho, bhikkhave, catunnaṃ iddhipādānaṃ bhāvitattā bahulīkatattā bhikkhunā dvinnaṃ phalānaṃ aññataraṃ phalaṃ pāṭikaṅkhaṃ–  diṭṭheva dhamme aññā, sati vā upādisese anāgāmitā”ti.

Pañcamaṃ.
