Saṃyutta Nikāya 42

1. Gāmaṇivagga

4. Hatthārohasutta

Atha kho hatthāroho gāmaṇi yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā … pe … ajjatagge pāṇupetaṃ saraṇaṃ gatanti.

Catutthaṃ.
