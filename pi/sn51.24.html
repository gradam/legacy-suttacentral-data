Saṃyutta Nikāya 51

3. Ayoguḷavagga

24. Suddhikasutta

“Cattārome, bhikkhave, iddhipādā. Katame cattāro? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu chanda­samā­dhippa­dhāna­saṅkhā­rasa­mannā­gataṃ iddhipādaṃ bhāveti, vīriyasamādhi … pe … cittasamādhi … vīmaṃsā­samā­dhippa­dhāna­saṅkhā­rasa­mannā­gataṃ iddhipādaṃ bhāveti–  ime kho, bhikkhave, cattāro iddhipādā”ti.

Catutthaṃ.
