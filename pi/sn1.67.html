Saṃyutta Nikāya 1

7. Addhavagga

67. Uḍḍitasutta

“Kenassu uḍḍito loko,
kenassu parivārito;
Kenassu pihito loko,
kismiṃ loko patiṭṭhito”ti.

“Taṇhāya uḍḍito loko,
jarāya parivārito;
Maccunā pihito loko,
dukkhe loko patiṭṭhito”ti.
