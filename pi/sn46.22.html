Saṃyutta Nikāya 46

3. Udāyivagga

22. Bojjhaṅgadesanāsutta

“Satta vo, bhikkhave, bojjhaṅge desessāmi; taṃ suṇātha. Katame ca, bhikkhave, satta bojjhaṅgā? Satisambojjhaṅgo … pe … upekkhāsambojjhaṅgo–  ime kho, bhikkhave, satta bojjhaṅgā”ti.

Dutiyaṃ.
