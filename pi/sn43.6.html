Saṃyutta Nikāya 43

1. Paṭhamavagga

6. Sammappadhānasutta

“Katamo ca, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo? Cattāro sammappadhānā. Ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo … pe ….

Chaṭṭhaṃ.
