Aṅguttara Nikāya 4

3. Uruvelavagga

25. Brahmacariyasutta

“Nayidaṃ, bhikkhave, brahmacariyaṃ vussati janakuhanatthaṃ, na janalapanatthaṃ, na lābha­sakkā­rasi­lokā­nisaṃsatthaṃ, na itivā­dappa­mokkhā­nisaṃsatthaṃ, na ‘iti maṃ jano jānātū’ti. Atha kho idaṃ, bhikkhave, brahmacariyaṃ vussati saṃvaratthaṃ pahānatthaṃ virāgatthaṃ nirodhatthanti.

Saṃvaratthaṃ pahānatthaṃ,
brahmacariyaṃ anītihaṃ;
Adesayi so bhagavā,
nibbānogadhagāminaṃ;
Esa maggo mahantehi,
anuyāto mahesibhi.

Ye ca taṃ paṭipajjanti,
yathā buddhena desitaṃ;
Dukkhassantaṃ karissanti,
satthusāsanakārino”ti.

Pañcamaṃ.
