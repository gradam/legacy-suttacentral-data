Saṃyutta Nikāya 56

9. Tatiya­āma­kadhañña­peyyā­lavagga

84. Āmakadhaññasutta

…  “Evameva kho, bhikkhave, appakā te sattā ye āmakadhaññapaṭiggahaṇā paṭiviratā; atha kho eteva bahutarā sattā ye āmakadhaññapaṭiggahaṇā appaṭiviratā … pe ….

Catutthaṃ.
