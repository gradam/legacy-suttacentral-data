Saṃyutta Nikaya 45

Connected Discourses on the Path

14. Arising (1)

At Savatthı̄. “Bhikkhus, these eight things, developed and cultivated, if unarisen do not arise apart from the appearance of a Tathagata, an Arahant, a Perfectly Enlightened One. What eight? Right view ...right concentration. These eight things….”
