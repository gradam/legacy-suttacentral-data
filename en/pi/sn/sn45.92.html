Saṃyutta Nikaya 45

Connected Discourses on the Path

92–96. Slanting to the East (2–6)

“Bhikkhus, just as the river Yamuna ... … the river Aciravati … the river Sarabhū … the river Mahı̄ … whatever great rivers there are—that is, the Ganges, the Yamuna, the Aciravati, the Sarabhū, the Mahı̄—all slant, slope, and incline towards the east, so too a bhikkhu who develops and cultivates the Noble Eightfold Path slants, slopes, and inclines towards Nibbana.”

Complete as in §91.
