Itivuttaka 36

Dutiyajananakuhanasutta

Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ: 

“Nayidaṃ, bhikkhave, brahmacariyaṃ vussati janakuhanatthaṃ, na janalapanatthaṃ, na lābha­sakkā­rasi­lokā­nisaṃsatthaṃ, na ‘iti maṃ jano jānātū’ti. Atha kho idaṃ, bhikkhave, brahmacariyaṃ vussati abhiññatthañceva pariññatthañcā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati: 

“Abhiññatthaṃ pariññatthaṃ,
brahmacariyaṃ anītihaṃ;
Adesayi so bhagavā,
nibbānogadhagāminaṃ.

Esa maggo mahattehi,
anuyāto mahesibhi;
Ye ye taṃ paṭipajjanti,
yathā buddhena desitaṃ;
Dukkhassantaṃ karissanti,
satthusāsanakārino”ti.

Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

Navamaṃ.
