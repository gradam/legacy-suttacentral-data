Udāna 7.7

Papañcakhayasutta

Evaṃ me sutaṃ—​   ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena bhagavā attano papañca­saññā­saṅkhā­pahānaṃ paccavekkhamāno nisinno hoti.

Atha kho bhagavā attano papañca­saññā­saṅkhā­pahānaṃ viditvā tāyaṃ velāyaṃ imaṃ udānaṃ udānesi: 

“Yassa papañcā ṭhiti ca natthi,
Sandānaṃ palighañca vītivatto;
Taṃ nittaṇhaṃ muniṃ carantaṃ,
Nāvajānāti sadevakopi loko”ti.

Sattamaṃ.
