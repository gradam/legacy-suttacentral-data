Aṅguttara Nikāya 3

12. Āpāyikavagga

114. Dullabhasutta

“Tiṇṇaṃ, bhikkhave, pātubhāvo dullabho lokasmiṃ. Katamesaṃ tiṇṇaṃ? Tathāgatassa, bhikkhave, arahato sammā­sambud­dhassa pātubhāvo dullabho lokasmiṃ, tathā­gatap­pa­vedi­tassa dhamma­vinayassa desetā puggalo dullabho lokasmiṃ, kataññū katavedī puggalo dullabho lokasmiṃ. Imesaṃ kho, bhikkhave, tiṇṇaṃ pātubhāvo dullabho lokasmin”ti.

Dutiyaṃ.
