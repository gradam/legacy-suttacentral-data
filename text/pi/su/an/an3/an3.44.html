Aṅguttara Nikāya 3

5. Cūḷavagga

44. Kathā­pa­vat­ti­sutta

“Tīhi, bhikkhave, ṭhānehi kathā pavattinī hoti. Katamehi tīhi? Yo dhammaṃ deseti so atthap­paṭi­saṃ­vedī ca hoti dhammap­paṭi­saṃ­vedī ca. Yo dhammaṃ suṇāti so atthap­paṭi­saṃ­vedī ca hoti dhammap­paṭi­saṃ­vedī ca. Yo ceva dhammaṃ deseti yo ca dhammaṃ suṇāti ubho atthap­paṭi­saṃ­ve­dino ca honti dhammap­paṭi­saṃ­ve­dino ca. Imehi kho, bhikkhave, tīhi ṭhānehi kathā pavattinī hotī”ti.

Catutthaṃ.
