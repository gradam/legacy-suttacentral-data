Aṅguttara Nikāya 10

14. Sādhuvagga

144. Duk­kha­vipāka­sutta

“Duk­kha­vipākañca vo, bhikkhave, dhammaṃ desessāmi sukhavipākañca. Taṃ suṇātha … pe … katamo ca, bhikkhave, dukkhavipāko dhammo? Micchādiṭṭhi … pe … micchāvimutti—ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, dukkhavipāko dhammo. Katamo ca, bhikkhave, sukhavipāko dhammo? Sammādiṭṭhi … pe … sammāvimutti—ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, sukhavipāko dhammo”ti.

Ekādasamaṃ.

Sādhuvaggo catuttho.
