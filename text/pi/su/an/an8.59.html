Aṅguttara Nikāya 8

6. Gotamīvagga

59. Paṭha­ma­pugga­la­sutta

“Aṭṭhime, bhikkhave, puggalā āhuneyyā pāhuneyyā dakkhiṇeyyā añjalikaraṇīyā anuttaraṃ puññakkhettaṃ lokassa? Katame aṭṭha? Sotāpanno, sotā­patti­phala­sacchi­kiriyāya paṭipanno, sakadāgāmī, sakadā­gāmi­phala­sacchi­kiriyāya paṭipanno, anāgāmī, anāgā­mi­phala­sacchi­kiriyāya paṭipanno, arahā, arahattāya paṭipanno. Ime kho, bhikkhave, aṭṭha puggalā āhuneyyā … pe … anuttaraṃ puññakkhettaṃ lokassāti.

Cattāro ca paṭipannā,
cattāro ca phale ṭhitā;
Esa saṅgho ujubhūto,
paññā­sī­lasamā­hito.

Yajamānānaṃ manussānaṃ,
Puññapekkhāna pāṇinaṃ;
Karotaṃ opadhikaṃ puññaṃ,
Saṅghe dinnaṃ mahapphalan”ti.

Navamaṃ.
