Aṅguttara Nikāya 8

6. Gotamīvagga

59. Paṭhamapuggalasutta

“Aṭṭhime, bhikkhave, puggalā āhuneyyā pāhuneyyā dakkhiṇeyyā añjalikaraṇīyā anuttaraṃ puññakkhettaṃ lokassa? Katame aṭṭha? Sotāpanno, sotāpatti­phala­sacchi­kiri­yāya paṭipanno, sakadāgāmī, sakadā­gāmi­phala­sacchi­kiri­yāya paṭipanno, anāgāmī, anāgā­mipha­lasacchi­kiri­yāya paṭipanno, arahā, arahattāya paṭipanno. Ime kho, bhikkhave, aṭṭha puggalā āhuneyyā … pe … anuttaraṃ puññakkhettaṃ lokassāti.

Cattāro ca paṭipannā,
cattāro ca phale ṭhitā;
Esa saṅgho ujubhūto,
paññāsīlasamāhito.

Yajamānānaṃ manussānaṃ,
Puññapekkhāna pāṇinaṃ;
Karotaṃ opadhikaṃ puññaṃ,
Saṅghe dinnaṃ mahapphalan”ti.

Navamaṃ.
