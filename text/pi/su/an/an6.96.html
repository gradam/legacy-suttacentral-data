Aṅguttara Nikāya 6

10. Ānisaṃsavagga

96. Pātubhāvasutta

“Channaṃ, bhikkhave, pātubhāvo dullabho lokasmiṃ. Katamesaṃ channaṃ? Tathāgatassa arahato sammā­sambud­dhassa pātubhāvo dullabho lokasmiṃ, tathā­gatap­pa­vedi­tassa dhamma­vinayassa desetā puggalo dullabho lokasmiṃ, ariyāyatane paccājāti dullabhā lokasmiṃ, indriyānaṃ avekallatā dullabhā lokasmiṃ, ajaḷatā aneḷamūgatā dullabhā lokasmiṃ, kusale dhamme chando dullabho lokasmiṃ. Imesaṃ kho, bhikkhave, channaṃ pātubhāvo dullabho lokasmin”ti.

Paṭhamaṃ.
