Aṅguttara Nikāya 4

2. Caravagga

17. Paṭha­ma­agati­sutta

“Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, agatigamanāni. Katamāni cattāri? Chandāgatiṃ gacchati, dosāgatiṃ gacchati, mohāgatiṃ gacchati, bhayāgatiṃ gacchati—imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri agati­gamanā­nīti.

* Chandā dosā bhayā mohā,
yo dhammaṃ ativattati;
Nihīyati tassa yaso,
kāḷapakkheva candimā”ti.

Sattamaṃ.
