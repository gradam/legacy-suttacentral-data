Aṅguttara Nikāya 4

16. Indriyavagga

155. Paṭi­saṅ­khā­na­bala­sutta

“Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, balāni. Katamāni cattāri? Paṭi­saṅ­khā­na­balaṃ, bhāvanābalaṃ, anavajjabalaṃ, saṅgahabalaṃ—imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri balānī”ti.

Pañcamaṃ.
