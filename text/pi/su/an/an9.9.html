Aṅguttara Nikāya 9

1. Sambodhivagga

9. Puggalasutta

“Navayime, bhikkhave, puggalā santo saṃvijjamānā lokasmiṃ. Katame nava? Arahā, arahattāya paṭipanno, anāgāmī, anāgā­mipha­lasacchi­kiri­yāya paṭipanno, sakadāgāmī, sakadā­gāmi­phala­sacchi­kiri­yāya paṭipanno, sotāpanno, sotāpatti­phala­sacchi­kiri­yāya paṭipanno, puthujjano–  ime kho, bhikkhave, nava puggalā santo saṃvijjamānā lokasmin”ti.

Navamaṃ.
