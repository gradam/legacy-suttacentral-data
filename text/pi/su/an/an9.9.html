Aṅguttara Nikāya 9

1. Sambodhivagga

9. Puggalasutta

“Navayime, bhikkhave, puggalā santo saṃvijjamānā lokasmiṃ. Katame nava? Arahā, arahattāya paṭipanno, anāgāmī, anāgā­mi­phala­sacchi­kiriyāya paṭipanno, sakadāgāmī, sakadā­gāmi­phala­sacchi­kiriyāya paṭipanno, sotāpanno, sotā­patti­phala­sacchi­kiriyāya paṭipanno, puthujjano–  ime kho, bhikkhave, nava puggalā santo saṃvijjamānā lokasmin”ti.

Navamaṃ.
