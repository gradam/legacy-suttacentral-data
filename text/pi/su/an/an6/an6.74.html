Aṅguttara Nikāya 6

7. Devatāvagga

74. Dutiya­taj­jhāna­sutta

“Cha, bhikkhave, dhamme appahāya abhabbo paṭhamaṃ jhānaṃ upasampajja viharituṃ. Katame cha? Kāmavitakkaṃ, ­byāpā­da­vitak­kaṃ, vihiṃ­sā­vitak­kaṃ, kāmasaññaṃ, byāpādasaññaṃ, vihiṃsāsaññaṃ—ime kho, bhikkhave, cha dhamme appahāya abhabbo paṭhamaṃ jhānaṃ upasampajja viharituṃ.

Cha, bhikkhave, dhamme pahāya bhabbo paṭhamaṃ jhānaṃ upasampajja viharituṃ. Katame cha? Kāmavitakkaṃ, ­byāpā­da­vitak­kaṃ, vihiṃ­sā­vitak­kaṃ, kāmasaññaṃ, byāpādasaññaṃ, vihiṃsāsaññaṃ—ime kho, bhikkhave, cha dhamme pahāya bhabbo paṭhamaṃ jhānaṃ upasampajja viharitun”ti.

Dasamaṃ.

Devatāvaggo sattamo.

Anāgāmi arahaṃ mittā,
saṅga­ṇikā­rāma­devatā;
Samā­dhi­sak­khi­bhabbaṃ balaṃ,
tajjhānā apare duveti.
