Aṅguttara Nikāya 10

13. Parisuddhavagga

124. Dutiyasutta

“Dasayime, bhikkhave, dhammā anuppannā uppajjanti, nāññatra sugatavinayā. Katame dasa? Sammādiṭṭhi … pe … sammāvimutti–  ime kho, bhikkhave, dasa dhammā anuppannā uppajjanti, nāññatra sugatavinayā”ti.

Dutiyaṃ.
