Aṅguttara Nikāya 5

23. Dīgha­cārika­vagga

223. Atinivāsasutta

“Pañcime, bhikkhave, ādīnavā atinivāse. Katame pañca? Bahubhaṇḍo hoti bahu­bhaṇḍa­sanni­cayo, bahubhesajjo hoti bahu­bhesaj­ja­sanni­cayo, bahukicco hoti bahukaraṇīyo byatto kiṅkaraṇīyesu, saṃsaṭṭho viharati gahaṭ­ṭha­pabba­ji­tehi ananulomikena gihisaṃsaggena, tamhā ca āvāsā pakkamanto sāpekkho pakkamati. Ime kho, bhikkhave, pañca ādīnavā atinivāse.

Pañcime, bhikkhave, ānisaṃsā samavatthavāse. Katame pañca? Na bahubhaṇḍo hoti na bahu­bhaṇḍa­sanni­cayo, na bahubhesajjo hoti na bahu­bhesaj­ja­sanni­cayo, na bahukicco hoti na bahukaraṇīyo na byatto kiṅkaraṇīyesu, asaṃsaṭṭho viharati gahaṭ­ṭha­pabba­ji­tehi ananulomikena gihisaṃsaggena, tamhā ca āvāsā pakkamanto anapekkho pakkamati. Ime kho, bhikkhave, pañca ānisaṃsā samavatthavāse”ti.

Tatiyaṃ.
