Aṅguttara Nikāya 6

10. Ānisaṃsavagga

97. Ānisaṃsasutta

“Chayime, bhikkhave, ānisaṃsā sotāpatti­phala­sacchi­kiri­yāya. Katame cha? Saddhammaniyato hoti, aparihānadhammo hoti, pariyantakatassa dukkhaṃ hoti, asādhāraṇena ñāṇena samannāgato hoti, hetu cassa sudiṭṭho, hetusamuppannā ca dhammā. Ime kho, bhikkhave, cha ānisaṃsā sotāpatti­phala­sacchi­kiri­yāyā”ti.

Dutiyaṃ.
