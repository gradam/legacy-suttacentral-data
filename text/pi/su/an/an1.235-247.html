Aṅguttara Nikāya 1

18. Pañcamavagga

235–​247 

“Etadaggaṃ, bhikkhave, mama sāvikānaṃ bhikkhunīnaṃ rattaññūnaṃ yadidaṃ mahā­pajā­pati­gotamī. (1)

… Mahāpaññānaṃ yadidaṃ khemā. (2)

… Iddhimantīnaṃ yadidaṃ uppalavaṇṇā. (3)

… Vinayadharānaṃ yadidaṃ paṭācārā. (4)

… Dhamma­ka­thi­kā­naṃ yadidaṃ dhammadinnā. (5)

… Jhāyīnaṃ yadidaṃ nandā. (6)

… Ārad­dha­vīriyā­naṃ yadidaṃ soṇā. (7)

… Dibba­cak­khu­kā­naṃ yadidaṃ bakulā. (8)

… Khippā­bhiññā­naṃ yadidaṃ bhaddā kuṇḍalakesā. (9)

… Pubbenivāsaṃ anussarantīnaṃ yadidaṃ bhaddā kāpilānī. (10)

… Mahā­bhiñ­ñā­pattā­naṃ yadidaṃ bhaddakaccānā. (11)

… Lūkha­cīvara­dharā­naṃ yadidaṃ kisāgotamī. (12)

… Sad­dhādhi­muttā­naṃ yadidaṃ siṅgālakamātā”ti. (13)

Vaggo pañcamo.
