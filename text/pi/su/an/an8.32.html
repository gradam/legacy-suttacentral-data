Aṅguttara Nikāya 8

4. Dānavagga

32. Dutiyadānasutta

“Saddhā hiriyaṃ kusalañca dānaṃ,
Dhammā ete sappurisānuyātā;
Etañhi maggaṃ diviyaṃ vadanti,
Etena hi gacchati devalokan”ti.

Dutiyaṃ.
