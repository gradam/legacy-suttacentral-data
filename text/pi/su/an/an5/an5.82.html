Aṅguttara Nikāya 5

9. Theravagga

82. Vītarāgasutta

“Pañcahi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato thero bhikkhu sab­rahma­cārī­naṃ appiyo ca hoti amanāpo ca agaru ca abhāvanīyo ca. Katamehi pañcahi? Avītarāgo hoti, avītadoso hoti, avītamoho hoti, makkhī ca, paḷāsī ca—imehi kho, bhikkhave, pañcahi dhammehi samannāgato thero bhikkhu sab­rahma­cārī­naṃ appiyo ca hoti amanāpo ca agaru ca abhāvanīyo ca.

Pañcahi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato thero bhikkhu sab­rahma­cārī­naṃ piyo ca hoti manāpo ca garu ca bhāvanīyo ca. Katamehi pañcahi? Vītarāgo hoti, vītadoso hoti, vītamoho hoti, amakkhī ca, apaḷāsī ca—imehi kho, bhikkhave, pañcahi dhammehi samannāgato thero bhikkhu sab­rahma­cārī­naṃ piyo ca hoti manāpo ca garu ca bhāvanīyo cā”ti.

Dutiyaṃ.
