Aṅguttara Nikāya 9

9. Iddhipādavagga

83. Sikkhasutta

“Pañcimāni, bhikkhave, sikkhā­dubbal­yāni. Katamāni pañca? Pāṇātipāto … pe … surā­meraya­majja­pa­mā­daṭṭhā­naṃ—imāni kho, bhikkhave, pañca sikkhā­dubbal­yāni.

Imesaṃ kho, bhikkhave, pañcannaṃ sikkhā­dubbal­yā­naṃ pahānāya cattāro iddhipādā bhāvetabbā. Katame cattāro? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu chanda­samā­dhi­pa­dhā­na­saṅ­khā­ra­saman­nā­gataṃ iddhipādaṃ bhāveti, vīriyasamādhi … cittasamādhi … vīmaṃ­sā­samā­dhi­pa­dhā­na­saṅ­khā­ra­saman­nā­gataṃ iddhipādaṃ bhāveti. Imesaṃ kho, bhikkhave, pañcannaṃ sikkhā­dubbal­yā­naṃ pahānāya ime cattāro iddhipādā bhāvetabbā”ti.

Paṭhamaṃ.
