Aṅguttara Nikāya 10

15. Ariyavagga

153. Anus­sari­tab­basutta

“Anus­sari­tab­bañca vo, bhikkhave, dhammaṃ desessāmi na anus­sari­tab­bañca. Taṃ suṇātha … pe … katamo ca, bhikkhave, na anussaritabbo dhammo? Micchādiṭṭhi … pe … micchāvimutti—ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, na anussaritabbo dhammo. Katamo ca, bhikkhave, anussaritabbo dhammo? Sammādiṭṭhi … pe … sammāvimutti—ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, anussaritabbo dhammo”ti.

Navamaṃ.
