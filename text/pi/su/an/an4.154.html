Aṅguttara Nikāya 4

16. Indriyavagga

154. Satibalasutta

“Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, balāni. Katamāni cattāri? Satibalaṃ, samādhibalaṃ, anavajjabalaṃ, saṅgahabalaṃ—imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri balānī”ti.

Catutthaṃ.
