Aṅguttara Nikāya 5

16. Saddhammavagga

160. ­Duppaṭi­vinoda­yasutta

“Pañcime, bhikkhave, uppannā ­duppaṭi­vinodayā. Katame pañca? Uppanno rāgo ­duppaṭi­vinodayo, uppanno doso ­duppaṭi­vinodayo, uppanno moho ­duppaṭi­vinodayo, uppannaṃ paṭibhānaṃ ­duppaṭi­vinoda­yaṃ, uppannaṃ gamikacittaṃ ­duppaṭi­vinoda­yaṃ. Ime kho, bhikkhave, pañca uppannā ­duppaṭi­vinodayā”ti.

Dasamaṃ.

Saddhammavaggo paṭhamo.

Tayo sammattaniyāmā,
tayo saddham­ma­sam­mosā;
Dukkathā ceva sārajjaṃ,
udāyi­dub­bino­dayāti.
