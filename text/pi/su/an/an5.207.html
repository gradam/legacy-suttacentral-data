Aṅguttara Nikāya 5

21. Kimilavagga

207. Yāgusutta

^ “Pañcime, bhikkhave, ānisaṃsā yāguyā. Katame pañca? Khuddaṃ paṭihanati, pipāsaṃ paṭivineti, vātaṃ anulometi, vatthiṃ sodheti, āmāvasesaṃ pāceti. Ime kho, bhikkhave, pañca ānisaṃsā yāguyā”ti.

Sattamaṃ.
