Aṅguttara Nikāya 10

13. Parisuddhavagga

125. Tatiyasutta

“Dasayime, bhikkhave, dhammā mahapphalā mahānisaṃsā, nāññatra sugatavinayā. Katame dasa? Sammādiṭṭhi … pe … sammāvimutti–  ime kho, bhikkhave, dasa dhammā mahapphalā mahānisaṃsā, nāññatra sugatavinayā”ti.

Tatiyaṃ.
