Aṅguttara Nikāya 9

7. Sati­paṭṭhā­na­vagga

66. Upādā­nak­khan­dha­sutta

“Pañcime, bhikkhave, upādā­nak­khan­dhā. Katame pañca? Rūpupā­dā­nak­khan­dho, vedanupā­dā­nak­khan­dho, saññu­pādā­nak­khan­dho, saṅ­khā­ru­pādā­nak­khan­dho, viñ­ñāṇupā­dā­nak­khan­dho–  ime kho, bhikkhave, pañcu­pādā­nak­khan­dhā.

Imesaṃ kho, bhikkhave, pañcannaṃ upādā­nak­khan­dhā­naṃ pahānāya … pe … ime cattāro satipaṭṭhānā bhāvetabbā”ti.

Catutthaṃ.
