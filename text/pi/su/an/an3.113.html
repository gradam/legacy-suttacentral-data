Aṅguttara Nikāya 3

12. Āpāyikavagga

113. Āpāyikasutta

“Tayome, bhikkhave, āpāyikā nerayikā idamappahāya. Katame tayo? Yo ca abrahmacārī brahma­cāri­paṭiñño, yo ca suddhaṃ brahmacariyaṃ carantaṃ amūlakena abrahma­cari­yena anuddhaṃseti, yo cāyaṃ evaṃvādī evaṃdiṭṭhi: ‘natthi kāmesu doso’ti, so tāya kāmesu pātabyataṃ āpajjati. Ime kho, bhikkhave, tayo āpāyikā nerayikā idamappahāyā”ti.

Paṭhamaṃ.
