Aṅguttara Nikāya 6

8. Arahattavagga

80. Mahan­tat­tasutta

“Chahi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato bhikkhu nacirasseva mahantattaṃ vepullattaṃ pāpuṇāti dhammesu. Katamehi chahi? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu ālokabahulo ca hoti yogabahulo ca vedabahulo ca asan­tuṭ­ṭhi­bahulo ca anikkhit­ta­dhuro ca kusalesu dhammesu uttari ca patāreti. Imehi kho, bhikkhave, chahi dhammehi samannāgato bhikkhu nacirasseva mahantattaṃ vepullattaṃ pāpuṇāti dhammesū”ti.

Chaṭṭhaṃ.
