Aṅguttara Nikāya 9

7. Sati­paṭṭhā­na­vagga

69. Maccha­ri­ya­sutta

“Pañcimāni, bhikkhave, macchariyāni. Katamāni pañca? Āvāsa­maccha­ri­yaṃ, kula­maccha­ri­yaṃ, lābha­maccha­ri­yaṃ, vaṇṇa­maccha­ri­yaṃ, dhamma­maccha­ri­yaṃ–  imāni kho, bhikkhave, pañca macchariyāni.

Imesaṃ kho, bhikkhave, pañcannaṃ macchariyānaṃ pahānāya … pe … ime cattāro satipaṭṭhānā bhāvetabbā”ti.

Sattamaṃ.
