Aṅguttara Nikāya 3

11. Sambodhavagga

107. Ruṇṇasutta

“Ruṇṇamidaṃ, bhikkhave, ariyassa vinaye yadidaṃ gītaṃ. Ummattakamidaṃ, bhikkhave, ariyassa vinaye yadidaṃ naccaṃ. Komārakamidaṃ, bhikkhave, ariyassa vinaye yadidaṃ ativelaṃ danta­vi­daṃsa­ka­hasitaṃ. Tasmātiha, bhikkhave, setughāto gīte, setughāto nacce, alaṃ vo dhammap­pamodi­tā­naṃ sataṃ sitaṃ sitamattāyā”ti.

Pañcamaṃ.
