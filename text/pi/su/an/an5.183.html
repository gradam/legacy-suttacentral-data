Aṅguttara Nikāya 5

19. Araññavagga

183. Rukkhamūlikasutta

“Pañcime, bhikkhave, rukkhamūlikā. Katame pañca? Mandattā momūhattā rukkhamūliko hoti … pe … idamatthitaṃyeva nissāya rukkhamūliko hoti. Ime kho, bhikkhave, pañca rukkhamūlikā”ti.

Tatiyaṃ.
