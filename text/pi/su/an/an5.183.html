Aṅguttara Nikāya 5

19. Araññavagga

183. Rukkha­mūlika­sutta

“Pañcime, bhikkhave, rukkhamūlikā. Katame pañca? Mandattā momūhattā rukkhamūliko hoti … pe … ida­matthi­taṃ­yeva nissāya rukkhamūliko hoti. Ime kho, bhikkhave, pañca rukkhamūlikā”ti.

Tatiyaṃ.
