Aṅguttara Nikāya 4

14. Puggalavagga

132. Paṭibhānasutta

* “Cattārome, bhikkhave, puggalā santo saṃvijjamānā lokasmiṃ. Katame cattāro?* Yuttap­pa­ṭibhāno, no muttap­pa­ṭibhāno; muttap­pa­ṭibhāno, no yuttap­pa­ṭibhāno; yuttap­pa­ṭibhāno ca muttap­pa­ṭibhāno ca; neva yuttap­pa­ṭibhāno na muttap­pa­ṭibhāno—ime kho, bhikkhave, cattāro puggalā santo saṃvijjamānā lokasmin”ti.

Dutiyaṃ.
