Aṅguttara Nikāya 4

5. Rohitassavagga

47. Suvidūrasutta

* “Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, suvidū­ra­vidū­rāni. Katamāni cattāri? Nabhañca, bhikkhave, pathavī ca; idaṃ paṭhamaṃ suvidūravidūre. Orimañca, bhikkhave, tīraṃ samuddassa pārimañca; idaṃ dutiyaṃ suvidūravidūre. Yato ca, bhikkhave, verocano abbhudeti yattha ca atthameti; idaṃ tatiyaṃ suvidūravidūre. Satañca, bhikkhave, dhammo asatañca dhammo; idaṃ catutthaṃ suvidūravidūre. Imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri suvidū­ra­vidū­rā­nīti.

* Nabhañca dūre pathavī ca dūre,
Pāraṃ samuddassa tadāhu dūre;
Yato ca verocano abbhudeti,
Pabhaṅkaro yattha ca atthameti;
Tato have dūrataraṃ vadanti,
Satañca dhammaṃ asatañca dhammaṃ.

Abyāyiko hoti sataṃ samāgamo,
Yāvāpi tiṭṭheyya tatheva hoti;
Khippañhi veti asataṃ samāgamo,
Tasmā sataṃ dhammo asabbhi ārakā”ti.

Sattamaṃ.
