Milindapañha

Opamma­kathā­pañha

Makkaṭakavagga

2. Thanas­sita­dārakaṅ­gapañha

“Bhante nāgasena, ‘thanas­sita­dārakassa ekaṃ aṅgaṃ gahetabban’ti yaṃ vadesi, katamaṃ taṃ ekaṃ aṅgaṃ gahetabban”ti? “Yathā, mahārāja, thanas­si­tadārako sadatthe laggati, khīratthiko rodati; evameva kho, mahārāja, yoginā yogāvacarena sadatthe laggitabbaṃ, sabbattha dhammañāṇena bhavitabbaṃ, uddese paripucchāya sammappayoge paviveke garusaṃvāse kal­yāṇa­mitta­sevane. Idaṃ, mahārāja, thanas­sita­dārakassa ekaṃ aṅgaṃ gahetabbaṃ. Bhāsitampetaṃ, mahārāja, bhagavatā devātidevena dīghanikā­ya­vare pari­nib­bā­na­suttante—

* ‘Iṅgha tumhe, ānanda, sāratthe ghaṭatha, sāratthe anuyuñjatha, sāratthe appamattā ātāpino pahitattā viharathā’”ti.

Thanas­sita­dārakaṅ­gapañho dutiyo.
