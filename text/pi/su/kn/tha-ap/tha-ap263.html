Therāpadāna

­Padu­muk­khipa­vagga

1 Ākā­suk­khi­piyat­thera­apadāna

“Suvaṇṇavaṇṇaṃ siddhatthaṃ,
Gacchantaṃ antarāpaṇe;
Jalajagge duve gayha,
Upāgacchiṃ narāsabhaṃ.

Ekañca pupphaṃ pādesu,
buddha­seṭṭhassa nikkhipiṃ;
Ekañca pupphaṃ paggayha,
ākāse ukkhipiṃ ahaṃ.

Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ puppha­ma­bhiro­payiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
puppha­dā­nassi­daṃ phalaṃ.

Ito chat­tiṃsa­kappamhi,
eko āsiṃ mahīpati;
Antalikkhakaro nāma,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ākāsukkhipiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Ākā­suk­khi­piyat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ paṭhamaṃ.
