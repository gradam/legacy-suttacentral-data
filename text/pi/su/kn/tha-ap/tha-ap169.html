Therāpadāna

Supāri­cari­ya­vagga

7 Yūthi­ka­pupphi­yat­thera­apadāna

“Canda­bhāgā­nadī­tīre,
anusotaṃ vajāmahaṃ;
Sayambhuṃ addasaṃ tattha,
sālarājaṃva phullitaṃ.

Pupphaṃ yūthikamādāya,
upagacchiṃ mahāmuniṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
buddhassa abhiropayiṃ.

Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ puppha­ma­bhiro­payiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddha­pū­jāyi­daṃ phalaṃ.

Satta­saṭṭhim­hito kappe,
eko sāmuddharo ahuṃ;
Satta­ratana­sam­panno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā yūthi­ka­pupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Yūthi­ka­pupphi­yat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ sattamaṃ.
