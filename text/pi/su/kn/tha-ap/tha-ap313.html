Therāpadāna

Ārak­kha­dāyaka­vagga

1. Ārak­kha­dāyakat­thera­apadāna

“Dhammadassissa munino,
vati kārāpitā mayā;
Ārakkho ca mayā dinno,
dvipadindassa tādino.

Aṭṭhārase kappasate,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Tena kammavisesena,
patto me āsavakkhayo.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;
Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ārakkhadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Ārak­kha­dāyakat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ paṭhamaṃ.
