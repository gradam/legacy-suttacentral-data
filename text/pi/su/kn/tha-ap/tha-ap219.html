Therāpadāna

Hatthivagga

7 Tāla­vaṇṭa­dāyakat­thera­apadāna

“Tālavaṇṭaṃ mayā dinnaṃ,
­tissas­sādicca­bandhuno;
Gimha­nib­bāpa­nat­thāya,
pari­ḷāhopa­santiyā.

Sannibbāpemi rāgaggiṃ,
dosaggiñca taduttariṃ;
Nibbāpemi ca mohaggiṃ,
tāla­vaṇṭas­sidaṃ phalaṃ.

Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
bhavā sabbe samūhatā;
Dhāremi antimaṃ dehaṃ,
sammā­sambud­dha­sāsane.

Dvenavute ito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
tāla­vaṇṭas­sidaṃ phalaṃ.

Tesaṭṭhimhi ito kappe,
mahā­nā­masa­nā­mako;
Satta­ratana­sam­panno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā tāla­vaṇṭa­dāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Tāla­vaṇṭa­dāyakat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ sattamaṃ.
