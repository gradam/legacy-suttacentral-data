Therāpadāna

Kuṭaja­pupphi­yavagga

6 Bodhi­u­paṭṭhā­kat­thera­apadāna

“Nagare rammavatiyā,
āsiṃ murajavādako;
Nic­cu­paṭṭhā­na­yuttomhi,
gatohaṃ bodhimuttamaṃ.

Sāyaṃ pātaṃ upaṭṭhitvā,
sukkamūlena codito;
Aṭṭhā­ra­sa­kappa­sate,
duggatiṃ nupapajjahaṃ.

Pannarase kappasate,
ito āsiṃ janādhipo;
Murajo nāma nāmena,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā bodhiupaṭṭhāko thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Bodhi­u­paṭṭhā­kat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
