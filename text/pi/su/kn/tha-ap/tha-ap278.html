Therāpadāna

Suvaṇ­ṇa­bibboha­navagga

6 ­Pottha­ka­dāyakat­thera­apadāna

“Satthāraṃ dhammamārabbha,
saṃghañcāpi mahesinaṃ;
Potthadānaṃ mayā dinnaṃ,
dakkhiṇeyye anuttare.

Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
­pottha­dā­nassi­daṃ phalaṃ.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā potthakadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

­Pottha­ka­dāyakat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
