Therāpadāna

Ālamba­ṇa­dāyaka­vagga

6 Aṅkola­pupphi­yat­thera­apadāna

“Nārado iti me nāmaṃ,
kassapo iti maṃ vidū;
Addasaṃ samaṇānaggaṃ,
vipassiṃ devasakkataṃ.

Anub­yañ­jana­dharaṃ buddhaṃ,
āhutīnaṃ paṭiggahaṃ;
Aṅkolapupphaṃ paggayha,
buddhassa abhiropayiṃ.

Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ puppha­ma­bhipū­jayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddha­pū­jāyi­daṃ phalaṃ.

Catusattatito kappe,
romaso nāma khattiyo;
Āmukka­mālābha­raṇo,
­sayogga­ba­lavāhano.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā aṅkolapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Aṅkola­pupphi­yat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
