Therāpadāna

Paduma­kesara­vagga

8 Anu­lepa­dāyakat­thera­apadāna

“Atthadassissa munino,
addasaṃ sāvakaṃ ahaṃ;
Navakammaṃ karontassa,
sīmāya upagacchahaṃ.

Niṭṭhite navakamme ca,
anule­pamadā­sahaṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
puññakkhette anuttare.

Aṭṭhārase kappasate,
yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
anulepassidaṃ phalaṃ.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā anulepadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Anu­lepa­dāyakat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
