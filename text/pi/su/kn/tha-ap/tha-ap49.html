Therāpadāna

Upālivagga

7 Sayanadā­ya­kat­the­ra­apadāna

“Siddhatthassa bhagavato,
mettacittassa tādino;
Sayanaggaṃ mayā dinnaṃ,
dussabhaṇḍehi atthataṃ.

Paṭiggahesi bhagavā,
kappiyaṃ sayanāsanaṃ;
Uṭṭhāya sayanā tamhā,
vehāsaṃ uggamī jino.

Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ sayana­madāsa­haṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
sayanassa idaṃ phalaṃ.

Ekapaññāsito kappe,
varako devasavhayo;
Satta­ratana­sam­panno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sayanadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Sayanadā­ya­kat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ sattamaṃ.
