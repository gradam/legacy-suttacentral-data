Therāpadāna

Saka­cinta­ni­yavagga

2 Avo­pupphi­yat­thera­apadāna

“Vihārā abhinikkhamma,
abbhuṭṭhāsi ca caṅkame;
Catusaccaṃ pakāsanto,
desento amataṃ padaṃ.

Sikhissa giramaññāya,
buddha­seṭṭhassa tādino;
Nānāpupphaṃ gahetvāna,
ākāsamhi samokiriṃ.

Tena kammena dvipadinda,
lokajeṭṭha narāsabha;
Pattomhi acalaṃ ṭhānaṃ,
hitvā jayaparājayaṃ.

Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ puppha­ma­bhiro­payiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
puppha­pū­jāyi­daṃ phalaṃ.

Ito vīsatikappamhi,
sumedho nāma khattiyo;
Satta­ratana­sam­panno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā avopupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Avo­pupphi­yat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ dutiyaṃ.
