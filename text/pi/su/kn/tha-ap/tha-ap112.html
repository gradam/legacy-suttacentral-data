Therāpadāna

Bhik­kha­dāyi­vagga

10. Reṇu­pūja­kat­the­ra­apadāna

“Suvaṇṇavaṇṇaṃ sambuddhaṃ,
Sataraṃsiṃva bhāṇumaṃ;
Obhāsentaṃ disā sabbā,
Uḷurājaṃva pūritaṃ.

Purakkhataṃ sāvakehi,
sāgareheva medaniṃ;
Nāgaṃ paggayha reṇūhi,
vipas­sis­sā­bhi­ro­payiṃ.

Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ reṇu­mabhi­ropa­yiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddha­pū­jāyi­daṃ phalaṃ.

Paṇṇatālīsito kappe,
reṇu nāmāsi khattiyo;
Satta­ratana­sam­panno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”. (1668)

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā reṇupūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Reṇu­pūja­kat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ dasamaṃ.

Bhik­kha­dāyi­vaggo ekādasamo.

Tassuddānaṃ

Bhikkhadāyī ñāṇasaññī,
hatthiyo padapūjako;
Muṭṭhipupphī udakado,
naḷamāli upaṭṭhako;
Biḷālidāyī reṇu ca,
gāthāyo cha ca saṭṭhi ca.
