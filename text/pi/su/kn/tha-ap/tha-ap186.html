Therāpadāna

Kuṭaja­pupphi­yavagga

4 Pañca­hatthi­yat­thera­apadāna

“Tisso nāmāsi bhagavā,
lokajeṭṭho narāsabho;
Purakkhato sāvakehi,
rathiyaṃ paṭipajjatha.

Pañca uppalahatthā ca,
cāturā ṭhapitā mayā;
Āhutiṃ dātukāmohaṃ,
paggaṇhiṃ vatasiddhiyā.

Suvaṇṇavaṇṇaṃ sambuddhaṃ,
Gacchantaṃ antarāpaṇe;
Buddharaṃsīhi phuṭṭhosmi,
Pūjesiṃ dvipaduttamaṃ.

Dvenavute ito kappe,
yaṃ puppha­ma­bhipū­jayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddha­pū­jāyi­daṃ phalaṃ.

Ito terasakappamhi,
pañca susabhasammatā;
Satta­ratana­sam­pannā,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā pañcahatthiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Pañca­hatthi­yat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ catutthaṃ.
