Therāpadāna

Kaṇikāravagga

4 Avaṭa­phali­yat­thera­apadāna

“Sahassaraṃsī bhagavā,
sayambhū aparājito;
Vivekā vuṭṭhahitvāna,
gocarā­yābhi­nikkhami.

Phalahattho ahaṃ disvā,
upagacchiṃ narāsabhaṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
avaṭaṃ adadiṃ phalaṃ.

Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ phalaṃ adadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
phala­dā­nassi­daṃ phalaṃ.

Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā avaṭaphaliyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Avaṭa­phali­yat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ catutthaṃ.
