Therāpadāna

Ava­ṭaphala­vagga

3. Udum­bara­phala­dāyakat­thera­apadāna

“Vinatānadiyā tīre,
vihāsi purisuttamo;
Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,
ekaggaṃ susamāhitaṃ.

Tasmiṃ pasannamānaso,
kile­samala­dho­vane;
Udumbaraphalaṃ gayha,
buddha­seṭṭhas­sadāsa­haṃ.

Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ phalamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
phala­dā­nassi­daṃ phalaṃ.

Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā udum­bara­pha­ladāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Udum­bara­phala­dāyakat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ tatiyaṃ.
