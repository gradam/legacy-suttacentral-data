Therāpadāna

­Padu­muk­khipa­vagga

8 Saḷala­pupphi­yat­thera­apadāna

“Canda­bhāgā­nadī­tīre,
ahosiṃ kinnaro tadā;
Vipassiṃ addasaṃ buddhaṃ,
raṃsi­jāla­samā­kulaṃ.

Pasannacitto sumano,
paramāya ca pītiyā;
Paggayha saḷalaṃ pupphaṃ,
vipassiṃ okiriṃ ahaṃ.

Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ puppha­ma­bhipū­jayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddha­pū­jāyi­daṃ phalaṃ.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā saḷalapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Saḷala­pupphi­yat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
