Therāpadāna

Sāla­kusumi­yavagga

3 Citaka­nibbā­pakat­thera­apadāna

“Dayhamāne sarīramhi,
vessabhussa mahesino;
Gandhodakaṃ gahetvāna,
citaṃ nibbāpayiṃ ahaṃ.

Ekatiṃse ito kappe,
citaṃ nibbāpayiṃ ahaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
­gan­dho­da­kas­sidaṃ phalaṃ.

Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā citaka­nib­bāpako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Citaka­nibbā­pakat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ tatiyaṃ.
