Therāpadāna

Naḷamālivagga

6. Kusaṭṭha­ka­dāyakat­thera­apadāna

“Kassapassa bhagavato,
brāhmaṇassa vusīmato;
Pasannacitto sumano,
kusaṭṭha­ka­ma­dā­sahaṃ.

Imasmiṃyeva kappasmiṃ,
kusaṭṭha­ka­ma­dā­sahaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
kusaṭṭha­kas­sidaṃ phalaṃ.

Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kusaṭṭha­ka­dāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Kusaṭṭha­ka­dāyakat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
