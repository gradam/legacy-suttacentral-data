Therāpadāna

­Paṇṇadā­yaka­vagga

2 Phala­dāyakat­thera­apadāna

“Sineru­samasan­toso,
dhara­ṇī­samasā­diso;
Vuṭṭhahitvā samādhimhā,
bhikkhāya mamupaṭṭhito.

Harītakaṃ āmalakaṃ,
amba­jambu­vi­bhīta­kaṃ;
Kolaṃ bhallātakaṃ billaṃ,
­phāru­sa­ka­phalāni ca.

Siddhatthassa mahesissa,
sabba­lo­kā­nukam­pino;
Tañca sabbaṃ mayā dinnaṃ,
vippasannena cetasā.

Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ phalaṃ adadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
phala­dā­nassi­daṃ phalaṃ.

Sattapaññāsito kappe,
ekajjho nāma khattiyo;
Satta­ratana­sam­panno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā phaladāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Phala­dāyakat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ dutiyaṃ.
