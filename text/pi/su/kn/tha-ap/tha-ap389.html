Therāpadāna

Ava­ṭaphala­vagga

7. Kadali­phala­dāyakat­thera­apadāna

“Kaṇikāraṃva jalitaṃ,
puṇṇamāyeva candimaṃ;
Jalantaṃ dīparukkhaṃva,
addasaṃ lokanāyakaṃ.

Kadaliphalaṃ paggayha,
adāsiṃ satthuno ahaṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
vanditvāna apakkamiṃ.

Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ phalamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
phala­dā­nassi­daṃ phalaṃ.

Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kadali­phala­dāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Kadali­phala­dāyakat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ sattamaṃ.
