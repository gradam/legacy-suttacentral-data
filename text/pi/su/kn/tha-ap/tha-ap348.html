Therāpadāna

Eka­padumi­yavagga

6 Campa­ka­pupphi­yat­thera­apadāna

“Hima­van­tassā­vidūre,
jāpalo nāma pabbato;
Buddho sudassano nāma,
vihāsi pabbatantare.

Pupphaṃ hemavantaṃ gayha,
Gacchaṃ vehāyasenahaṃ;
Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,
Oghatiṇ­ṇa­ma­nāsa­vaṃ.

Satta campa­ka­pupphāni,
sīse katvānahaṃ tadā;
Buddhassa abhiropesiṃ,
sayambhussa mahesino.

Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ puppha­ma­bhiro­payiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddha­pū­jāyi­daṃ phalaṃ.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā campa­ka­pupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Campa­ka­pupphi­yat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
