Therāpadāna

Sāla­kusumi­yavagga

2 Citaka­pūja­kat­the­ra­apadāna

“Jhāyamānassa bhagavato,
sikhino lokabandhuno;
Aṭṭha campa­ka­pupphāni,
citakaṃ abhiropayiṃ.

Ekatiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ puppha­ma­bhiro­payiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
citapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā citakapūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Citaka­pūja­kat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ dutiyaṃ.
