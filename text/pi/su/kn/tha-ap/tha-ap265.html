Therāpadāna

­Padu­muk­khipa­vagga

3 Aḍḍha­can­di­yat­the­ra­apadāna

“Tissassa kho bhagavato,
bodhiyā pādaputtame;
Aḍḍhacandaṃ mayā dinnaṃ,
dhara­ṇī­ru­hapādape.

Dvenavute ito kappe,
yaṃ canda­ma­bhiro­payiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
bodhipūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

Pañcavīse ito kappe,
devalo nāma khattiyo;
Satta­ratana­sam­panno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā aḍḍhacandiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Aḍḍha­can­diyat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ tatiyaṃ.
