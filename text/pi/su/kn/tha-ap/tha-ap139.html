Therāpadāna

Sobhitavagga

7 Attha­sandas­sakat­thera­apadāna

“Visālamāḷe āsīno,
addasaṃ lokanāyakaṃ;
Khīṇāsavaṃ balappattaṃ,
­bhik­khu­saṃgha­pu­rak­kha­taṃ.

Satasahassā tevijjā,
chaḷabhiññā mahiddhikā;
Parivārenti sambuddhaṃ,
ko disvā nappasīdati.

Ñāṇe upanidhā yassa,
na vijjati sadevake;
Anantañāṇaṃ sambuddhaṃ,
ko disvā nappasīdati.

Dhammakāyañca dīpentaṃ,
kevalaṃ ratanākaraṃ;
Vikappetuṃ na sakkonti,
ko disvā nappasīdati.

Imāhi tīhi gāthāhi,
nāra­dov­ha­ya­vacchalo;
Padumuttaraṃ thavitvāna,
sambuddhaṃ aparājitaṃ.

Tena cittappasādena,
bud­dhasan­thava­nena ca;
Kappānaṃ satasahassaṃ,
duggatiṃ nupapajjahaṃ.

Ito tiṃsakappasate,
sumitto nāma khattiyo;
Satta­ratana­sam­panno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā attha­sandas­sako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Attha­sandas­sakat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ sattamaṃ.
