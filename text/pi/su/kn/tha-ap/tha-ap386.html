Therāpadāna

Ava­ṭaphala­vagga

4 Pilak­kha­phala­dāyakat­thera­apadāna

“Vanantare buddhaṃ disvā,
atthadassiṃ mahāyasaṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
pilak­khas­sādadiṃ phalaṃ.

Aṭṭhārase kappasate,
yaṃ phalamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
phala­dā­nassi­daṃ phalaṃ.

Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā pilak­kha­pha­ladāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Pilak­kha­phala­dāyakat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ catutthaṃ.
