Therāpadāna

Kaṇikāravagga

8 Amoda­phali­yat­thera­apadāna

“Suvaṇṇavaṇṇaṃ sambuddhaṃ,
Āhutīnaṃ paṭiggahaṃ;
Rathiyaṃ paṭipajjantaṃ,
Āmodamadadiṃ phalaṃ.

Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ phalaṃ adadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
phala­dā­nassi­daṃ phalaṃ.

Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā amodaphaliyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Amoda­phali­yat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
