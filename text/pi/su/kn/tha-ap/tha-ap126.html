Therāpadāna

Sereyyavagga

4 ­Gan­dho­da­ki­yat­thera­apadāna

“Nisajja pāsādavare,
vipassiṃ addasaṃ jinaṃ;
Kakudhaṃ vilasantaṃva,
sabbaññuṃ tamanāsakaṃ.

Pāsā­dassā­vi­dūre ca,
gacchati lokanāyako;
Pabhā niddhāvate tassa,
yathā ca sataraṃsino.

Gandhodakañca paggayha,
buddhaseṭṭhaṃ samokiriṃ;
Tena cittappasādena,
tattha kālaṅkato ahaṃ.

Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ ­gan­dho­da­ka­mākiriṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddha­pū­jāyi­daṃ phalaṃ.

Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
sugandho nāma khattiyo;
Satta­ratana­sam­panno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā gandhodakiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

­Gan­dho­da­ki­yat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ catutthaṃ.
