Therāpadāna

Ava­ṭaphala­vagga

8 Pana­sa­pha­la­dāyakat­thera­apadāna

“Ajjuno nāma sambuddho,
himavante vasī tadā;
Caraṇena ca sampanno,
samādhikusalo muni.

Kumbhamattaṃ gahetvāna,
panasaṃ jīvajīvakaṃ;
Chattapaṇṇe ṭhapetvāna,
adāsiṃ satthuno ahaṃ.

Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ phalamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
phala­dā­nassi­daṃ phalaṃ.

Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā pana­sa­pha­la­dāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Pana­sa­pha­la­dāyakat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
