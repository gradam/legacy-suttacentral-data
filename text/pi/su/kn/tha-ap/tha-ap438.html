Therāpadāna

Ekavihārivagga

6 ­Kaḷamba­dāyakat­thera­apadāna

“Romaso nāma sambuddho,
vasate pabbatantare;
Kaḷambaṃ tassa pādāsiṃ,
pasanno sehi pāṇibhi.

Catunnavutito kappe,
yaṃ dānamadadiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
kaḷambassa idaṃ phalaṃ.

Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kaḷambadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

­Kaḷamba­dāyakat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
