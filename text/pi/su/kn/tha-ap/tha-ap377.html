Therāpadāna

Bodhi­vanda­na­vagga

5. Satta­paṇṇi­yat­thera­apadāna

“Sumano nāma sambuddho,
uppajji lokanāyako;
Pasannacitto sumano,
satta­paṇṇi­mapū­jayiṃ.

Satasahassito kappe,
Satta­paṇṇi­mapū­jayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
Satta­paṇṇi­pū­jāyi­daṃ phalaṃ.

Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sattapaṇṇiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Satta­paṇṇi­yat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ pañcamaṃ.
