Therāpadāna

Hatthivagga

5 Raṃsi­sañña­kat­the­ra­apadāna

“Udentaṃ sataraṃsiṃva,
pītaraṃsiṃva bhāṇumaṃ;
Byag­ghūsa­bhaṃva pavaraṃ,
sujātaṃ pabbatantare.

Buddhassa ānubhāvo so,
jalate pabbatantare;
Raṃse cittaṃ pasādetvā,
kappaṃ saggamhi modahaṃ.

Avasesesu kappesu,
kusalaṃ caritaṃ mayā;
Tena cittappasādena,
buddhā­nus­sati­yāpi ca.

Tiṃsa­kappa­sa­has­seto,
yaṃ saññamalabhiṃ tadā;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
bud­dhasaññā­yidaṃ phalaṃ.

Satta­paññā­sa­kappamhi,
eko āsiṃ janādhipo;
Sujāto nāma nāmena,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā raṃsisaññako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Raṃsi­sañña­kat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ pañcamaṃ.
