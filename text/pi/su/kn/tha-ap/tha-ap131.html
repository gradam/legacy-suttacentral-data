Therāpadāna

Sereyyavagga

9 Gaṇṭhi­pupphi­yat­thera­apadāna

“Suvaṇṇavaṇṇo sambuddho,
vipassī dakkhiṇāraho;
Purakkhato sāvakehi,
ārāmā abhinikkhami.

Disvānahaṃ buddhaseṭṭhaṃ,
sabbaññuṃ tamanāsakaṃ;
Pasannacitto sumano,
gaṇṭhipupphaṃ apūjayiṃ.

Tena cittappasādena,
dvipadindassa tādino;
Haṭṭho haṭṭhena cittena,
puna vandiṃ tathāgataṃ.

Ekanavutito kappe,
yaṃ puppha­ma­bhiro­payiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
buddha­pū­jāyi­daṃ phalaṃ.

Ekatālīsito kappe,
caraṇo nāma khattiyo;
Satta­ratana­sam­panno,
cakkavattī mahabbalo.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā gaṇṭhipupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Gaṇṭhi­pupphi­yat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ navamaṃ.
