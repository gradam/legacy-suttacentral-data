Therāpadāna

Jagati­dāyaka­vagga

9 Paṭṭi­pupphi­yat­thera­apadāna

“Nīharante sarīramhi,
vajjamānāsu bherisu;
Pasannacitto sumano,
paṭṭi­puppha­mapū­jayiṃ.

Satasahassito kappe,
yaṃ puppha­ma­bhipū­jayiṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
dehapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ.

Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,
… pe …
viharāmi anāsavo.

Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā paṭṭipupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Paṭṭi­pupphi­yat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ navamaṃ.
