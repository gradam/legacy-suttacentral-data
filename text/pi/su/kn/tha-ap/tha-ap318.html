Therāpadāna

Ārak­kha­dāyaka­vagga

6 Āsana­san­thavi­kat­thera­apadāna

“Cetiyaṃ uttamaṃ nāma,
sikhino lokabandhuno;
Araññe irīṇe vane,
andhā­hiṇ­ḍāma­haṃ tadā.

Pavanā nikkhamantena,
diṭṭhaṃ sīhāsanaṃ mayā;
Ekaṃsaṃ añjaliṃ katvā,
santhaviṃ lokanāyakaṃ.

Divasabhāgaṃ thavitvāna,
buddhaṃ lokagganāyakaṃ;
Haṭṭho haṭṭhena cittena,
imaṃ vācaṃ udīrayiṃ.

‘Namo te purisājañña,
namo te purisuttama;
Sabbaññūsi mahāvīra,
lokajeṭṭha narāsabha’.

Abhitthavitvā sikhinaṃ,
nimitta­karaṇe­nahaṃ;
Āsanaṃ abhivādetvā,
pakkāmiṃ uttarāmukho.

Ekattiṃse ito kappe,
yaṃ thaviṃ vadataṃ varaṃ;
Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,
thomanāya idaṃ phalaṃ.

Sattavīse ito kappe,
atulā satta āsu te;
Satta­ratana­sam­pannā,
cakkavattī mahabbalā.

Paṭisambhidā catasso,
… pe …
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā āsana­san­thaviko thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

Āsana­san­thavi­kat­theras­sāpadā­naṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
