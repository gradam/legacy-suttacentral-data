Kalyāṇavagga

6. Kaḷāya­muṭṭhi­jātaka

“Bālo vatāyaṃ duma­sā­kha­gocaro,
Paññā janinda nayimassa vijjati;
Kaḷāyamuṭṭhiṃ avakiriya kevalaṃ,
Ekaṃ kaḷāyaṃ patitaṃ gavesati”.

“Evameva mayaṃ rāja,
ye caññe atilobhino;
Appena bahuṃ jiyyāma,
kaḷāyeneva vānaro”ti.

Kaḷāya­muṭṭhi­jātakaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
