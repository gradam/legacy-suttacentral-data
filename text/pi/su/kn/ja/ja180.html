Jātaka

Dukanipāta

Kalyāṇavagga

10 Duddadajātaka

“Duddadaṃ dadamānānaṃ,
dukkaraṃ kamma kubbataṃ;
Asanto nānukubbanti,
sataṃ dhammo durannayo”.

“Tasmā satañca asataṃ,
nānā hoti ito gati;
Asanto nirayaṃ yanti,
santo sag­gaparā­yaṇāti.

Duddadajātakaṃ dasamaṃ.
Kalyāṇavaggo tatiyo.

Tassuddānaṃ

Susamañ­ña­mi­gādhi­bhū māṇavako,
Vāri­pahūta­rūpādic­cu­paṭṭhānā;
Sakaḷāya­sa­tin­du­ka­paṅka puna,
Satadhamma sududdadakena dasā”ti.
