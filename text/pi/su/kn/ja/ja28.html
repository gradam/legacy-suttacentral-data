Jātaka

Ekakanipāta

Kuruṅgavagga

8 Nandivisālajātaka

“Manuññameva bhāseyya,
nāmanuññaṃ kudācanaṃ;
Manuññaṃ bhāsamānassa,
garuṃ bhāraṃ udaddhari;
Dhanañca naṃ alābhesi,
tena cattamano ahū”ti.

Nandivisālajātakaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
