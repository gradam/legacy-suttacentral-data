Jātaka

Ekakanipāta

Littavagga

6 Tela­pattajā­taka

“Samatittikaṃ anavasekaṃ,
Telapattaṃ yathā parihareyya;
Evaṃ sacitta­ma­nurak­khe,
Patthayāno disaṃ agatapubban”ti.

Tela­patta­jātakaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
