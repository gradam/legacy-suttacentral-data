Jātaka

Dukanipāta

Kāsāvavagga

2 Cūḷanandiyajātaka

“Idaṃ tadācariyavaco,
pārāsariyo yadabravi;
Māsu tvaṃ akari pāpaṃ,
yaṃ tvaṃ pacchā kataṃ tape.

Yāni karoti puriso,
tāni attani passati;
Kalyāṇakārī kalyāṇaṃ,
pāpakārī ca pāpakaṃ;
Yādisaṃ vapate bījaṃ,
tādisaṃ harate phalan”ti.

Cūḷanandiyajātakaṃ dutiyaṃ.
