Jātaka

Ekakanipāta

Sīlavagga

1 ­Lak­kha­ṇa­mi­gajā­taka

“Hoti sīlavataṃ attho,
paṭi­san­thā­ra­vuttinaṃ;
Lakkhaṇaṃ passa āyantaṃ,
ñāti­saṅgha­pu­rak­kha­taṃ;
Atha passasimaṃ kāḷaṃ,
suvihīnaṃva ñātibhī”ti.

­Lak­kha­ṇa­miga­jātakaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
