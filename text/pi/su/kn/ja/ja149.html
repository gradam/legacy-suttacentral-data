Ekakanipāta

Kakaṇṭakavagga

9 Ekapaṇṇajātaka

“Ekapaṇṇo ayaṃ rukkho,
na bhūmyā caturaṅgulo;
Phalena visakappena,
mahāyaṃ kiṃ bhavissatī”ti.

Ekapaṇ­ṇa­jātakaṃ navamaṃ.
