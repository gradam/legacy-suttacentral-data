Jātaka

Ekakanipāta

Itthivagga

6 Mudu­lak­kha­ṇa­jātaka

“Ekā icchā pure āsi,
aladdhā mudulakkhaṇaṃ;
Yato laddhā aḷārakkhī,
icchā icchaṃ vijāyathā”ti.

Mudu­lak­kha­ṇa­jātakaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
