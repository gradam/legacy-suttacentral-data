Jātaka

Ekakanipāta

Kusanāḷivagga

2 Dummedhajātaka

“Yasaṃ laddhāna dummedho,
anatthaṃ carati attano;
Attano ca paresañca,
hiṃsāya paṭipajjatī”ti.

Dummedhajātakaṃ dutiyaṃ.
