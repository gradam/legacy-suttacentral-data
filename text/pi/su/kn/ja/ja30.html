Jātaka

Ekakanipāta

Kuruṅgavagga

10 Munikajātaka

“Mā munikassa pihayi,
āturannāni bhuñjati;
Appossukko bhusaṃ khāda,
etaṃ dīghā­yu­lak­kha­ṇan”ti.

Munikajātakaṃ dasamaṃ.
Kuruṅgavaggo tatiyo.

Tassuddānaṃ

Kuruṅgassa kukku­ra­go­javaro,
Puna vāḷavas­sa­siriv­hayano;
Mahiḷā­mu­kha­nā­manuñ­ña­varo,
Vahate dhura munikena dasāti.
