Jātaka

Ekakanipāta

Itthivagga

7 Ucchaṅgajātaka

“Ucchaṅge deva me putto,
Pathe dhāvantiyā pati;
Tañca desaṃ na passāmi,
Yato sodariyamānayeti.

Ucchaṅgajātakaṃ sattamaṃ.
