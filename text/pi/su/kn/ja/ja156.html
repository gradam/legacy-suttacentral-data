Jātaka

Dukanipāta

Daḷhavagga

6 Alīna­citta­jātaka

“Alīnacittaṃ nissāya,
pahaṭṭhā mahatī camū;
Kosalaṃ senāsantuṭṭhaṃ,
jīvaggāhaṃ agāhayi.

Evaṃ ­nissa­ya­sam­panno,
bhikkhu āraddhavīriyo;
Bhāvayaṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ,
yogakkhemassa pattiyā;
Pāpuṇe anupubbena,
sabba­saṃ­yoja­nak­kha­yan”ti.

Alīna­citta­jātakaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
