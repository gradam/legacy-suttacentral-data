Parosatavagga

9. Kuṇḍa­pūva­jātaka

“Yathanno puriso hoti,
tathannā tassa devatā;
Āharetaṃ kuṇḍapūvaṃ,
mā me bhāgaṃ vināsayā”ti.

Kuṇḍa­pūva­jātakaṃ navamaṃ.
