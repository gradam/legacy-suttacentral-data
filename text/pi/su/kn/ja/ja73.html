Jātaka

Ekakanipāta

Varuṇavagga

3 Saccaṃ­ki­rajātaka

“Saccaṃ kirevamāhaṃsu,
narā ekacciyā idha;
Kaṭṭhaṃ niplavitaṃ seyyo,
na tvevekacciyo naro”ti.

Saccaṃ­ki­rajāta­kaṃ tatiyaṃ.
