Jātaka

Dukanipāta

Asadisavagga

7 Catumaṭṭhajātaka

“Ucce viṭabhimāruyha,
mantayavho rahogatā;
Nīce oruyha mantavho,
migarājāpi sossati.

Yaṃ suvaṇṇo suvaṇṇena,
devo devena mantaye;
Kiṃ tettha catumaṭṭhassa,
bilaṃ pavisa jambukā”ti.

Catumaṭṭhajātakaṃ sattamaṃ.
