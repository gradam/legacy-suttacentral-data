Ekakanipāta

Asam­padā­na­vagga

7 Babbujātaka

“Yattheko labhate babbu,
dutiyo tattha jāyati;
Tatiyo ca catuttho ca,
idaṃ te babbukā bilan”ti.

Babbujātakaṃ sattamaṃ.
