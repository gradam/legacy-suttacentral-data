Āsīsavagga

5. Pañcā­vudha­jātaka

“Yo alīnena cittena,
alīnamanaso naro;
Bhāveti kusalaṃ dhammaṃ,
yogakkhemassa pattiyā;
Pāpuṇe anupubbena,
sabba­saṃ­yoja­nak­kha­yan”ti.

Pañcā­vudha­jātakaṃ pañcamaṃ.
