Jātaka

Ekakanipāta

Asam­padā­na­vagga

4 Jhāna­so­dha­na­jātaka

“Ye saññino tepi duggatā,
Yepi asaññino tepi duggatā;
Etaṃ ubhayaṃ vivajjaya,
Taṃ samā­patti­su­khaṃ anaṅgaṇan”ti.

Jhāna­so­dha­na­jātakaṃ catutthaṃ.
