Jātaka

Ekakanipāta

Āsīsavagga

6 Kañ­canak­khan­dha­jātaka

“Yo pahaṭṭhena cittena,
pahaṭṭhamanaso naro;
Bhāveti kusalaṃ dhammaṃ,
yogakkhemassa pattiyā;
Pāpuṇe anupubbena,
sabba­saṃ­yoja­nak­kha­yan”ti.

Kañ­canak­khan­dha­jātakaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
