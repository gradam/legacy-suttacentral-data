Jātaka

Tikanipāta

Kumbhavagga

10 Vakajātaka

“Parapāṇarodhā jīvanto,
maṃ­salo­hi­ta­bhojano;
Vako vataṃ samādāya,
upapajji uposathaṃ.

Tassa sakko vataññāya,
ajarū­pe­nupāgami;
Vītatapo ajjhappatto,
bhañji lohitapo tapaṃ.

Evameva idhekacce,
Samādānamhi dubbalā;
Lahuṃ karonti attānaṃ,
Vakova ajakāraṇā”ti. (501)

Vakajātakaṃ dasamaṃ.
Kumbhavaggo pañcamo.

Tassuddānaṃ

Varakumbha supatta­siriv­hayano,
Sucisammata bindusaro cusabho;
Saritaṃpati caṇḍi jarākapinā,
Atha makkaṭiyā vakakena dasāti.

Atha vagguddānaṃ

Saṅkappo padumo ceva,
Udapānena tatiyaṃ;
Abbhantaraṃ ghaṭabhedaṃ,
Tika­nipātam­hilaṅ­katanti.

Tikanipātaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
