Varuṇavagga

6. Asaṃkiyajātaka

“Asaṅkiyomhi gāmamhi,
araññe natthi me bhayaṃ;
Ujuṃ maggaṃ samārūḷho,
mettāya karuṇāya cā”ti.

Asaṃ­kiya­jātakaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
