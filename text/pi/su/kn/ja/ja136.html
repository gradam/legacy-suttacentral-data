Jātaka

Ekakanipāta

Asam­padā­na­vagga

6 Suvaṇ­ṇa­haṃsa­jātaka

* “Yaṃ laddhaṃ tena tuṭṭhabbaṃ,
Atilobho hi pāpako;
Haṃsarājaṃ gahetvāna,
Suvaṇṇā parihāyathā”ti.

Suvaṇ­ṇa­haṃsa­jātakaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
