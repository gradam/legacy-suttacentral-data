Jātaka

Dukanipāta

Kalyāṇavagga

7 Tindukajātaka

“Dhanuhatthakalāpehi,
nettiṃ savaradhāribhi;
Samantā parikiṇṇamha,
kathaṃ mokkho bhavissati”.

“Appeva bahukiccānaṃ,
attho jāyetha koci naṃ;
Atthi rukkhassa acchinnaṃ,
khajjathaññeva tindukan”ti.

Tindukajātakaṃ sattamaṃ.
