Jātaka

Ekakanipāta

Asam­padā­na­vagga

9 Ubhato­bhaṭ­ṭha­jātaka

“Akkhī bhinnā paṭo naṭṭho,
sakhigehe ca bhaṇḍanaṃ;
Ubhato paduṭṭhā kammantā,
udakamhi thalamhi cā”ti.

Ubhato­bhaṭ­ṭha­jātakaṃ navamaṃ.
