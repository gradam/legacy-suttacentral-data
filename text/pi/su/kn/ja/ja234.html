Jātaka

Dukanipāta

Upāhanavagga

4 Asitābhūjātaka

“Tvameva dānimakara,
Yaṃ kāmo byagamā tayi;
Soyaṃ appaṭisandhiko,
Kharachinnaṃva renukaṃ”.

“Atricchaṃ atilobhena,
atilobhamadena ca;
Evaṃ hāyati atthamhā,
ahaṃva asitābhuyā”ti.

Asitābhūjātakaṃ catutthaṃ.
