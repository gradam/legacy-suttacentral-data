Jātaka

Tikanipāta

Kumbhavagga

6 Samuddajātaka

“Ko nāyaṃ loṇatoyasmiṃ,
samantā paridhāvati;
Macche makare ca vāreti,
ūmīsu ca vihaññati”.

“Anantapāyī sakuṇo,
atittoti disāsuto;
Samuddaṃ pātumicchāmi,
sāgaraṃ saritaṃ patiṃ”.

“So ayaṃ hāyati ceva,
pūrate ca mahodadhi;
Nāssa nāyati pītanto,
apeyyo kira sāgaro”ti.

Samuddajātakaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
