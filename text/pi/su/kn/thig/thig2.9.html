Therīgāthā

Dukanipāta

9 Abhayā­therī­gāthā

“Abhaye bhiduro kāyo,
yattha satā puthujjanā;
­Nikkhi­pis­sāmi­maṃ dehaṃ,
sampajānā satīmatī.

Bahūhi dukkhadhammehi,
appamādaratāya me;
Taṇhakkhayo anuppatto,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanan”ti.

… Abhayā therī ….
