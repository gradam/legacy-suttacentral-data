Therīgāthā

Ekakanipāta

8 Mittā­therī­gāthā

“Saddhāya pabbajitvāna,
mitte mittaratā bhava;
Bhāvehi kusale dhamme,
yogakkhemassa pattiyā”ti.

… Mittā therī ….
