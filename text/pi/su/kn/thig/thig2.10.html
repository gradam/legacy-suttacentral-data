Therīgāthā

Dukanipāta

10 Sāmātherīgāthā

“Catukkhattuṃ pañcakkhattuṃ,
vihārā upanikkhamiṃ;
Aladdhā cetaso santiṃ,
citte avasavattinī;
Tassā me aṭṭhamī ratti,
yato taṇhā samūhatā.

Bahūhi dukkhadhammehi,
appamādaratāya me;
Taṇhakkhayo anuppatto,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanan”ti.

… Sāmā therī ….

Dukanipāto niṭṭhito.
