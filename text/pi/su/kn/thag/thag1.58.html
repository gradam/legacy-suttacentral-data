Chaṭṭhavagga

1.58. Ramaṇī­ya­kuṭikat­thera­gāthā

“Ramaṇīyā me kuṭikā,
Saddhādeyyā manoramā;
Na me attho kumārīhi,
Yesaṃ attho tahiṃ gacchatha nāriyo”ti.

… Ramaṇīyakuṭiko thero ….
