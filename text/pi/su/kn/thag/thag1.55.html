Theragāthā

Ekakanipāta

Chaṭṭhavagga

1.55 Añjana­vaniyat­thera­gāthā

“Āsandiṃ kuṭikaṃ katvā,
ogayha añjanaṃ vanaṃ;
Tisso vijjā anuppattā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanan”ti.

… Añjanavaniyo thero ….
