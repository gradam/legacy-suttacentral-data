Theragāthā

Dukanipāta

Paṭhamavagga

2.8. Surā­dhat­thera­gāthā

“Khīṇā hi mayhaṃ jāti,
vusitaṃ jinasāsanaṃ;
Pahīno jālasaṅkhāto,
bhavanetti samūhatā.

Yassatthāya pabbajito,
agāras­mā­nagā­riyaṃ;
So me attho anuppatto,
sabba­saṃ­yoja­nak­khayo”ti.

… Surādho thero ….
