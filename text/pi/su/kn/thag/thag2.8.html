Theragāthā

Dukanipāta

Paṭhamavagga

Surādhattheragāthā

“Khīṇā hi mayhaṃ jāti,
vusitaṃ jinasāsanaṃ;
Pahīno jālasaṅkhāto,
bhavanetti samūhatā.

Yassatthāya pabbajito,
agārasmānagāriyaṃ;
So me attho anuppatto,
sabbasaṃyojanakkhayo”ti.

…  Surādho thero… .
