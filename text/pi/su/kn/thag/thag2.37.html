Theragāthā

Dukanipāta

Catutthavagga

7 Soṇa­po­ṭiriyat­thera­gāthā

“Na tāva supituṃ hoti,
ratti ­nak­khat­ta­mālinī;
Paṭi­jaggi­tume­vesā,
ratti hoti vijānatā”.

“Hatthik­khan­dhā­vapati­taṃ,
kuñjaro ce anukkame;
Saṅgāme me mataṃ seyyo,
yañce jīve parājito”ti.

… Soṇo poṭiriyo thero ….
