Theragāthā

Ekakanipāta

Pañcamavagga

1.47 Ujjayat­thera­gāthā

“Namo te buddha vīratthu,
vippamuttosi sabbadhi;
Tuyhāpadāne viharaṃ,
viharāmi anāsavo”ti.

… Ujjayo thero ….
