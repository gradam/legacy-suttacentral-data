Ekakanipāta

Dasamavagga

1.91 Pari­puṇṇa­kat­thera­gāthā

“Na tathā mataṃ satarasaṃ,
Sudhannaṃ yaṃ mayajja paribhuttaṃ;
Apari­mita­dassinā gotamena,
Buddhena desito dhammo”ti.

… Paripuṇṇako thero ….
