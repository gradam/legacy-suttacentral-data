Ekakanipāta

Catutthavagga

1.39. Tissat­thera­gāthā

“Sattiyā viya omaṭṭho,
ḍayhamānova matthake;
­Kāmarā­gap­pahānāya,
sato bhikkhu paribbaje”ti.

… Tisso thero ….
