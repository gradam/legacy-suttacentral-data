Theragāthā

Ekakanipāta

Dutiyavagga

4 Sivaka­sāma­ṇera­gāthā

“Upajjhāyo maṃ avaca,
ito gacchāma sīvaka;
Gāme me vasati kāyo,
araññaṃ me gato mano;
Semānakopi gacchāmi,
natthi saṅgo vijānatan”ti.

… Vanavacchassa therassa (siviko) sāmaṇero ….
