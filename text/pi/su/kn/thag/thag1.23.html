Theragāthā

Ekakanipāta

Tatiyavagga

1.23. Gosālat­thera­gāthā

“Ahaṃ kho veḷugumbasmiṃ,
bhutvāna madhupāyasaṃ;
Padakkhiṇaṃ sammasanto,
khandhānaṃ udayabbayaṃ;
Sānuṃ paṭigamissāmi,
viveka­manub­rūha­yan”ti.

… Gosālo thero ….
