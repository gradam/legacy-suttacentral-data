Theragāthā

Ekakanipāta

Navamavagga

6 Nāgitattheragāthā

“Ito bahiddhā puthu aññavādinaṃ,
Maggo na nibbānagamo yathā ayaṃ;
Itissu saṃghaṃ bhagavānusāsati,
Satthā sayaṃ pāṇitaleva dassayan”ti.

… Nāgito thero ….
