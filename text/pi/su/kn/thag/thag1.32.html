Theragāthā

Ekakanipāta

Catutthavagga

2 Suppiyattheragāthā

“Ajaraṃ jīramānena,
tappamānena nibbutiṃ;
Nimiyaṃ paramaṃ santiṃ,
yogakkhemaṃ anuttaran”ti.

… Suppiyo thero ….
