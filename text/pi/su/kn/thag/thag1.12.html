Theragāthā

Ekakanipāta

Dutiyavagga

Mahāvacchattheragāthā

“Paññābalī sīlavatūpapanno,
Samāhito jhānarato satīmā;
Yadatthiyaṃ bhojanaṃ bhuñjamāno,
Kaṅkhetha kālaṃ idha vītarāgo”ti.

…  Mahāvaccho thero… .
