Theragāthā

Dukanipāta

Catutthavagga

Usabhattheragāthā

“Ambapallavasaṅkāsaṃ,
aṃse katvāna cīvaraṃ;
Nisinno hatthigīvāyaṃ,
gāmaṃ piṇḍāya pāvisiṃ.

Hatthikkhandhato oruyha,
saṃvegaṃ alabhiṃ tadā;
Sohaṃ ditto tadā santo,
patto me āsavakkhayo”ti.

…  Usabho thero… .
