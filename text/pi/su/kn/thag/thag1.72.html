Theragāthā

Ekakanipāta

Aṭṭhamavagga

1.72 Ātumat­thera­gāthā

“Yathā kaḷīro susu vaḍḍhitaggo,
Dunnikkhamo hoti pasākhajāto;
Evaṃ ahaṃ bhari­yā­yānitāya,
Anumaññaṃ maṃ pabbajitomhi dānī”ti.

… Ātumo thero ….
