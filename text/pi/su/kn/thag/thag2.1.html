Theragāthā

Dukanipāta

Paṭhamavagga

1 Uttarat­thera­gāthā

“Natthi koci bhavo nicco,
saṅkhārā vāpi sassatā;
Uppajjanti ca te khandhā,
cavanti aparāparaṃ.

Etamādīnavaṃ ñatvā,
bhavenamhi anatthiko;
Nissaṭo sabbakāmehi,
patto me āsavakkhayo”ti.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā uttaro thero gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.
