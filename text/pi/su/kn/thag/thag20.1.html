Paṭhamavagga

20.1. ­Mahā­mog­gallā­nat­thera­gāthā

“11501146104p_19Th1_1512Āraññikā piṇḍapātikā,
uñchāpattāgate ratā;
Dālemu maccuno senaṃ,
ajjhattaṃ susamāhitā.

11511147p_19Th1_1513Āraññikā piṇḍapātikā,
uñchāpattāgate ratā;
Dhunāma maccuno senaṃ,
naḷāgāraṃva kuñjaro.

11521148p_19Th1_1514Rukkhamūlikā sātatikā,
uñchāpattāgate ratā;
Dālemu maccuno senaṃ,
ajjhattaṃ susamāhitā.

11531149p_19Th1_1515Rukkhamūlikā sātatikā,
uñchāpattāgate ratā;
Dhunāma maccuno senaṃ,
naḷāgāraṃva kuñjaro”.

“11541150p_19Th1_1516Aṭṭhi­kaṅ­kala­kuṭike,
maṃsan­hārupa­sibbite;
Dhiratthu pure duggandhe,
paragatte mamāyase.

11551151p_19Th1_1517Gūthabhaste taconaddhe,
ura­gaṇḍi­pisā­cini;
Nava sotāni te kāye,
yāni sandanti sabbadā.

11561152p_19Th1_1518Tava sarīraṃ navasotaṃ,
Duggandhakaraṃ paribandhaṃ;
Bhikkhu parivajjayate taṃ,
Mīḷhaṃ ca yathā sucikāmo.

11571153p_19Th1_1519Evañce taṃ jano jaññā,
yathā jānāmi taṃ ahaṃ;
Ārakā parivajjeyya,
gūthaṭṭhānaṃva pāvuse”.

“11581154p_19Th1_1520Evametaṃ mahāvīra,
yathā samaṇa bhāsasi;
Ettha ceke visīdanti,
paṅkamhiva jaraggavo”.

“11591155p_19Th1_1521Ākāsamhi haliddiyā,
yo maññetha rajetave;
Aññena vāpi raṅgena,
vighā­tudaya­meva taṃ.

11601156p_19Th1_1522Tadākāsasamaṃ cittaṃ,
ajjhattaṃ susamāhitaṃ;
Mā pāpacitte āsādi,
aggikhandhaṃva pakkhimā”.

“11611157p_19Th1_1523Passa cittakataṃ bimbaṃ,
arukāyaṃ samussitaṃ;
Āturaṃ bahusaṅkappaṃ,
yassa natthi dhuvaṃ ṭhiti.

1162p_19Th1_1524Passa cittakataṃ rūpaṃ,
maṇinā kuṇḍalena ca;
Aṭṭhiṃ tacena onaddhaṃ,
saha vatthehi sobhati.

1163p_19Th1_1525Alattakakatā pādā,
mukhaṃ cuṇṇa­ka­mak­khi­taṃ;
Alaṃ bālassa mohāya,
no ca pāragavesino.

1164p_19Th1_1526Aṭṭhapadakatā kesā,
nettā añjanamakkhitā;
Alaṃ bālassa mohāya,
no ca pāragavesino.

1165p_19Th1_1527Añjanīva navā cittā,
pūtikāyo alaṅkato;
Alaṃ bālassa mohāya,
no ca pāragavesino.

1166p_19Th1_1528Odahi migavo pāsaṃ,
nāsadā vāguraṃ migo;
Bhutvā nivāpaṃ gacchāma,
kaddante migabandhake.

1167p_19Th1_1529Chinno pāso migavassa,
nāsadā vāguraṃ migo;
Bhutvā nivāpaṃ gacchāma,
socante migaluddake”.

“11681158p_19Th1_1530Tadāsi yaṃ bhiṃsanakaṃ,
tadāsi lomahaṃsanaṃ;
Anekākā­ra­sam­panne,
sāriputtamhi nibbute.

* 11691159105p_19Th1_1531Aniccā vata saṅkhārā,
uppā­davaya­dham­mino;
Upajjitvā nirujjhanti,
tesaṃ vūpasamo sukho.

11701160p_19Th1_1532Sukhumaṃ te paṭivijjhanti,
vālaggaṃ usunā yathā;
Ye pañcakkhandhe passanti,
parato no ca attato.

11711161p_19Th1_1533Ye ca passanti saṅkhāre,
parato no ca attato;
Paccabyādhiṃsu nipuṇaṃ,
vālaggaṃ usunā yathā.

* 11721162p_19Th1_1534Sattiyā viya omaṭṭho,
ḍayhamānova matthake;
­Kāmarā­gap­pahānāya,
sato bhikkhu paribbaje.

11731163p_19Th1_1535Sattiyā viya omaṭṭho,
ḍayhamānova matthake;
Bhava­rāgap­pahānāya,
sato bhikkhu paribbaje.

11741164p_19Th1_1536Codito bhāvitattena,
sarīran­tima­dhārinā;
Migāramā­tu­pāsādaṃ,
pādaṅguṭṭhena kampayiṃ.

* 11751165p_19Th1_1537Nayidaṃ sithi­la­mārab­bha,
nayidaṃ appena thāmasā;
Nib­bā­na­madhi­gantab­baṃ,
sabba­gantha­pa­moca­naṃ.

11761166p_19Th1_1538Ayañca daharo bhikkhu,
ayamutta­ma­poriso;
Dhāreti antimaṃ dehaṃ,
jetvā māraṃ savāhiniṃ.

11771167p_19Th1_1539Vivara­manupa­bhanti vijjutā,
Vebhārassa ca paṇḍavassa ca;
Nagavivaragato jhāyati,
Putto appaṭimassa tādino.

11781168p_19Th1_1540Upasanto uparato,
pantasenāsano muni;
Dāyādo buddha­seṭṭhassa,
brahmunā abhivandito”.

“11791169p_19Th1_1541Upasantaṃ uparataṃ,
pantasenāsanaṃ muniṃ;
Dāyādaṃ buddha­seṭṭhassa,
vanda brāhmaṇa kassapaṃ.

11801170p_19Th1_1542Yo ca jātisataṃ gacche,
sabbā brāhmaṇajātiyo;
Sottiyo vedasampanno,
manussesu punappunaṃ.

11811171p_19Th1_1543Ajjhāyakopi ce assa,
tiṇṇaṃ vedāna pāragū;
Etassa vandanāyetaṃ,
kalaṃ nāgghati soḷasiṃ.

11821172p_19Th1_1544Yo so aṭṭha vimokkhāni,
purebhattaṃ aphassayi;
Anulomaṃ paṭilomaṃ,
tato piṇḍāya gacchati.

11831173p_19Th1_1545Tādisaṃ bhikkhuṃ māsādi,
māttānaṃ khaṇi brāhmaṇa;
Abhippasādehi manaṃ,
arahantamhi tādine;
Khippaṃ pañjaliko vanda,
mā te vijaṭi matthakaṃ”.

“11841174p_19Th1_1546Neso passati saddhammaṃ,
saṃsārena purakkhato;
Adhogamaṃ jimhapathaṃ,
kummag­ga­manu­dhāvati.

11851175p_19Th1_1547Kimīva mīḷhasallitto,
saṅkhāre adhimucchito;
Pagāḷho lābhasakkāre,
tuccho gacchati poṭṭhilo”.

“11861176p_19Th1_1548Imañca passa āyantaṃ,
sāriputtaṃ sudassanaṃ;
Vimuttaṃ ubhatobhāge,
ajjhattaṃ susamāhitaṃ.

11871177106p_19Th1_1549Visallaṃ khīṇasaṃyogaṃ,
tevijjaṃ maccuhāyinaṃ;
Dakkhiṇeyyaṃ manussānaṃ,
puññakkhettaṃ anuttaraṃ”.

“11881178p_19Th1_1550Ete sambahulā devā,
iddhimanto yasassino;
Dasa devasahassāni,
sabbe brahmapurohitā;
Moggallānaṃ namassantā,
tiṭṭhanti pañjalīkatā.

‘11891179p_19Th1_1551Namo te purisājañña,
namo te purisuttama;
Yassa te āsavā khīṇā,
dakkhiṇeyyosi mārisa’.

11901180p_19Th1_1552Pūjito naradevena,
uppanno maraṇābhibhū;
Puṇḍarīkaṃva toyena,
saṅ­khā­renu­palip­pati.

11911181p_19Th1_1553Yassa muhuttena sahassadhā loko,
Saṃvidito sabrahmakappo vasi;
Iddhiguṇe cutupapāte kāle,
Passati devatā sa bhikkhu”.

“11921182p_19Th1_1554Sāriputtova paññāya,
sīlena upasamena ca;
Yopi pāraṅgato bhikkhu,
etāvaparamo siyā.

11931183p_19Th1_1555Koṭi­sata­sahas­sassa,
Attabhāvaṃ khaṇena nimmine;
Ahaṃ vikubbanāsu kusalo,
Vasībhūtomhi iddhiyā.

11941184p_19Th1_1556Samā­dhi­vijjā­vasi­pāramī­gato,
Mog­gallā­na­gotto asitassa sāsane;
Dhīro samucchindi samāhitindriyo,
Nāgo yathā pūtilataṃva bandhanaṃ.

11951185p_19Th1_1557Pariciṇṇo mayā satthā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ;
Ohito garuko bhāro,
bhavanetti samūhatā.

11961186p_19Th1_1558Yassa catthāya pabbajito,
agāras­mā­nagā­riyaṃ;
So me attho anuppatto,
sabba­saṃ­yoja­nak­khayo.

* 11971187p_19Th1_1559Kīdiso nirayo āsi,
yattha dussī apaccatha;
Vidhuraṃ sāvakamāsajja,
kakusandhañca brāhmaṇaṃ.

* 11981188p_19Th1_1560Sataṃ āsi ayosaṅkū,

Īdiso nirayo āsi,
yattha dussī apaccatha;
Vidhuraṃ sāvakamāsajja,
kakusandhañca brāhmaṇaṃ.

bhikkhu buddhassa sāvako;

kaṇha dukkhaṃ nigacchasi.

12081198p_19Th1_1570Yo brahmānaṃ paripucchati,
Sudhammāyaṃ ṭhito sabhaṃ;
Ajjāpi tyāvuso sā diṭṭhi,
Yā te diṭṭhi pure ahu;
Passasi vītivattantaṃ,
Brahmaloke pabhassaraṃ.

12091199p_19Th1_1571Tassa brahmā viyākāsi,
pañhaṃ puṭṭho yathātathaṃ;

12101200p_19Th1_1572Passāmi vītivattantaṃ,
brahmaloke pabhassaraṃ;

12131203p_19Th1_1575Yo etamabhijānāti,

12141204p_19Th1_1576Na ve aggi cetayati,
