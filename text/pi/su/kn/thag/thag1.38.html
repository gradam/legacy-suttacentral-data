Theragāthā

Ekakanipāta

Catutthavagga

8 Gavampatittheragāthā

“Yo iddhiyā sarabhuṃ aṭṭhapesi,
So gavampati asito anejo;
Taṃ sabbasaṅgātigataṃ mahāmuniṃ,
Devā namassanti bhavassa pāragun”ti.

… Gavampatitthero ….
