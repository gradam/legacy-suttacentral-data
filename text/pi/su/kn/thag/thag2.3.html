Theragāthā

Dukanipāta

Paṭhamavagga

3 Valliyattheragāthā

“Makkaṭo pañcadvārāyaṃ,
kuṭikāyaṃ pasakkiya;
Dvārena anupariyeti,
ghaṭṭayanto muhuṃ muhuṃ.

Tiṭṭha makkaṭa mā dhāvi,
na hi te taṃ yathā pure;
Niggahītosi paññāya,
neva dūraṃ gamissatī”ti.

… Valliyo thero ….
