Theragāthā

Ekakanipāta

Dasamavagga

10 (Dutiya) Devasabhattheragāthā

“Sammappadhānasampanno,
Satipaṭṭhānagocaro;
Vimuttikusumasañchanno,
Parinibbissatyanāsavo”ti.

… Devasabho thero ….
Vaggo dasamo.

Tassuddānaṃ

Paripuṇṇako ca vijayo,
erako mettajī muni;
Cakkhupālo khaṇḍasumano,
tisso ca abhayo tathā;
Uttiyo ca mahāpañño,
thero devasabhopi cāti.
