Theragāthā

Ekakanipāta

Pañcamavagga

5 Ramaṇī­ya­vihārit­thera­gāthā

“Yathāpi bhaddo ājañño,
khalitvā patitiṭṭhati;
Evaṃ das­sa­nasam­pannaṃ,
sammā­sambud­dha­sāvakan”ti.

… Ramaṇī­ya­vihārit­thero ….
