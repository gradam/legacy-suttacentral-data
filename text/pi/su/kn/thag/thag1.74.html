Theragāthā

Ekakanipāta

Aṭṭhamavagga

4 Suyāmanattheragāthā

“Kāmacchando ca byāpādo,
Thinamiddhañca bhikkhuno;
Uddhaccaṃ vicikicchā ca,
Sabbasova na vijjatī”ti.

… Suyāmano thero ….
