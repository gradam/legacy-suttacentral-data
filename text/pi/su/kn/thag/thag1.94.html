Theragāthā

Ekakanipāta

Dasamavagga

1.94 Metta­jit­thera­gāthā

“Namo hi tassa bhagavato,
sakyaputtassa sirīmato;
Tenāyaṃ aggappattena,
aggadhammo sudesito”ti.

… Mettaji thero ….
