Theragāthā

Aṭṭhakanipāta

Paṭhamavagga

8.2 Siri­mittat­thera­gāthā

“Akkodha­nonu­panāhī,
amāyo rittapesuṇo;
Sa ve tādisako bhikkhu,
evaṃ pecca na socati.

Akkodha­nonu­panāhī,
amāyo rittapesuṇo;
Guttadvāro sadā bhikkhu,
evaṃ pecca na socati.

Akkodha­nonu­panāhī,
amāyo rittapesuṇo;
Kalyāṇasīlo so bhikkhu,
evaṃ pecca na socati.

Akkodha­nonu­panāhī,
amāyo rittapesuṇo;
Kalyāṇamitto so bhikkhu,
evaṃ pecca na socati.

Akkodha­nonu­panāhī,
amāyo rittapesuṇo;
Kalyāṇapañño so bhikkhu,
evaṃ pecca na socati.

* Yassa saddhā tathāgate,
acalā suppatiṭṭhitā;
Sīlañca yassa kalyāṇaṃ,
ariyakantaṃ pasaṃsitaṃ.

Saṃghe pasādo yassatthi,
ujubhūtañca dassanaṃ;
‘Adaliddo’ti taṃ āhu,
amoghaṃ tassa jīvitaṃ.

Tasmā saddhañca sīlañca,
pasādaṃ dhammadassanaṃ;
Anuyuñjetha medhāvī,
saraṃ buddhāna sāsanan”ti.

… Sirimitto thero ….
