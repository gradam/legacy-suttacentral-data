Ekakanipāta

Ekādasamavagga

7 Dhamma­savat­thera­gāthā

“Pabbajiṃ tulayitvāna,
agāras­mā­nagā­riyaṃ;
Tisso vijjā anuppattā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanan”ti.

… Dhammasavo thero ….
