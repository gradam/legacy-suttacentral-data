Theragāthā

Tikanipāta

Paṭhamavagga

3.5 Mātaṅ­ga­puttat­thera­gāthā

“Atisītaṃ atiuṇhaṃ,
atisāyamidaṃ ahu;
Iti vis­saṭṭha­kam­mante,
khaṇā accenti māṇave.

Yo ca sītañca uṇhañca,
tiṇā bhiyyo na maññati;
Karaṃ purisakiccāni,
so sukhā na vihāyati.

Dabbaṃ kusaṃ poṭakilaṃ,
usīraṃ muñjapabbajaṃ;
Urasā panudissāmi,
viveka­manub­rūha­yan”ti.

… Mātaṅgaputto thero ….
