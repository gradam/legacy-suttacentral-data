Theragāthā

Ekakanipāta

Sattamavagga

4 Vimala­koṇ­ḍaññat­thera­gāthā

“Dumavhayāya uppanno,
jāto paṇḍaraketunā;
Ketuhā ketunāyeva,
mahāketuṃ padhaṃsayī”ti.

… Vimalakoṇḍañño thero ….
