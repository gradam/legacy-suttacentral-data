Ekakanipāta

Ekādasamavagga

1.103 Bandhu­rat­thera­gāthā

“Nāhaṃ etena atthiko,
Sukhito dhammarasena tappito;
Pitvā rasag­ga­muttamaṃ,
Na ca kāhāmi visena santhavan”ti.

… Bandhuro thero ….
