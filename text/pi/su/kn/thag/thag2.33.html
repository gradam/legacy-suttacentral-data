Theragāthā

Dukanipāta

Catutthavagga

3 Upavāṇattheragāthā

“Arahaṃ sugato loke,
vātehābādhiko muni;
Sace uṇhodakaṃ atthi,
munino dehi brāhmaṇa.

Pūjito pūjaneyyānaṃ,
sakkareyyāna sakkato;
Apacitopaceyyānaṃ,
tassa icchāmi hātave”ti.

… Upavāṇo thero ….
