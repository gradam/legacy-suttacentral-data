Theragāthā

Ekakanipāta

Dutiyavagga

5 Kuṇḍadhānattheragāthā

“Pañca chinde pañca jahe,
pañca cuttari bhāvaye;
Pañcasaṅgātigo bhikkhu,
oghatiṇṇoti vuccatī”ti.

… Kuṇḍadhāno thero ….
