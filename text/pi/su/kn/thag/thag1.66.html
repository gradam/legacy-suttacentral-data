Theragāthā

Ekakanipāta

Sattamavagga

Meghiyattheragāthā

“Anusāsi mahāvīro,
sabbadhammāna pāragū;
Tassāhaṃ dhammaṃ sutvāna,
vihāsiṃ santike sato;
Tisso vijjā anuppattā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanan”ti.

…  Meghiyo thero… .
