Theragāthā

Ekakanipāta

Navamavagga

1.88 Ajjunat­thera­gāthā

“Asakkhiṃ vata attānaṃ,
uddhātuṃ udakā thalaṃ;
Vuyhamāno mahogheva,
saccāni paṭivijjhahan”ti.

… Ajjuno thero ….
