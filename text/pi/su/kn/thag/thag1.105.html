Theragāthā

Ekakanipāta

Ekādasamavagga

1.105 Mali­tavam­bhat­thera­gāthā

“Ukkaṇṭhitopi na vase,
ramamānopi pakkame;
Na tve­vānat­tha­saṃhi­taṃ,
vase vāsaṃ vicakkhaṇo”ti.

… Malitavambho thero ….
