Theragāthā

Ekakanipāta

Sattamavagga

1.63 ­Pak­khat­thera­gāthā

“Cutā patanti patitā,
giddhā ca punarāgatā;
Kataṃ kiccaṃ rataṃ rammaṃ,
sukhe­nanvāga­taṃ sukhan”ti.

… Pakkho thero ….
