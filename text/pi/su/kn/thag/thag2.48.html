Theragāthā

Dukanipāta

Pañcamavagga

8 Vajji­tat­thera­gāthā

“Saṃsaraṃ dīghamaddhānaṃ,
gatīsu parivattisaṃ;
Apassaṃ ariyasaccāni,
andhabhūto puthujjano.

Tassa me appamattassa,
saṃsārā vinaḷīkatā;
Sabbā gatī samucchinnā,
natthi dāni punabbhavo”ti.

… Vajjito thero ….
