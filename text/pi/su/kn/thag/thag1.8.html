Theragāthā

Ekakanipāta

Paṭhamavagga

Vīrattheragāthā

“Yo duddamiyo damena danto,
Vīro santusito vitiṇṇakaṅkho;
Vijitāvī apetalomahaṃso,
Vīro so parinibbuto ṭhitatto”ti.

Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā vīro thero gāthaṃ abhāsitthāti.
