Theragāthā

Ekakanipāta

Dvādasamavagga

1.119 Vajji­puttat­thera­gāthā

“Rukkha­mūla­gahanaṃ pasakkiya,
Nibbānaṃ hadayasmiṃ opiya;
Jhāya gotama mā ca pamādo,
Kiṃ te biḷibiḷikā karissatī”ti.

… Vajjiputto thero ….
