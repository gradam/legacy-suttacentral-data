Theragāthā

Ekakanipāta

Sattamavagga

2 Vajji­puttat­thera­gāthā

“Ekakā mayaṃ araññe viharāma,
Apaviddhaṃva vanasmiṃ dārukaṃ;
Tassa me bahukā pihayanti,
Nerayikā viya saggagāminan”ti.

… Vajjiputto thero ….
