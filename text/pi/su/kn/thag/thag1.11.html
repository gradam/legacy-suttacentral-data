Theragāthā

Ekakanipāta

Dutiyavagga

1 Cūḷa­vacchat­thera­gāthā

“Pāmojjabahulo bhikkhu,
dhamme buddhap­pa­vedite;
Adhigacche padaṃ santaṃ,
saṅ­khā­rū­pasa­maṃ sukhan”ti.

… Cūḷavaccho thero ….
