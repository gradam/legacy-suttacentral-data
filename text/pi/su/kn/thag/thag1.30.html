Theragāthā

Ekakanipāta

Tatiyavagga

10 Uttiyattheragāthā

“Ābādhe me samuppanne,
sati me udapajjatha;
Ābādho me samuppanno,
kālo me nappamajjitun”ti.

… Uttiyo thero ….
Vaggo tatiyo.

Tassuddānaṃ

Nigrodho cittako thero,
gosālathero sugandho;
Nandiyo abhayo thero,
thero lomasakaṅgiyo;
Jambugāmikaputto ca,
hārito uttiyo isīti.
