Theragāthā

Ekakanipāta

Tatiyavagga

1.21 Nigrodhat­thera­gāthā

“Nāhaṃ bhayassa bhāyāmi,
Satthā no amatassa kovido;
Yattha bhayaṃ nāvatiṭṭhati,
Tena maggena vajanti bhikkhavo”ti.

… Nigrodho thero ….
