Theragāthā

Ekakanipāta

Dasamavagga

8 Abhayattheragāthā

“Rūpaṃ disvā sati muṭṭhā,
Piyaṃ nimittaṃ manasikaroto;
Sārattacitto vedeti,
Tañca ajjhosa tiṭṭhati;
Tassa vaḍḍhanti āsavā,
Bhavamūlopagāmino”ti.

… Abhayo thero ….
