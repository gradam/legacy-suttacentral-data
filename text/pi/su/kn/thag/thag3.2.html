Theragāthā

Tikanipāta

Paṭhamavagga

Paccayattheragāthā

“Pañcāhāhaṃ pabbajito,
sekho appattamānaso;
Vihāraṃ me paviṭṭhassa,
cetaso paṇidhī ahu.

Nāsissaṃ na pivissāmi,
vihārato na nikkhame;
Napi passaṃ nipātessaṃ,
taṇhāsalle anūhate.

Tassa mevaṃ viharato,
passa vīriyaparakkamaṃ;
Tisso vijjā anuppattā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanan”ti.

…  Paccayo thero… .
