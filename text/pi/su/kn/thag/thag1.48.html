Theragāthā

Ekakanipāta

Pañcamavagga

1.48 Sañ­jayat­thera­gāthā

“Yato ahaṃ pabbajito,
Agāras­mā­nagā­riyaṃ;
Nābhijānāmi saṅkappaṃ,
Anariyaṃ dosasaṃhitan”ti.

… Sañjayo thero ….
