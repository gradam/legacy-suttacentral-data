Theragāthā

Ekakanipāta

Dasamavagga

2 Vijayat­thera­gāthā

“Yassāsavā parikkhīṇā,
āhāre ca anissito;
Suññato animitto ca,
vimokkho yassa gocaro;
Ākāseva sakuntānaṃ,
padaṃ tassa durannayan”ti.

… Vijayo thero ….
