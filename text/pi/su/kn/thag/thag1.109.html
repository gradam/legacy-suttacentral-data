Ekakanipāta

Ekādasamavagga

9 Saṃgha­rak­khi­tat­thera­gāthā

“Na nūnāyaṃ parama­hi­tā­nukam­pino,
Rahogato anuvigaṇeti sāsanaṃ;
Tathāhayaṃ viharati pākatindriyo,
Migī yathā taruṇajātikā vane”ti.

… Saṃgharakkhito thero ….
