Theragāthā

Pañcakanipāta

Paṭhamavagga

1 Rāja­dattat­thera­gāthā

“Bhikkhu sivathikaṃ gantvā,
Addasa itthimujjhitaṃ;
Apaviddhaṃ susānasmiṃ,
Khajjantiṃ kimihī phuṭaṃ.

Yañhi eke jigucchanti,
mataṃ disvāna pāpakaṃ;
Kāmarāgo pāturahu,
andhova savatī ahuṃ.

Oraṃ odanapākamhā,
tamhā ṭhānā apakkamiṃ;
Satimā sampajānohaṃ,
ekamantaṃ upāvisiṃ.

Tato me manasīkāro,
yoniso udapajjatha;
Ādīnavo pāturahu,
nibbidā samatiṭṭhatha.

Tato cittaṃ vimucci me,
passa dhamma­su­dhamma­taṃ;
Tisso vijjā anuppattā,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanan”ti.

… Rājadatto thero ….
