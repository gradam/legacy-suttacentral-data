Dasamavagga

1.99 Utti­yat­thera­gāthā

“Saddaṃ sutvā sati muṭṭhā,
Piyaṃ nimittaṃ manasikaroto;
Sārattacitto vedeti,
Tañca ajjhosa tiṭṭhati;
Tassa vaḍḍhanti āsavā,
Saṃsāraṃ upagāmino”ti.

… Uttiyo thero ….
