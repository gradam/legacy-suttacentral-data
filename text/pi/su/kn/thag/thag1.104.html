Theragāthā

Ekakanipāta

Ekādasamavagga

4 Khi­takat­thera­gāthā

“Lahuko vata me kāyo,
Phuṭṭho ca pītisukhena vipulena;
Tūlamiva eritaṃ mālutena,
Pilavatīva me kāyo”ti.

… Khitako thero ….
