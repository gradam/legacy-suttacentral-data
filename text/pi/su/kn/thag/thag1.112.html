Theragāthā

Ekakanipāta

Dvādasamavagga

Vacchagottattheragāthā

“Tevijjohaṃ mahājhāyī,
cetosamathakovido;
Sadattho me anuppatto,
kataṃ buddhassa sāsanan”ti.

…  Vacchagotto thero… .
