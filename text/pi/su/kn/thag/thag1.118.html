Theragāthā

Ekakanipāta

Dvādasamavagga

1.118 Kimi­lat­thera­gāthā

“Abhisattova nipatati,
Vayo rūpaṃ aññamiva tatheva santaṃ;
Tasseva sato avippavasato,
Aññasseva sarāmi attānan”ti.

… Kimilo thero ….
