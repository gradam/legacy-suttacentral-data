Theragāthā

Ekakanipāta

Sattamavagga

5 Ukkhepa­ka­ta­vacchat­thera­gāthā

“Ukkhepa­ka­ta­vacchassa,
Saṅkalitaṃ bahūhi vassehi;
Taṃ bhāsati gahaṭṭhānaṃ,
Sunisinno uḷārapāmojjo”ti.

… Ukkhepa­ka­tavac­cho thero ….
