Theragāthā

Dukanipāta

Tatiyavagga

2.22. Bhadda­jit­thera­gāthā

“Panādo nāma so rājā,
yassa yūpo suvaṇṇayo;
Tiriyaṃ soḷasubbedho,
ubbhamāhu sahassadhā.

Sahassakaṇḍo satageṇḍu,
dhajālu haritāmayo;
Anaccuṃ tattha gandhabbā,
chasahassāni sattadhā”ti.

… Bhaddajitthero ….
