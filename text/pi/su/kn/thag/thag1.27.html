Theragāthā

Ekakanipāta

Tatiyavagga

Lomasakaṅgiyattheragāthā

“Dabbaṃ kusaṃ poṭakilaṃ,
usīraṃ muñjapabbajaṃ;
Urasā panudissāmi,
vivekamanubrūhayan”ti.

…  Lomasakaṅgiyo thero… .
