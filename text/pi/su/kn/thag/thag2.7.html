Theragāthā

Dukanipāta

Paṭhamavagga

Rādhattheragāthā

“Yathā agāraṃ ducchannaṃ,
vuṭṭhī samativijjhati;
Evaṃ abhāvitaṃ cittaṃ,
rāgo samativijjhati.

Yathā agāraṃ succhannaṃ,
Vuṭṭhī na samativijjhati;
Evaṃ subhāvitaṃ cittaṃ,
Rāgo na samativijjhatī”ti.

…  Rādho thero… .
