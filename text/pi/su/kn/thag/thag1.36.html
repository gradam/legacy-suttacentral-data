Ekakanipāta

Catutthavagga

1.36 Kumā­puttat­thera­gāthā

“Sādhu sutaṃ sādhu caritakaṃ,
Sādhu sadā aniketavihāro;
Atthapucchanaṃ padak­khi­ṇa­kammaṃ,
Etaṃ sāmañ­ña­makiñ­ca­nassā”ti.

… Kumāputto thero ….
