Theragāthā

Ekakanipāta

Dasamavagga

5 Cakkhupālattheragāthā

“Andhohaṃ hatanettosmi,
kantāraddhānapakkhando;
Sayamānopi gacchissaṃ,
na sahāyena pāpenā”ti.

… Cakkhupālo thero ….
