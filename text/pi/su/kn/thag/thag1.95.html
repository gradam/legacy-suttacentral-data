Ekakanipāta

Dasamavagga

1.95. Cak­khu­pālat­thera­gāthā

“Andhohaṃ hatanettosmi,
kantā­rad­dhāna­pak­khando;
Sayamānopi gacchissaṃ,
na sahāyena pāpenā”ti.

… Cakkhupālo thero ….
