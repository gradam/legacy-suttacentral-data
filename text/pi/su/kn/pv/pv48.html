Petavatthu

Mahāvagga

13 Akkharukkhapetavatthu

“Yaṃ dadāti na taṃ hoti,
Detheva dānaṃ datvā ubhayaṃ tarati;
Ubhayaṃ tena dānena gacchati,
Jāgaratha māpamajjathā”ti.

Akkharukkhapetavatthu terasamaṃ.
