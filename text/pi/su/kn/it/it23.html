Itivuttaka 23

Ubhayat­thasutta

Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā ­vuttama­rahatāti me sutaṃ:

“Ekadhammo, bhikkhave, bhāvito bahulīkato ubho atthe samadhigayha tiṭṭhati–  diṭṭha­dham­mikañ­ceva atthaṃ samparāyikañca. Katamo ekadhammo? Appamādo kusalesu dhammesu. Ayaṃ kho, bhikkhave, ekadhammo bhāvito bahulīkato ubho atthe samadhigayha tiṭṭhati–  diṭṭha­dham­mikañ­ceva atthaṃ samparāyikañcā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati: 

^ “Appamādaṃ pasaṃsanti,
puññakiriyāsu paṇḍitā;
Appamatto ubho atthe,
adhigaṇhāti paṇḍito.

Diṭṭhe dhamme ca yo attho,
yo cattho samparāyiko;
Atthābhisamayā dhīro,
paṇḍitoti pavuccatī”ti.

Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

Tatiyaṃ.
