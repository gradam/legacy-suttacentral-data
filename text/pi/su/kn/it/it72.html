Itivuttaka 72

Nis­saraṇi­ya­sutta

Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā ­vuttama­rahatāti me sutaṃ: 

“Tisso imā, bhikkhave, nissaraṇiyā dhātuyo. Katamā tisso? Kāmānametaṃ nissaraṇaṃ yadidaṃ nekkhammaṃ, rūpānametaṃ nissaraṇaṃ yadidaṃ āruppaṃ, yaṃ kho pana kiñci bhūtaṃ saṅkhataṃ paṭic­ca­samup­pannaṃ nirodho tassa nissaraṇaṃ—imā kho, bhikkhave, tisso nissaraṇiyā dhātuyo”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati:

“Kāmanissaraṇaṃ ñatvā,
rūpānañca atikkamaṃ;
Sabba­saṅ­khā­ra­sama­thaṃ,
phusaṃ ātāpi sabbadā.

Sa ve sammaddaso bhikkhu,
yato tattha vimuccati;
Abhiññāvosito santo,
sa ve yogātigo munī”ti.

Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

Tatiyaṃ.
