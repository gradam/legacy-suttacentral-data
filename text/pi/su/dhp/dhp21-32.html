Dhammapada 21–​32.

Appamādavagga

21. Sāmāvatīvatthu

Appamādo amatapadaṃ,
pamādo maccuno padaṃ;
Appamattā na mīyanti,
ye pamattā yathā matā.

Evaṃ visesato ñatvā,
appamādamhi paṇḍitā;
Appamāde pamodanti,
ariyānaṃ gocare ratā.

Te jhāyino sātatikā,
niccaṃ daḷhaparakkamā;
Phusanti dhīrā nibbānaṃ,
yogakkhemaṃ anuttaraṃ.

22. Kumbaghosakaseṭṭhivatthu

Uṭṭhānavato satīmato,
Sucikammassa nisammakārino;
Saññatassa dhammajīvino,
Appamattassa yasobhivaḍḍhati.

23. Cūḷapanthakavatthu

Uṭṭhānenappamādena,
saṃyamena damena ca;
Dīpaṃ kayirātha medhāvī,
yaṃ ogho nābhikīrati.

24. Bālanakkhatta­saṅghuṭṭha­vatthu

Pamādamanuyuñjanti,
bālā dummedhino janā;
Appamādañca medhāvī,
dhanaṃ seṭṭhaṃva rakkhati.

Mā pamādamanuyuñjetha,
mā kāmaratisanthavaṃ;
Appamatto hi jhāyanto,
pappoti vipulaṃ sukhaṃ.

25. Mahākassapattheravatthu

Pamādaṃ appamādena,
yadā nudati paṇḍito;
Paññāpāsādamāruyha,
asoko sokiniṃ pajaṃ;
Pabbataṭṭhova bhūmaṭṭhe,
dhīro bāle avekkhati.

26. Pamattā­pamattadve­sahā­yaka­bhikkhu­vatthu

Appamatto pamattesu,
suttesu bahujāgaro;
Abalassaṃva sīghasso,
hitvā yāti sumedhaso.

27. Maghavatthu

Appamādena maghavā,
devānaṃ seṭṭhataṃ gato;
Appamādaṃ pasaṃsanti,
pamādo garahito sadā.

28. Aññatarabhikkhuvatthu

Appamādarato bhikkhu,
pamāde bhayadassi vā;
Saṃyojanaṃ aṇuṃ thūlaṃ,
ḍahaṃ aggīva gacchati.

29. Nigama­vāsi­tissatthe­ravatthu

Appamādarato bhikkhu,
pamāde bhayadassi vā;
Abhabbo parihānāya,
nibbānasseva santike.

Appamādavaggo dutiyo.
