Saṃyutta Nikāya 17

4. Catutthavagga

32. Kusala­mūla­sutta

Sāvatthiyaṃ viharati. “Dāruṇo, bhikkhave, lābhasakkā­ra­siloko. Lābhasakkā­ra­silokena abhibhūtassa ­pariyā­diṇṇa­cittassa, bhikkhave, devadattassa kusalamūlaṃ ­samuc­cheda­magamā. Evaṃ dāruṇo kho, bhikkhave, lābhasakkā­ra­siloko … pe … sikkhitabban”ti.

Dutiyaṃ.
