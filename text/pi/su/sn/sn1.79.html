Saṃyutta Nikāya 1

8. Chetvāvagga

79. Pātheyyasutta

“Kiṃsu bandhati pātheyyaṃ,
kiṃsu bhogānamāsayo;
Kiṃsu naraṃ parikassati,
kiṃsu lokasmi dujjahaṃ;
Kismiṃ baddhā puthū sattā,
pāsena sakuṇī yathā”ti.

“Saddhā bandhati pātheyyaṃ,
sirī bhogānamāsayo;
Icchā naraṃ parikassati,
icchā lokasmi dujjahā;
Icchābaddhā puthū sattā,
pāsena sakuṇī yathā”ti.

Pali text from the Mahāsaṅgīti Tipiṭaka Buddhavasse 2500: World Tipiṭaka Edition in Roman Script. Edited and published by The M.L. Maniratana Bunnag Dhamma Society Fund, 2005.
