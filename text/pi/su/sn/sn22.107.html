Saṃyutta Nikāya 22

11. Antavagga

107. Samaṇasutta

Sāvatthi­nidānaṃ. “Pañcime, bhikkhave, upādā­nak­khan­dhā. Katame pañca? Seyyathidaṃ—rūpupā­dā­nak­khan­dho … pe … viñ­ñāṇupā­dā­nak­khan­dho. Ye hi keci, bhikkhave, samaṇā vā brāhmaṇā vā imesaṃ pañcannaṃ upādā­nak­khan­dhā­naṃ assādañca ādīnavañca nissaraṇañca yathābhūtaṃ nappajānanti … pe … pajānanti, sayaṃ abhiññā sacchikatvā upasampajja viharantī”ti.

Pañcamaṃ.
