Saṃyutta Nikāya 46

5. Cakka­vatti­vagga

43. Mārasutta

“Māra­senap­pa­madda­naṃ vo, bhikkhave, maggaṃ desessāmi; taṃ suṇātha. Katamo ca, bhikkhave, māra­senap­pa­maddano maggo? Yadidaṃ—satta bojjhaṅgā. Katame satta? Sati­sam­boj­jhaṅgo … pe … upekkhā­sam­boj­jhaṅgo—ayaṃ kho, bhikkhave, māra­senap­pa­maddano maggo”ti.

Tatiyaṃ.
