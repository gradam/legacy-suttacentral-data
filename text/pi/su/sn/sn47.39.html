Saṃyutta Nikāya 47

4. Ananus­suta­vagga

39. Bhāvanāsutta

“Catunnaṃ, bhikkhave, sati­paṭṭhā­nā­naṃ bhāvanaṃ desessāmi. Taṃ suṇātha. Katamā, bhikkhave, catunnaṃ sati­paṭṭhā­nā­naṃ bhāvanā? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu kāye kāyānupassī viharati ātāpī sampajāno satimā, vineyya loke abhij­jhā­do­manas­saṃ; vedanāsu … pe … citte … pe … dhammesu dhammānupassī viharati ātāpī sampajāno satimā, vineyya loke abhij­jhā­do­manas­saṃ. Ayaṃ kho, bhikkhave, catunnaṃ sati­paṭṭhā­nā­naṃ bhāvanā”ti.

Navamaṃ.
