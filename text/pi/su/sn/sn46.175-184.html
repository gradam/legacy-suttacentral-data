Saṃyutta Nikāya 46

18. Punaoghavagga

175–​184. Puna­oghā­di­sutta

… pe …

Boj­jhaṅ­ga­saṃ­yuttassa punaoghavaggo aṭṭhārasamo.

Ogho yogo upādānaṃ,
Ganthā anusayena ca;
Kāmaguṇā nīvaraṇā,
Khandhā oruddham­bhāgi­yā­nīti.

(Rāga­vinaya­pari­yosāna-dosa­vinaya­pari­yosāna-moha­vina­ya­pari­yosāna­vaggo vitthāretabbo.) (Yadapi maggasaṃyuttaṃ vitthāretabbaṃ, tadapi boj­jhaṅ­ga­saṃ­yuttaṃ vitthāretabbaṃ.)

Boj­jhaṅ­ga­saṃ­yuttaṃ dutiyaṃ.
