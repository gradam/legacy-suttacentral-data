Saṃyutta Nikāya 19

2. Dutiyavagga

18. Pāpa­bhik­khu­nī­sutta

“Addasaṃ bhikkhuniṃ vehāsaṃ gacchantiṃ. Tassā saṅghāṭipi ādittā … pe … pāpabhikkhunī ahosi … pe ….

Aṭṭhamaṃ.
