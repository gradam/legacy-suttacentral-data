Saṃyutta Nikāya 26

1. Uppādavagga

7. Sañcetanāsutta

Sāvatthi­nidānaṃ. “Yo kho, bhikkhave, rūpa­sañ­ceta­nāya uppādo ṭhiti … pe … jarāmaraṇassa pātubhāvo … pe … yo dhamma­sañ­ceta­nāya uppādo ṭhiti abhinibbatti pātubhāvo, dukkhasseso uppādo, rogānaṃ ṭhiti, jarāmaraṇassa pātubhāvo. Yo ca kho, bhikkhave, rūpa­sañ­ceta­nāya nirodho … pe … jarāmaraṇassa atthaṅgamo … pe … yo dhamma­sañ­ceta­nāya nirodho vūpasamo atthaṅgamo, dukkhasseso nirodho, rogānaṃ vūpasamo, jarāmaraṇassa atthaṅgamo”ti.

Sattamaṃ.
