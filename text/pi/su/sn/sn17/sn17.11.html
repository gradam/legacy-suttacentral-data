Saṃyutta Nikāya 17

2. Dutiyavagga

11. Suvaṇ­ṇa­pāti­sutta

Sāvatthiyaṃ viharati. “Dāruṇo, bhikkhave, lābhasakkā­ra­siloko … pe … adhigamāya. Idhāhaṃ, bhikkhave, ekaccaṃ puggalaṃ evaṃ cetasā ceto paricca pajānāmi: ‘na cāyamāyasmā suvaṇ­ṇa­pāti­yāpi rūpiya­cuṇṇa­pari­pūrāya hetu sampajānamusā bhāseyyā’ti. Tamenaṃ passāmi aparena samayena lābhasakkā­ra­silokena abhibhūtaṃ ­pariyā­diṇṇa­cittaṃ sampajānamusā bhāsantaṃ. Evaṃ dāruṇo kho, bhikkhave, lābhasakkā­ra­siloko … pe … evañhi vo, bhikkhave, sikkhitabban”ti.

Paṭhamaṃ.
