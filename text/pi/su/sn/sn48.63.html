Saṃyutta Nikāya 48

7. Bodhi­pak­khi­ya­vagga

63. Pariññāsutta

“Pañcimāni, bhikkhave, indriyāni bhāvitāni bahulīkatāni addhā­na­pariñ­ñāya saṃvattanti. Katamāni pañca? Saddhindriyaṃ … pe … paññindriyaṃ—imāni kho, bhikkhave, pañcindriyāni bhāvitāni bahulīkatāni addhā­na­pariñ­ñāya saṃvattantī”ti.

Tatiyaṃ.
