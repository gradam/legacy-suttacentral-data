Saṃyutta Nikāya 22

11. Antavagga

111. Chandap­pahāna­sutta

Sāvatthi­nidānaṃ. “Rūpe, bhikkhave, yo chando yo rāgo yā nandī yā taṇhā, taṃ pajahatha. Evaṃ taṃ rūpaṃ pahīnaṃ bhavissati ucchinnamūlaṃ tālā­vatthu­ka­taṃ anabhāvaṃkataṃ āyatiṃ anup­pā­da­dham­maṃ. Vedanāya … pe … saññāya … saṅkhāresu … viññāṇe yo chando yo rāgo yā nandī yā taṇhā, taṃ pajahatha. Evaṃ taṃ viññāṇaṃ pahīnaṃ bhavissati ucchinnamūlaṃ tālā­vatthu­ka­taṃ anabhāvaṃkataṃ āyatiṃ anup­pā­da­dham­man”ti.

Navamaṃ.
