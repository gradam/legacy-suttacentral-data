Saṃyutta Nikāya 45

3. Micchattavagga

23. Paṭha­ma­paṭi­padā­sutta

Sāvatthi­nidānaṃ. “Micchā­paṭi­padañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi, sammā­paṭi­padañca. Taṃ suṇātha. Katamā ca, bhikkhave, micchāpaṭipadā? Seyyathidaṃ—micchādiṭṭhi … pe … micchāsamādhi. Ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, micchāpaṭipadā. Katamā ca, bhikkhave, sammāpaṭipadā? Seyyathidaṃ—sammādiṭṭhi … pe … sammāsamādhi. Ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, sammāpaṭipadā”ti.

Tatiyaṃ.
