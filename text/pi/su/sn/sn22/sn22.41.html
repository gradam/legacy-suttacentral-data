Saṃyutta Nikāya 22

4. Natumhākavagga

41. Tatiya­anu­dhamma­sutta

Sāvatthi­nidānaṃ. “Dhammā­nu­dhammap­paṭi­pannassa, bhikkhave, bhikkhuno ayamanudhammo hoti yaṃ rūpe dukkhānupassī vihareyya … pe … parimuccati dukkhasmāti vadāmī”ti.

Navamaṃ.
