Saṃyutta Nikāya 51

3. Ayoguḷavagga

23. Bhikkhusutta

“Cattārome, bhikkhave, iddhipādā. Katame cattāro? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu chanda­samā­dhippa­dhāna­saṅkhā­rasa­mannā­gataṃ iddhipādaṃ bhāveti, vīriyasamādhi … pe … cittasamādhi … vīmaṃsā­samā­dhippa­dhāna­saṅkhā­rasa­mannā­gataṃ iddhipādaṃ bhāveti–  ime kho, bhikkhave, cattāro iddhipādā. Imesaṃ kho, bhikkhave, catunnaṃ iddhipādānaṃ bhāvitattā bahulīkatattā bhikkhu āsavānaṃ khayā anāsavaṃ cetovimuttiṃ paññāvimuttiṃ diṭṭheva dhamme sayaṃ abhiññā sacchikatvā upasampajja viharatī”ti.

Tatiyaṃ.
