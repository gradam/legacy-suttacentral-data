Saṃyutta Nikāya 52

2. Dutiyavagga

12. Iddhi­vidha­sutta

“Imesañca panāhaṃ, āvuso, catunnaṃ sati­paṭṭhā­nā­naṃ bhāvitattā bahulīkatattā anekavihitaṃ iddhividhaṃ paccanubhomi—ekopi hutvā bahudhā homi … pe … yāva brahmalokāpi kāyena vasaṃ vattemī”ti.

Dutiyaṃ.
