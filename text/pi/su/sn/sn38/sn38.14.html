Saṃyutta Nikāya 38

1. Jambu­khā­daka­vagga

14. Duk­kha­pañhā­sutta

“‘Dukkhaṃ, dukkhan’ti, āvuso sāriputta, vuccati. Katamaṃ nu kho, āvuso, dukkhan”ti? “Tisso imā, āvuso, dukkhatā. Dukkhadukkhatā, saṅ­khā­ra­duk­khatā, vipari­ṇāma­duk­khatā—imā kho, āvuso, tisso dukkhatā”ti. “Atthi panāvuso maggo atthi paṭipadā, etāsaṃ dukkhatānaṃ pariññāyā”ti? “Atthi kho, āvuso, maggo atthi paṭipadā, etāsaṃ dukkhatānaṃ pariññāyā”ti. “Katamo panāvuso, maggo katamā paṭipadā, etāsaṃ dukkhatānaṃ pariññāyā”ti? “Ayameva kho, āvuso, ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo, etāsaṃ dukkhatānaṃ pariññāya, seyyathidaṃ—sammādiṭṭhi … pe … sammāsamādhi. Ayaṃ kho, āvuso, maggo ayaṃ paṭipadā, etāsaṃ dukkhatānaṃ pariññāyā”ti. “Bhaddako, āvuso, maggo bhaddikā paṭipadā, etāsaṃ dukkhatānaṃ pariññāya. Alañca panāvuso sāriputta, appamādāyā”ti.

Cuddasamaṃ.
