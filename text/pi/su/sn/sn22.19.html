Saṃyutta Nikāya 22

2. Aniccavagga

19. Sahetu­duk­kha­sutta

Sāvatthi­nidānaṃ. “Rūpaṃ, bhikkhave, dukkhaṃ. Yopi hetu yopi paccayo rūpassa uppādāya, sopi dukkho. Duk­kha­sam­bhū­taṃ, bhikkhave, rūpaṃ kuto sukhaṃ bhavissati. Vedanā dukkhā … saññā dukkhā … saṅkhārā dukkhā … viññāṇaṃ dukkhaṃ. Yopi hetu yopi paccayo viññāṇassa uppādāya, sopi dukkho. Duk­kha­sam­bhū­taṃ, bhikkhave, viññāṇaṃ kuto sukhaṃ bhavissati. Evaṃ passaṃ … pe … nāparaṃ itthattāyā’ti pajānātī”ti.

Aṭṭhamaṃ.
