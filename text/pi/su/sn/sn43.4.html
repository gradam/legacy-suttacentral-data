Saṃyutta Nikāya 43

1. Paṭhamavagga

4. Suññatasamādhisutta

“Katamo ca, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo? Suññato samādhi, animitto samādhi, appaṇihito samādhi–  ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo … pe ….

Catutthaṃ.
