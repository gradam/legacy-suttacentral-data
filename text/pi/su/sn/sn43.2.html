Saṃyutta Nikāya 43

1. Paṭhamavagga

2. Sama­tha­vi­passa­nā­sutta

“Asaṅkhatañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi asaṅ­kha­ta­gāmiñca maggaṃ. Taṃ suṇātha. Katamañca, bhikkhave, asaṅkhataṃ? Yo, bhikkhave, rāgakkhayo dosakkhayo mohakkhayo–  idaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, asaṅkhataṃ. Katamo ca, bhikkhave, asaṅ­kha­ta­gāmi­maggo? Samatho ca vipassanā ca. Ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, asaṅ­kha­ta­gāmi­maggo … pe ….

Dutiyaṃ.
