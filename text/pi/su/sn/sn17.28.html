Saṃyutta Nikāya 17

3. Tatiyavagga

28. Chavisutta

Sāvatthiyaṃ viharati. “Dāruṇo, bhikkhave, lābhasakkā­ra­siloko. Lābhasakkā­ra­siloko, bhikkhave, chaviṃ chindati, chaviṃ chetvā cammaṃ chindati, cammaṃ chetvā maṃsaṃ chindati, maṃsaṃ chetvā nhāruṃ chindati, nhāruṃ chetvā aṭṭhiṃ chindati, aṭṭhiṃ chetvā aṭṭhimiñjaṃ āhacca tiṭṭhati. Evaṃ dāruṇo kho, bhikkhave, lābhasakkā­ra­siloko … pe … evañhi vo bhikkhave, sikkhitabban”ti.

Aṭṭhamaṃ.
