Saṃyutta Nikāya 27

1. Kilesavagga

10. Khandhasutta

Sāvatthi­nidānaṃ. “Yo, bhikkhave, rūpasmiṃ chandarāgo, cittasseso upakkileso … pe … yo viññāṇasmiṃ chandarāgo, cittasseso upakkileso. Yato kho, bhikkhave, bhikkhuno imesu pañcasu ṭhānesu cetaso upakkileso pahīno hoti, nekkham­ma­ninnañ­cassa cittaṃ hoti. Nekkham­ma­pari­bhāvi­taṃ cittaṃ kammaniyaṃ khāyati, abhiññā ­sacchi­karaṇī­yesu dhammesū”ti.

Dasamaṃ.

Kilesavaggo paṭhamo.

Cakkhu rūpañca viññāṇaṃ,
phasso ca vedanāya ca;
Saññā ca cetanā taṇhā,
dhātu khandhena te dasāti.

Kilesa­saṃ­yuttaṃ samattaṃ.
