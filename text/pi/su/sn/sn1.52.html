Saṃyutta Nikāya 1

6. Jarāvagga

52. Ajarasāsutta

“Kiṃsu ajarasā sādhu,
kiṃsu sādhu adhiṭṭhitaṃ;
Kiṃsu narānaṃ ratanaṃ,
kiṃsu corehyahāriyan”ti.

“Sīlaṃ ajarasā sādhu,
saddhā sādhu adhiṭṭhitā;
Paññā narānaṃ ratanaṃ,
puññaṃ corehyahāriyan”ti.
