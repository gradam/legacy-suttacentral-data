Saṃyutta Nikāya 56

10. Catut­tha­āmaka­dhañña­peyyā­la­vagga

94. Tulākūṭasutta

… “Evameva kho, bhikkhave, appakā te sattā ye tulā­kūṭa­kaṃsa­kūṭa­māna­kūṭā paṭiviratā; atha kho eteva bahutarā sattā ye tulā­kūṭa­kaṃsa­kūṭa­māna­kūṭā appaṭiviratā … pe ….

Catutthaṃ.
