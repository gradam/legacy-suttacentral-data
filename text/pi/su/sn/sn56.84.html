Saṃyutta Nikāya 56

9. Tatiya­āmaka­dhañña­peyyā­la­vagga

84. Āmaka­dhañña­sutta

… “Evameva kho, bhikkhave, appakā te sattā ye āmaka­dhañña­paṭig­gahaṇā paṭiviratā; atha kho eteva bahutarā sattā ye āmaka­dhañña­paṭig­gahaṇā appaṭiviratā … pe ….

Catutthaṃ.
