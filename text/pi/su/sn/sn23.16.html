Saṃyutta Nikāya 23

2. Dutiya­māravagga

16. Duk­kha­dhamma­sutta

Sāvatthi­nidānaṃ. Ekamantaṃ nisinno kho āyasmā rādho bhagavantaṃ etadavoca: “‘dukkhadhammo, dukkhadhammo’ti, bhante, vuccati. Katamo nu kho, bhante, dukkhadhammo”ti? “Rūpaṃ kho, rādha, dukkhadhammo, vedanā dukkhadhammo, saññā dukkhadhammo, saṅkhārā dukkhadhammo, viññāṇaṃ dukkhadhammo. Evaṃ passaṃ … pe … nāparaṃ itthattāyāti pajānātī”ti.

Chaṭṭhaṃ.
