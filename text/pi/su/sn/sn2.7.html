Saṃyutta Nikāya 2

1. Paṭhamavagga

7. Pañcā­la­caṇḍa­sutta

Sāvatthi­nidānaṃ. Ekamantaṃ ṭhito kho pañcālacaṇḍo devaputto bhagavato santike imaṃ gāthaṃ abhāsi: 

“Sambādhe vata okāsaṃ,
Avindi bhūrimedhaso;
Yo jhānamabujjhi buddho,
Paṭi­līna­nisa­bho munī”ti.

“Sambādhe vāpi vindanti, (pañcālacaṇḍāti bhagavā)
Dhammaṃ nibbānapattiyā;
Ye satiṃ paccalatthaṃsu,
Sammā te susamāhitā”ti.
