Saṃyutta Nikāya 52

2. Dutiyavagga

12. Iddhividhasutta

“Imesañca panāhaṃ, āvuso, catunnaṃ satipaṭṭhānānaṃ bhāvitattā bahulīkatattā anekavihitaṃ iddhividhaṃ paccanubhomi–  ekopi hutvā bahudhā homi … pe … yāva brahmalokāpi kāyena vasaṃ vattemī”ti.

Dutiyaṃ.
