Saṃyutta Nikāya 52

2. Dutiyavagga

24. Āsa­vak­kha­ya­sutta

“Imesañca panāhaṃ, āvuso, catunnaṃ sati­paṭṭhā­nā­naṃ bhāvitattā bahulīkatattā āsavānaṃ khayā anāsavaṃ cetovimuttiṃ paññāvimuttiṃ diṭṭheva dhamme sayaṃ abhiññā sacchikatvā upasampajja viharāmī”ti.

Cuddasamaṃ.

Dutiyo vaggo.

Mahābhiññaṃ iddhi dibbaṃ,
Cetopariyaṃ ṭhānaṃ kammaṃ;
Sabbat­tha­dhātu­dhi­mutti,
Indriyaṃ jhānaṃ tisso vijjāti.

Anu­ruddha­saṃ­yuttaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
