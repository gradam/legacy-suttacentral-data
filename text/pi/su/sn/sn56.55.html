Saṃyutta Nikāya 56

6. Abhisa­maya­vagga

55. Paṭha­ma­mahā­patha­vī­sutta

^ “Seyyathāpi, bhikkhave, puriso mahāpathaviyā satta kolaṭ­ṭhi­mat­tiyo guḷikā upanikkhipeyya. Taṃ kiṃ maññatha, bhikkhave, katamaṃ nu kho bahutaraṃ—yā vā satta kolaṭ­ṭhi­mat­tiyo guḷikā upanikkhittā, ayaṃ vā mahāpathavī”ti? “Etadeva, bhante, bahutaraṃ, yadidaṃ—mahāpathavī; appamattikā satta kolaṭ­ṭhi­mat­tiyo guḷikā upanikkhittā. Saṅkhampi na upenti, upanidhampi na upenti, kalabhāgampi na upenti mahāpathaviṃ upanidhāya satta kolaṭ­ṭhi­mat­tiyo guḷikā upanikkhittā”ti. “Evameva kho, bhikkhave, ariyasāvakassa … pe … yogo karaṇīyo”ti.

Pañcamaṃ.
