Saṃyutta Nikāya 27

1. Kilesavagga

5. Samphas­sa­jasutta

Sāvatthi­nidānaṃ. “Yo, bhikkhave, cak­khu­samphas­sa­jāya vedanāya chandarāgo, cittasseso upakkileso. Yo sota­samphas­sa­jāya vedanāya … yo ghāna­samphas­sa­jāya vedanāya … yo jivhā­samphas­sa­jāya vedanāya … yo kāya­samphas­sa­jāya vedanāya … yo mano­samphas­sa­jāya vedanāya chandarāgo, cittasseso upakkileso. Yato kho, bhikkhave, bhikkhuno … pe … abhiññā ­sacchi­karaṇī­yesu dhammesū”ti.

Pañcamaṃ.
