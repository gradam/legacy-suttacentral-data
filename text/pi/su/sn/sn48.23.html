Saṃyutta Nikāya 48

3. Cha­ḷindriya­vagga

23. Aññindriya­sutta

^ “Tīṇimāni, bhikkhave, indriyāni. Katamāni tīṇi? Anaññātañ­ñassā­mī­tindriyaṃ, aññindriyaṃ, aññātā­vin­dri­yaṃ–  imāni kho, bhikkhave, tīṇi indriyānī”ti.

Tatiyaṃ.
