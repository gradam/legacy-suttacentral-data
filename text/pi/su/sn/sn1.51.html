Saṃyutta Nikāya 1

6. Jarāvagga

51. Jarāsutta

“Kiṃsu yāva jarā sādhu,
kiṃsu sādhu patiṭṭhitaṃ;
Kiṃsu narānaṃ ratanaṃ,
kiṃsu corehi dūharan”ti.

“Sīlaṃ yāva jarā sādhu,
saddhā sādhu patiṭṭhitā;
Paññā narānaṃ ratanaṃ,
puññaṃ corehi dūharan”ti.

Pali text from the Mahāsaṅgīti Tipiṭaka Buddhavasse 2500: World Tipiṭaka Edition in Roman Script. Edited and published by The M.L. Maniratana Bunnag Dhamma Society Fund, 2005.
