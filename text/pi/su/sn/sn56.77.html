Saṃyutta Nikāya 56

8. Dutiya­āmaka­dhañña­peyyā­la­vagga

77. Samphap­palā­pa­sutta

… “Evameva kho, bhikkhave, appakā te sattā ye samphappalāpā paṭiviratā; atha kho eteva bahutarā sattā ye samphappalāpā appaṭiviratā … pe ….

Sattamaṃ.
