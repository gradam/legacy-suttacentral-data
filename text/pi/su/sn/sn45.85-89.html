Saṃyutta Nikāya 45

8. Dutiya­ekadham­ma­peyyā­la­vagga

85–​89. Dutiya­sīla­sam­padā­di­sutta­pañcaka

“Nāhaṃ, bhikkhave, aññaṃ ekadhammampi samanupassāmi, yena anuppanno vā ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo uppajjati, uppanno vā ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo bhāva­nā­pāri­pūriṃ gacchati, yathayidaṃ, bhikkhave, sīlasampadā … pe … yathayidaṃ, bhikkhave, chandasampadā … pe … yathayidaṃ, bhikkhave, attasampadā … pe … yathayidaṃ, bhikkhave, diṭṭhisampadā … pe … yathayidaṃ, bhikkhave, appa­māda­sam­padā … pe ….

Chaṭṭhaṃ.
