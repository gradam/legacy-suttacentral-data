Saṃyutta Nikāya 1

2. Nandanavagga

14. Khattiyasutta

“Khattiyo dvipadaṃ seṭṭho,
balībaddo catuppadaṃ;
Komārī seṭṭhā bhariyānaṃ,
yo ca puttāna pubbajo”ti.

“Sambuddho dvipadaṃ seṭṭho,
ājānīyo catuppadaṃ;
Sussūsā seṭṭhā bhariyānaṃ,
yo ca puttānamassavo”ti.
