Saṃyutta Nikāya 17

1. Paṭhamavagga

6. Asanisutta

Sāvatthiyaṃ viharati. “Dāruṇo, bhikkhave, lābhasakkā­ra­siloko … pe … adhigamāya*. Kaṃ, bhikkhave, asanivicakkaṃ āgacchatu, sekhaṃ appattamānasaṃ lābhasakkā­ra­siloko anupāpuṇātu.

Asani­vi­cakkanti kho, bhikkhave, lābhasakkā­ra­silokas­setaṃ adhivacanaṃ. Evaṃ dāruṇo kho, bhikkhave, lābhasakkā­ra­siloko … pe … evañhi vo, bhikkhave, sikkhitabban”ti.

Chaṭṭhaṃ.
