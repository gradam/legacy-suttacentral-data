Saṃyutta Nikāya 48

7. Bodhi­pak­khi­ya­vagga

62. Anusayasutta

“Pañcimāni, bhikkhave, indriyāni bhāvitāni bahulīkatāni anusa­ya­samug­ghātāya saṃvattanti. Katamāni pañca? Saddhindriyaṃ … pe … paññindriyaṃ–  imāni kho, bhikkhave, pañcindriyāni bhāvitāni bahulīkatāni anusa­ya­samug­ghātāya saṃvattantī”ti.

Dutiyaṃ.
