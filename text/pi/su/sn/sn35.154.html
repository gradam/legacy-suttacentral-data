Saṃyutta Nikāya 35

15. ­Nava­purā­ṇa­vagga

154. Indriya­sam­panna­sutta

Atha kho aññataro bhikkhu yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami … pe … ekamantaṃ nisinno kho so bhikkhu bhagavantaṃ etadavoca: “‘indriya­sam­panno, indriya­sam­panno’ti, bhante, vuccati. Kittāvatā nu kho, bhante, indriya­sam­panno hotī”ti?

“Cakkhundriye ce, bhikkhu, udayab­bayā­nu­passī viharanto cakkhundriye nibbindati … pe … jivhindriye ce, bhikkhu, udayab­bayā­nu­passī viharanto jivhindriye nibbindati … pe … manindriye ce, bhikkhu, udayab­bayā­nu­passī viharanto manindriye nibbindati. Nibbindaṃ virajjati … pe … vimuttasmiṃ vimuttamiti ñāṇaṃ hoti. ‘Khīṇā jāti, vusitaṃ brahmacariyaṃ, kataṃ karaṇīyaṃ, nāparaṃ itthattāyā’ti pajānāti. Ettāvatā kho, bhikkhu, indriya­sam­panno hotī”ti.

Navamaṃ.
