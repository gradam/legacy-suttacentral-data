Saṃyutta Nikāya 45

4. Paṭipattivagga

31. Paṭha­ma­paṭi­patti­sutta

Sāvatthi­nidānaṃ. “Micchā­paṭi­pattiñca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi, sammā­paṭi­pattiñca. Taṃ suṇātha. Katamā ca, bhikkhave, micchā­paṭi­patti? Seyyathidaṃ—micchādiṭṭhi … pe … micchāsamādhi. Ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, micchā­paṭi­patti. Katamā ca, bhikkhave, sammāpaṭipatti? Seyyathidaṃ— sammādiṭṭhi … pe … sammāsamādhi. Ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, sammāpaṭipattī”ti.

Paṭhamaṃ.
