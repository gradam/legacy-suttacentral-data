Saṃyutta Nikāya 46

4. Nīvaraṇavagga

36. Yoniso­ma­nasikā­ra­sutta

“Yoniso ca kho, bhikkhave, manasikaroto anuppanno ceva sati­sam­boj­jhaṅgo uppajjati, uppanno ca sati­sam­boj­jhaṅgo bhāva­nā­pāri­pūriṃ gacchati … pe … anuppanno ceva upekkhā­sam­boj­jhaṅgo uppajjati, uppanno ca upekkhā­sam­boj­jhaṅgo bhāva­nā­pāri­pūriṃ gacchatī”ti.

Chaṭṭhaṃ.
