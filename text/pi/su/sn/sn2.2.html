Saṃyutta Nikāya 2

1. Paṭhamavagga

2. Dutiya­kas­sa­pasutta

Sāvatthi­nidānaṃ. Ekamantaṃ ṭhito kho kassapo devaputto bhagavato santike imaṃ gāthaṃ abhāsi: 

“Bhikkhu siyā jhāyī vimuttacitto,
Ākaṅkhe ce hadayas­sānupat­tiṃ;
Lokassa ñatvā udayabbayañca,
Sucetaso anissito tadānisaṃso”ti.
