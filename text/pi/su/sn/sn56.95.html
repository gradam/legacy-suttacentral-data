Saṃyutta Nikāya 56

10. Catutthaāmakadhaññapeyyālavagga

95. Ukkoṭanasutta

… “Evameva kho, bhikkhave, appakā te sattā ye ukkoṭanavañcananikatisāciyogā paṭiviratā; atha kho eteva bahutarā sattā ye ukkoṭanavañcananikatisāciyogā appaṭiviratā … pe ….

Pañcamaṃ.
