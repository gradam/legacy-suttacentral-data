Saṃyutta Nikāya 45

4. Paṭipattivagga

40. Dutiyab­rahma­cari­ya­sutta

Sāvatthi­nidānaṃ. “Brahma­cari­yañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi, brahma­cari­yat­thañca. Taṃ suṇātha. Katamañca, bhikkhave, brahmacariyaṃ? Ayameva ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo, seyyathidaṃ—sammādiṭṭhi … pe … sammāsamādhi. Idaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, brahmacariyaṃ. Katamo ca, bhikkhave, brahma­cari­yat­tho? Yo kho, bhikkhave, rāgakkhayo dosakkhayo mohakkhayo—ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, brahma­cari­yat­tho”ti.

Dasamaṃ.

Paṭipattivaggo catuttho.

Paṭipatti paṭipanno ca,
viraddhañca pāraṅgamā;
Sāmaññena ca dve vuttā,
brahmaññā apare duve;
Brahmacariyena dve vuttā,
vaggo tena pavuccatīti.
