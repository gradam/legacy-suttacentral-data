Saṃyutta Nikāya 23

2. Dutiya­māravagga

21. Samuda­ya­dhamma­sutta

Sāvatthi­nidānaṃ. Ekamantaṃ nisinno kho āyasmā rādho bhagavantaṃ etadavoca: “‘samudayadhammo, samudayadhammo’ti, bhante, vuccati. Katamo nu kho, bhante, samudayadhammo”ti? “Rūpaṃ kho, rādha, samudayadhammo, vedanā samudayadhammo, saññā samudayadhammo, saṅkhārā samudayadhammo, viññāṇaṃ samudayadhammo. Evaṃ passaṃ … pe … nāparaṃ itthattāyāti pajānātī”ti.

Ekādasamaṃ.
