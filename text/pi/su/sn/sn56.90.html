Saṃyutta Nikāya 56

9. Tatiya­āma­kadhañña­peyyā­lavagga

90. Hatthigavassasutta

…  “Evameva kho, bhikkhave, appakā te sattā ye hatthi­gavassa­vaḷa­vapa­ṭigga­haṇā paṭiviratā; atha kho eteva bahutarā sattā ye hatthi­gavassa­vaḷa­vapa­ṭigga­haṇā appaṭiviratā … pe ….

Dasamaṃ.

Tatiya­āma­kadhañña­peyyā­lavaggo navamo.

Naccaṃ sayanaṃ rajataṃ,
dhaññaṃ maṃsaṃ kumārikā;
Dāsī ajeḷakañceva,
kukkuṭasūkarahatthīti.
