Saṃyutta Nikāya 26

1. Uppādavagga

8. Taṇhāsutta

Sāvatthi­nidānaṃ. “Yo kho, bhikkhave, rūpataṇhāya uppādo ṭhiti … pe … jarāmaraṇassa pātubhāvo … pe … yo dhammataṇhāya uppādo ṭhiti abhinibbatti pātubhāvo, dukkhasseso uppādo, rogānaṃ ṭhiti, jarāmaraṇassa pātubhāvo. Yo ca kho, bhikkhave, rūpataṇhāya nirodho … pe … jarāmaraṇassa atthaṅgamo … pe … yo dhammataṇhāya nirodho vūpasamo atthaṅgamo, dukkhasseso nirodho, rogānaṃ vūpasamo, jarāmaraṇassa atthaṅgamo”ti.

Aṭṭhamaṃ.
