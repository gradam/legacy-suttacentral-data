Saṃyutta Nikāya 22

12. Dhamma­ka­thi­ka­vagga

118. Pari­pucchi­ta­sutta

Sāvatthi­nidānaṃ. “Taṃ kiṃ maññatha, bhikkhave, rūpaṃ ‘etaṃ mama, esohamasmi, eso me attā’ti samanupassathā”ti? “No hetaṃ, bhante”. “Sādhu, bhikkhave. Rūpaṃ, bhikkhave, ‘netaṃ mama, nesohamasmi, na meso attā’ti evametaṃ yathābhūtaṃ sammappaññāya daṭṭhabbaṃ. Vedanaṃ … saññaṃ … saṅkhāre … viññāṇaṃ ‘etaṃ mama, esohamasmi, eso me attā’ti samanupassathā”ti? “No hetaṃ, bhante”. “Sādhu, bhikkhave. Viññāṇaṃ, bhikkhave, ‘netaṃ mama, nesohamasmi, na meso attā’ti evametaṃ yathābhūtaṃ sammappaññāya daṭṭhabbaṃ … pe … evaṃ passaṃ … pe … nāparaṃ itthattāyāti pajānātī”ti.

Chaṭṭhaṃ.
