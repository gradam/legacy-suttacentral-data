Saṃyutta Nikāya 48

3. Cha­ḷindriya­vagga

27. Arahantasutta

“Chayimāni, bhikkhave, indriyāni. Katamāni cha? Cakkhundriyaṃ, sotindriyaṃ, ghānindriyaṃ, jivhindriyaṃ, kāyindriyaṃ, manindriyaṃ. Yato kho, bhikkhave, bhikkhu imesaṃ channaṃ indriyānaṃ samudayañca atthaṅgamañca assādañca ādīnavañca nissaraṇañca yathābhūtaṃ viditvā anupādāvimutto hoti—ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, ‘bhikkhu arahaṃ khīṇāsavo vusitavā katakaraṇīyo ohitabhāro anup­patta­sadat­tho parik­khī­ṇa­bhavasaṃ­yojano samma­dañ­ñā­vimutto’”ti.

Sattamaṃ.
