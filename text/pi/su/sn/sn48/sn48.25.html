Saṃyutta Nikāya 48

3. Cha­ḷindriya­vagga

25. Suddhakasutta

“Chayimāni, bhikkhave, indriyāni. Katamāni cha? Cakkhundriyaṃ, sotindriyaṃ, ghānindriyaṃ, jivhindriyaṃ, kāyindriyaṃ, manindriyaṃ—imāni kho, bhikkhave, cha indriyānī”ti.

Pañcamaṃ.
