Saṃyutta Nikāya 55

3. Saraṇānivagga

29. Dutiya­bhaya­ve­rū­pasan­ta­sutta

* Sāvatthi­nidānaṃ … pe … “yato kho, bhikkhave, ariyasāvakassa imāni pañca bhayāni verāni vūpasantāni honti, imehi catūhi sotā­patti­yaṅ­gehi samannāgato hoti, ayañcassa ariyo ñāyo paññāya sudiṭṭho hoti suppaṭividdho; so ākaṅkhamāno attanāva attānaṃ byākareyya: ‘khīṇanirayomhi khīṇa­ti­racchā­na­yoni khīṇa­petti­visayo khīṇā­pāya­dugga­ti­vini­pāto; sotā­panno­hamasmi avini­pāta­dhammo niyato sam­bodhi­parā­yaṇo’”ti.

Navamaṃ.
