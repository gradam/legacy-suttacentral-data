Saṃyutta Nikāya 23

2. Dutiya­māravagga

20. Vaya­dham­ma­sutta

Sāvatthi­nidānaṃ. Ekamantaṃ nisinno kho āyasmā rādho bhagavantaṃ etadavoca: “‘vayadhammo, vayadhammo’ti, bhante, vuccati. Katamo nu kho, bhante, vayadhammo”ti? “Rūpaṃ kho, rādha, vayadhammo, vedanā vayadhammo, saññā vayadhammo, saṅkhārā vayadhammo, viññāṇaṃ vayadhammo. Evaṃ passaṃ … pe … nāparaṃ itthattāyāti pajānātī”ti.

Dasamaṃ.
