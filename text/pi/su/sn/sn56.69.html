Saṃyutta Nikāya 56

7. Paṭha­ma­āmaka­dhañña­peyyā­la­vagga

69. Brahmaññasutta

… “Evameva kho, bhikkhave, appakā te sattā ye brahmaññā; atha kho eteva bahutarā sattā ye abrahmaññā … pe ….

Navamaṃ.
