Saṃyutta Nikāya 50

9. Punaoghavagga

99–​108. Puna­oghā­di­sutta

“Pañcimāni, bhikkhave, uddham­bhāgi­yāni saṃyojanāni. Katamāni pañca? Rūparāgo, arūparāgo, māno, uddhaccaṃ avijjā—imāni kho, bhikkhave, pañ­cuddham­bhāgi­yāni saṃyojanāni. Imesaṃ kho, bhikkhave, pañcannaṃ uddham­bhāgi­yā­naṃ saṃyojanānaṃ abhiññāya pariññāya parikkhayāya pahānāya pañca balāni bhāvetabbāni. Katamāni pañca? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu saddhābalaṃ bhāveti … pe … paññābalaṃ bhāveti rāga­vina­ya­pari­yosānaṃ dosa­vina­ya­pari­yosānaṃ moha­vina­ya­pari­yosānaṃ. Imesaṃ kho, bhikkhave, pañcannaṃ uddham­bhāgi­yā­naṃ saṃyojanānaṃ abhiññāya pariññāya parikkhayāya pahānāya imāni pañca balāni bhāvetabbānī”ti.

Dasamaṃ.

Oghavaggo dasamo.

Ogho yogo upādānaṃ,
ganthā anusayena ca;
Kāmaguṇā nīvaraṇā,
khandhā oruddham­bhāgi­yāti.

Balasaṃyuttaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
