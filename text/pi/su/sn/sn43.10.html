Saṃyutta Nikāya 43

1. Paṭhamavagga

10. Bojjhaṅgasutta

“Katamo ca, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo? Satta bojjhaṅgā. Ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo … pe ….

Dasamaṃ.
