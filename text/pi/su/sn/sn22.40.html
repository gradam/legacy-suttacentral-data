Saṃyutta Nikāya 22

4. Natumhākavagga

40. Dutiyaanudhammasutta

Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Dhammā­nudhammappa­ṭipannassa, bhikkhave, bhikkhuno ayamanudhammo hoti yaṃ rūpe aniccānupassī vihareyya … pe … parimuccati dukkhasmāti vadāmī”ti.

Aṭṭhamaṃ.
