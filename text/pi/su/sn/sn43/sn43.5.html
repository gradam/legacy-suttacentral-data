Saṃyutta Nikāya 43

1. Paṭhamavagga

5. Sati­paṭṭhā­na­sutta

“Katamo ca, bhikkhave, asaṅ­kha­ta­gāmi­maggo? Cattāro satipaṭṭhānā. Ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, asaṅ­kha­ta­gāmi­maggo … pe ….

Pañcamaṃ.
