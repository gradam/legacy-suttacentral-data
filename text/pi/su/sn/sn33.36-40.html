Saṃyutta Nikāya 33

1. Vacchagottavagga

36–​40. Rūpa­appaccu­palakkha­ṇādi­sutta­pañcaka

Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Rūpe kho, vaccha, appaccupalakkhaṇā … pe … viññāṇe kho, vaccha, appaccupalakkhaṇā … pe …. Cattālīsamaṃ.
