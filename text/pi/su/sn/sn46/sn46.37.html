Saṃyutta Nikāya 46

4. Nīvaraṇavagga

37. Buddhisutta

“Sattime, bhikkhave, bojjhaṅgā bhāvitā bahulīkatā buddhiyā aparihānāya saṃvattanti. Katame satta? Sati­sam­boj­jhaṅgo … pe … upekkhā­sam­boj­jhaṅgo. Ime kho, bhikkhave, satta bojjhaṅgā bhāvitā bahulīkatā buddhiyā aparihānāya saṃvattantī”ti.

Sattamaṃ.
