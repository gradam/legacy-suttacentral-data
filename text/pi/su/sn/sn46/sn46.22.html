Saṃyutta Nikāya 46

3. Udāyivagga

22. Boj­jhaṅ­ga­desanā­sutta

“Satta vo, bhikkhave, bojjhaṅge desessāmi; taṃ suṇātha. Katame ca, bhikkhave, satta bojjhaṅgā? Sati­sam­boj­jhaṅgo … pe … upekkhā­sam­boj­jhaṅgo—ime kho, bhikkhave, satta bojjhaṅgā”ti.

Dutiyaṃ.
