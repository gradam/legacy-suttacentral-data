Saṃyutta Nikāya 46

4. Nīvaraṇavagga

38. A­nīvara­ṇa­sutta

Yasmiṃ, bhikkhave, samaye ariyasāvako aṭṭhiṃ katvā manasi katvā sabbaṃ cetaso samannāharitvā ohitasoto dhammaṃ suṇāti, imassa pañca nīvaraṇā tasmiṃ samaye na honti. Satta bojjhaṅgā tasmiṃ samaye bhāva­nā­pāri­pūriṃ gacchanti.

Katame pañca nīvaraṇā tasmiṃ samaye na honti? Kāmacchan­da­nīvara­ṇaṃ tasmiṃ samaye na hoti, ­byāpā­da­nīvara­ṇaṃ tasmiṃ samaye na hoti, thina­middha­nīvara­ṇaṃ tasmiṃ samaye na hoti, uddhac­ca­kukkuc­ca­nīvara­ṇaṃ tasmiṃ samaye na hoti, vici­kicchā­nīvara­ṇaṃ tasmiṃ samaye na hoti. Imassa pañca nīvaraṇā tasmiṃ samaye na honti.

Katame satta bojjhaṅgā tasmiṃ samaye bhāva­nā­pāri­pūriṃ gacchanti? Sati­sam­boj­jhaṅgo tasmiṃ samaye bhāva­nā­pāri­pūriṃ gacchati … pe … upekkhā­sam­boj­jhaṅgo tasmiṃ samaye bhāva­nā­pāri­pūriṃ gacchati. Ime satta bojjhaṅgā tasmiṃ samaye bhāva­nā­pāri­pūriṃ gacchanti. Yasmiṃ, bhikkhave, samaye ariyasāvako aṭṭhiṃ katvā manasi katvā sabbaṃ cetaso samannāharitvā ohitasoto dhammaṃ suṇāti, imassa pañca nīvaraṇā tasmiṃ samaye na honti. Ime satta bojjhaṅgā tasmiṃ samaye bhāva­nā­pāri­pūriṃ gacchantī”ti.

Aṭṭhamaṃ.
