Saṃyutta Nikāya 46

2. Gilānavagga

18. Viraddhasutta

“Yesaṃ kesañci, bhikkhave, satta bojjhaṅgā viraddhā, viraddho tesaṃ ariyo maggo sammā duk­khak­kha­ya­gāmī. Yesaṃ kesañci, bhikkhave, satta bojjhaṅgā āraddhā, āraddho tesaṃ ariyo maggo sammā duk­khak­kha­ya­gāmī. Katame satta? Sati­sam­boj­jhaṅgo … pe … upekkhā­sam­boj­jhaṅgo—yesaṃ kesañci, bhikkhave, ime satta bojjhaṅgā viraddhā, viraddho tesaṃ ariyo maggo sammā duk­khak­kha­ya­gāmī. Yesaṃ kesañci, bhikkhave, ime satta bojjhaṅgā āraddhā, āraddho tesaṃ ariyo maggo sammā duk­khak­kha­ya­gāmī”ti.

Aṭṭhamaṃ.
