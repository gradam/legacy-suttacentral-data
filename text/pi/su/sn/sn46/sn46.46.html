Saṃyutta Nikāya 46

5. Cakka­vatti­vagga

46. Daliddasutta

“‘Daliddo, daliddo’ti, bhante, vuccati. Kittāvatā nu kho, bhante, ‘daliddo’ti vuccatī”ti? “Sattannaṃ kho, bhikkhu, bojjhaṅgānaṃ abhāvitattā abahulīkatattā ‘daliddo’ti vuccati. Katamesaṃ sattannaṃ? Sati­sam­boj­jhaṅ­gassa … pe … upekkhā­sam­boj­jhaṅ­gassa—imesaṃ kho, bhikkhu, sattannaṃ bojjhaṅgānaṃ abhāvitattā abahulīkatattā ‘daliddo’ti vuccatī”ti.

Chaṭṭhaṃ.
