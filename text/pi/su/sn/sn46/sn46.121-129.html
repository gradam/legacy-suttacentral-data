Saṃyutta Nikāya 46

13. Oghavagga

121–​129. Oghādisutta

“Cattārome, bhikkhave, oghā. Katame cattāro? Kāmogho, bhavogho, diṭṭhogho, avijjogho”ti vitthāretabbaṃ.
