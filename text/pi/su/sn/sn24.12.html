Saṃyutta Nikāya 24

1. Sotāpattivagga

12. Anantavāsutta

Sāvatthi­nidānaṃ. “Kismiṃ nu kho, bhikkhave, sati, kiṃ upādāya, kiṃ abhinivissa evaṃ diṭṭhi uppajjati: ‘anantavā loko’”ti? “Bhagavaṃmūlakā no, bhante, dhammā … pe … niyato sam­bodhi­parā­yano”ti.

Dvādasamaṃ.
