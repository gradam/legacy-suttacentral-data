Saṃyutta Nikāya 43

1. Paṭhamavagga

3. Savitakkasavicārasutta

“Katamo ca, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo? Savitakkasavicāro samādhi, avitakkavicāramatto samādhi, avitakkaavicāro samādhi–  ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo … pe ….

Tatiyaṃ.
