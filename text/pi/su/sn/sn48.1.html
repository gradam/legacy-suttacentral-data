Saṃyutta Nikāya 48

1. Suddhikavagga

1. Suddhikasutta

Sāvatthinidānaṃ. Tatra kho bhagavā etadavoca. “Pañcimāni, bhikkhave, indriyāni. Katamāni pañca? Saddhindriyaṃ, vīriyindriyaṃ, satindriyaṃ, samādhindriyaṃ, paññindriyaṃ–  imāni kho, bhikkhave, pañcindriyānī”ti.

Paṭhamaṃ.
