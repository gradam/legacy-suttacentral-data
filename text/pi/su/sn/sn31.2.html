Saṃyutta Nikāya 31

1. Gandhabbavagga

2. Sucaritasutta

Sāvatthi­nidānaṃ. Ekamantaṃ nisinno kho so bhikkhu bhagavantaṃ etadavoca: “ko nu kho, bhante, hetu, ko paccayo, yena midhekacco kāyassa bhedā paraṃ maraṇā gandhab­ba­kāyikā­naṃ devānaṃ sahabyataṃ upapajjatī”ti? “Idha, bhikkhu, ekacco kāyena sucaritaṃ carati, vācāya sucaritaṃ carati, manasā sucaritaṃ carati. Tassa sutaṃ hoti: ‘gandhab­ba­kāyikā devā dīghāyukā vaṇṇavanto sukhabahulā’ti. Tassa evaṃ hoti: ‘aho vatāhaṃ kāyassa bhedā paraṃ maraṇā gandhab­ba­kāyikā­naṃ devānaṃ sahabyataṃ upapajjeyyan’ti. So kāyassa bhedā paraṃ maraṇā gandhab­ba­kāyikā­naṃ devānaṃ sahabyataṃ upapajjati. Ayaṃ kho, bhikkhu, hetu, ayaṃ paccayo, yena midhekacco kāyassa bhedā paraṃ maraṇā gandhab­ba­kāyikā­naṃ devānaṃ sahabyataṃ upapajjatī”ti.

Dutiyaṃ.
