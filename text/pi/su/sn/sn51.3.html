Saṃyutta Nikāya 51

1. Cāpālavagga

3. Ariyasutta

“Cattārome, bhikkhave, iddhipādā bhāvitā bahulīkatā ariyā niyyānikā niyyanti takkarassa sammā dukkhakkhayāya. Katame cattāro? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu chanda­samā­dhippa­dhāna­saṅkhā­rasa­mannā­gataṃ iddhipādaṃ bhāveti, vīriyasamādhi … pe … cittasamādhi … pe … vīmaṃsā­samā­dhippa­dhāna­saṅkhā­rasa­mannā­gataṃ iddhipādaṃ bhāveti. Ime kho, bhikkhave, cattāro iddhipādā bhāvitā bahulīkatā ariyā niyyānikā niyyanti takkarassa sammā dukkhakkhayāyā”ti.

Tatiyaṃ.
