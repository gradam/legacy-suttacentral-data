Saṃyutta Nikāya 25

1. Cakkhuvagga

6. Rūpasaññāsutta

Sāvatthi­nidānaṃ. “Rūpasaññā, bhikkhave, aniccā vipariṇāmī aññathābhāvī; saddasaññā … gandhasaññā … rasasaññā … ­phoṭṭhab­ba­saññā … dhammasaññā aniccā vipariṇāmī aññathābhāvī. Yo, bhikkhave, ime dhamme evaṃ saddahati adhimuccati, ayaṃ vuccati ‘saddhānusārī … pe … sam­bodhi­parā­yano’”ti.

Chaṭṭhaṃ.
