Saṃyutta Nikāya 24

1. Sotāpattivagga

11. Antavāsutta

Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Kismiṃ nu kho, bhikkhave, sati, kiṃ upādāya, kiṃ abhinivissa evaṃ diṭṭhi uppajjati:  ‘antavā loko’”ti? “Bhagavaṃmūlakā no, bhante, dhammā … pe … niyato sambodhiparāyano”ti.

Ekādasamaṃ.
