Saṃyutta Nikāya 43

1. Paṭhamavagga

8. Indriyasutta

“Katamo ca, bhikkhave, asaṅ­kha­ta­gāmi­maggo? Pañcindriyāni. Ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, asaṅ­kha­ta­gāmi­maggo … pe ….

Aṭṭhamaṃ.
