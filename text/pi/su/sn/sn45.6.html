Saṃyutta Nikāya 45

1. Avijjāvagga

6. Paṭha­ma­aññata­ra­bhik­khu­sutta

Sāvatthi­nidānaṃ. Atha kho aññataro bhikkhu yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami … pe … ekamantaṃ nisinno kho so bhikkhu bhagavantaṃ etadavoca: “‘brahmacariyaṃ, brahmacariyan’ti, bhante, vuccati. Katamaṃ nu kho, bhante, brahmacariyaṃ, katamaṃ brahma­cari­ya­pari­yosānan”ti?

“Ayameva kho, bhikkhu, ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo brahmacariyaṃ, seyyathidaṃ— sammādiṭṭhi … pe … sammāsamādhi. Yo kho, bhikkhu, rāgakkhayo dosakkhayo mohakkhayo—idaṃ brahma­cari­ya­pari­yosānan”ti.

Chaṭṭhaṃ.
