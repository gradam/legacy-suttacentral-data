Saṃyutta Nikāya 7

1. Arahantavagga

3. Asu­rinda­kasutta

Ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā rājagahe viharati veḷuvane kalanda­ka­nivāpe. Assosi kho asu­rinda­ka­bhārad­vājo brāhmaṇo: “bhārad­vāja­gotto brāhmaṇo kira samaṇassa gotamassa santike agārasmā anagāriyaṃ pabbajito”ti kupito anattamano yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṃ asabbhāhi pharusāhi vācāhi akkosati paribhāsati. Evaṃ vutte, bhagavā tuṇhī ahosi. Atha kho asu­rinda­ka­bhārad­vājo brāhmaṇo bhagavantaṃ etadavoca: “jitosi, samaṇa, jitosi, samaṇā”ti.

“Jayaṃ ve maññati bālo,
vācāya pharusaṃ bhaṇaṃ;
Jayañcevassa taṃ hoti,
yā titikkhā vijānato.

Tasseva tena pāpiyo,
yo kuddhaṃ paṭikujjhati;
Kuddhaṃ appa­ṭikuj­jhanto,
saṅgāmaṃ jeti dujjayaṃ.

Ubhinnamatthaṃ carati,
attano ca parassa ca;
Paraṃ saṅkupitaṃ ñatvā,
yo sato upasammati.

Ubhinnaṃ tikicchantānaṃ,
attano ca parassa ca;
Janā maññanti bāloti,
ye dhammassa akovidā”ti.

Evaṃ vutte, asu­rinda­ka­bhārad­vājo brāhmaṇo bhagavantaṃ etadavoca: “abhikkantaṃ, bho gotama … pe … abbhaññāsi. Aññataro ca panāyasmā bhāradvājo arahataṃ ahosīti.
