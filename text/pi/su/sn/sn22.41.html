Saṃyutta Nikāya 22

4. Natumhākavagga

41. Tatiyaanudhammasutta

Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Dhammā­nudhammappa­ṭipannassa, bhikkhave, bhikkhuno ayamanudhammo hoti yaṃ rūpe dukkhānupassī vihareyya … pe … parimuccati dukkhasmāti vadāmī”ti.

Navamaṃ.
