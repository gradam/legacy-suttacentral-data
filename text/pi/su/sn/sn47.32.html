Saṃyutta Nikāya 47

4. Ananus­suta­vagga

32. Virāgasutta

“Cattārome, bhikkhave, satipaṭṭhānā bhāvitā bahulīkatā ekanta­nibbidāya virāgāya nirodhāya upasamāya abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saṃvattanti.

Katame cattāro? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu kāye kāyānupassī viharati ātāpī sampajāno satimā, vineyya loke abhij­jhā­do­manas­saṃ; vedanāsu … pe … citte … pe … dhammesu dhammānupassī viharati ātāpī sampajāno satimā, vineyya loke abhij­jhā­do­manas­saṃ. Ime kho, bhikkhave, cattāro satipaṭṭhānā bhāvitā bahulīkatā ekanta­nibbidāya virāgāya nirodhāya upasamāya abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saṃvattantī”ti.

Dutiyaṃ.
