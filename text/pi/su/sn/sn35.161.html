Saṃyutta Nikāya 35

16. Nandik­kha­ya­vagga

161. Jīvakam­ba­va­na­paṭi­sallā­na­sutta

Ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā rājagahe viharati jīvakambavane. Tatra kho bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi … pe … “paṭisallāne, bhikkhave, yogamāpajjatha. Paṭisallīnassa, bhikkhave, bhikkhuno yathābhūtaṃ okkhāyati. Kiñca yathābhūtaṃ okkhāyati? Cakkhuṃ aniccanti yathābhūtaṃ okkhāyati, rūpā aniccāti yathābhūtaṃ okkhāyati, cakkhuviññāṇaṃ aniccanti yathābhūtaṃ okkhāyati, cak­khu­samphasso aniccoti yathābhūtaṃ okkhāyati, yampidaṃ cak­khu­samphas­sa­pac­cayā uppajjati vedayitaṃ sukhaṃ vā dukkhaṃ vā aduk­kha­ma­su­khaṃ vā tampi aniccanti yathābhūtaṃ okkhāyati … pe … mano aniccoti yathābhūtaṃ okkhāyati, dhammā … manoviññāṇaṃ … manosamphasso … yampidaṃ mano­samphas­sa­pac­cayā uppajjati vedayitaṃ sukhaṃ vā dukkhaṃ vā aduk­kha­ma­su­khaṃ vā tampi aniccanti yathābhūtaṃ okkhāyati. Paṭisallāne, bhikkhave, yogamāpajjatha. Paṭisallīnassa, bhikkhave, bhikkhuno yathābhūtaṃ okkhāyatī”ti.

Chaṭṭhaṃ.
