Saṃyutta Nikāya 35

16. Nandik­kha­ya­vagga

157. Bāhira­nandik­kha­ya­sutta

“Anicceyeva, bhikkhave, bhikkhu rūpe aniccāti passati, sāssa hoti sammādiṭṭhi. Sammā passaṃ nibbindati. Nandikkhayā rāgakkhayo; rāgakkhayā nandikkhayo. Nandi­rā­gak­khayā cittaṃ suvimuttanti vuccati. Anicceyeva, bhikkhave, bhikkhu sadde … gandhe … rase … phoṭṭhabbe … dhamme aniccāti passati, sāssa hoti sammādiṭṭhi. Sammā passaṃ nibbindati. Nandikkhayā rāgakkhayo; rāgakkhayā nandikkhayo. Nandi­rā­gak­khayā cittaṃ suvimuttanti vuccatī”ti.

Dutiyaṃ.
