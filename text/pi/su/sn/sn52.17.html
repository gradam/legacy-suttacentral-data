Saṃyutta Nikāya 52

2. Dutiyavagga

17. Sabbatthagāminisutta

“Imesañca panāhaṃ, āvuso, catunnaṃ satipaṭṭhānānaṃ bhāvitattā bahulīkatattā sabbattha­gāmi­nippa­ṭipadaṃ yathābhūtaṃ pajānāmī”ti.

Sattamaṃ.
