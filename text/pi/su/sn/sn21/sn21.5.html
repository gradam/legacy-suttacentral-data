Saṃyutta Nikāya 21

1. Bhikkhuvagga

5. Sujātasutta

Sāvatthiyaṃ viharati. Atha kho āyasmā sujāto yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami. Addasā kho bhagavā āyasmantaṃ sujātaṃ dūratova āgacchantaṃ. Disvāna bhikkhū āmantesi: “ubhayenevāyaṃ, bhikkhave, kulaputto sobhati—yañca abhirūpo dassanīyo pāsādiko paramāya vaṇṇa­pok­kha­ra­tāya samannāgato, yassa catthāya kulaputtā sammadeva agārasmā anagāriyaṃ pabbajanti tadanuttaraṃ brahma­cari­ya­pari­yosānaṃ diṭṭheva dhamme sayaṃ abhiññā sacchikatvā upasampajja viharatī”ti. Idamavoca bhagavā … pe … satthā: 

“Sobhati vatāyaṃ bhikkhu,
ujubhūtena cetasā;
Vippayutto visaṃyutto,
anupādāya nibbuto;
Dhāreti antimaṃ dehaṃ,
jetvā māraṃ savāhinin”ti.

Pañcamaṃ.
