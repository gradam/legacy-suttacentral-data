Saṃyutta Nikāya 56

9. Tatiya­āmaka­dhañña­peyyā­la­vagga

88. Ajeḷakasutta

… “Evameva kho, bhikkhave, appakā te sattā ye ajeḷa­ka­paṭig­gahaṇā paṭiviratā; atha kho eteva bahutarā sattā ye ajeḷa­ka­paṭig­gahaṇā appaṭiviratā … pe ….

Aṭṭhamaṃ.
