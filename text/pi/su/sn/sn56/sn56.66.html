Saṃyutta Nikāya 56

7. Paṭha­ma­āmaka­dhañña­peyyā­la­vagga

66. Matteyyasutta

… “Evameva kho, bhikkhave, appakā te sattā ye matteyyā; atha kho eteva bahutarā sattā ye amatteyyā … pe ….

Chaṭṭhaṃ.
