Saṃyutta Nikāya 56

7. Paṭha­ma­āmaka­dhañña­peyyā­la­vagga

70. Pacāyikasutta

… “Evameva kho, bhikkhave, appakā te sattā ye kule jeṭṭhāpacāyino; atha kho eteva bahutarā sattā ye kule ajeṭṭhā­pacāyi­noti … pe ….

Dasamaṃ.

Paṭha­ma­āmaka­dhañña­peyyā­la­vaggo sattamo.

Aññatra paccantaṃ paññā,
Surā­meraya­odakā;
Matteyya petteyyā cāpi,
Sāmaññaṃ brah­mapa­cāyi­kanti.
