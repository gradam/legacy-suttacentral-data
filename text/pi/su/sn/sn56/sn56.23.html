Saṃyutta Nikāya 56

3. Koṭigāmavagga

23. Sammā­sambud­dha­sutta

Sāvatthi­nidānaṃ. Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, ariyasaccāni. Katamāni cattāri? Dukkhaṃ ariyasaccaṃ … pe … duk­kha­nirodha­gāminī paṭipadā ariyasaccaṃ—imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri ariyasaccāni. Imesaṃ kho, bhikkhave, catunnaṃ ariyasaccānaṃ yathābhūtaṃ abhisam­buddhattā tathāgato ‘arahaṃ sammāsambuddho’ti vuccati.

Tasmātiha, bhikkhave, ‘idaṃ dukkhan’ti yogo karaṇīyo … pe … ‘ayaṃ duk­kha­nirodha­gāminī paṭipadā’ti yogo karaṇīyo”ti.

Tatiyaṃ.
