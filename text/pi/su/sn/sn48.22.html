Saṃyutta Nikāya 48

3. Cha­ḷindriya­vagga

22. Jīvi­tin­driya­sutta

“Tīṇimāni, bhikkhave, indriyāni. Katamāni tīṇi? Itthindriyaṃ, purisindriyaṃ, jīvitindriyaṃ—imāni kho, bhikkhave, tīṇi indriyānī”ti.

Dutiyaṃ.
