Saṃyutta Nikāya 35

16. Nandik­kha­ya­vagga

156. Ajjhat­ta­nandik­kha­ya­sutta

“Aniccaṃyeva, bhikkhave, bhikkhu cakkhuṃ aniccanti passati, sāssa hoti sammādiṭṭhi. Sammā passaṃ nibbindati. Nandikkhayā rāgakkhayo; rāgakkhayā nandikkhayo. Nandi­rā­gak­khayā cittaṃ suvimuttanti vuccati … pe … aniccaṃyeva, bhikkhave, bhikkhu jivhaṃ aniccanti passati, sāssa hoti sammādiṭṭhi. Sammā passaṃ nibbindati. Nandikkhayā rāgakkhayo; rāgakkhayā … pe … cittaṃ suvimuttanti vuccati … pe … aniccaṃyeva, bhikkhave, bhikkhu manaṃ aniccanti passati, sāssa hoti sammādiṭṭhi. Sammā passaṃ nibbindati. Nandikkhayā rāgakkhayo; rāgakkhayā nandikkhayo. Nandi­rā­gak­khayā cittaṃ suvimuttanti vuccatī”ti.

Paṭhamaṃ.
