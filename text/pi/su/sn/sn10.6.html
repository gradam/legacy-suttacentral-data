Saṃyutta Nikāya 10

1. Indakavagga

6. Piyaṅkarasutta

Ekaṃ samayaṃ āyasmā anuruddho sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anātha­piṇḍi­kassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena āyasmā anuruddho rattiyā paccūsasamayaṃ paccuṭṭhāya dhammapadāni bhāsati. Atha kho piyaṅkaramātā yakkhinī puttakaṃ evaṃ tosesi: 

“Mā saddaṃ kari piyaṅkara,
Bhikkhu dhammapadāni bhāsati;
Api ca dhammapadaṃ vijāniya,
Paṭipajjema hitāya no siyā.

Pāṇesu ca saṃyamāmase,
Sampajānamusā na bhaṇāmase;
Sikkhema susīlyamattano,
Api muccema pisācayoniyā”ti.
