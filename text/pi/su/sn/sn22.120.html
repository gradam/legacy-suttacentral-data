Saṃyutta Nikāya 22

12. Dhamma­ka­thi­ka­vagga

120. Saṃyo­jani­ya­sutta

Sāvatthi­nidānaṃ. “Saṃyojaniye ca, bhikkhave, dhamme desessāmi saṃyojanañca. Taṃ suṇātha. Katame ca, bhikkhave, saṃyojaniyā dhammā, katamaṃ saṃyojanaṃ? Rūpaṃ, bhikkhave, saṃyojaniyo dhammo; yo tattha chandarāgo, taṃ tattha saṃyojanaṃ. Vedanā … pe … saññā … saṅkhārā … viññāṇaṃ saṃyojaniyo dhammo; yo tattha chandarāgo, taṃ tattha saṃyojanaṃ. Ime vuccanti, bhikkhave, saṃyojaniyā dhammā, idaṃ saṃyojanan”ti.

Aṭṭhamaṃ.
