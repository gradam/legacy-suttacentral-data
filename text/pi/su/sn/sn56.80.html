Saṃyutta Nikāya 56

8. Dutiya­āmaka­dhañña­peyyā­la­vagga

80. Gandha­vi­lepa­na­sutta

… “Evameva kho, bhikkhave, appakā te sattā ye mālā­gandha­vi­lepa­na­dhāra­ṇa­maṇḍa­na­vi­bhū­sa­naṭ­ṭhānā paṭiviratā; atha kho eteva bahutarā sattā ye mālā­gandha­vi­lepa­na­dhāra­ṇa­maṇḍa­na­vi­bhū­sa­naṭ­ṭhānā appaṭiviratā … pe ….

Dasamaṃ.

Dutiya­āmaka­dhañña­peyyā­la­vaggo aṭṭhamo.

Pāṇaṃ adinnaṃ kāmesu,
musāvādañca pesuññaṃ;
Pharusaṃ samphappalāpaṃ,
bījañca vikālaṃ gandhanti.
