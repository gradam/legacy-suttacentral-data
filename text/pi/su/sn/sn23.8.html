Saṃyutta Nikāya 23

1. Paṭha­ma­māravagga

8. Arahantasutta

Sāvatthi­nidānaṃ. Ekamantaṃ nisinnaṃ kho āyasmantaṃ rādhaṃ bhagavā etadavoca: “pañcime, rādha, upādā­nak­khan­dhā. Katame pañca? Rūpupā­dā­nak­khan­dho … pe … viñ­ñāṇupā­dā­nak­khan­dho. Yato kho, rādha, bhikkhu imesaṃ pañcannaṃ upādā­nak­khan­dhā­naṃ samudayañca atthaṅgamañca assādañca ādīnavañca nissaraṇañca yathābhūtaṃ viditvā anupādāvimutto hoti–  ayaṃ vuccati, rādha, bhikkhu arahaṃ khīṇāsavo vusitavā katakaraṇīyo ohitabhāro anup­patta­sadat­tho parik­khī­ṇa­bhavasaṃ­yojano samma­dañ­ñā­vimutto”ti.

Aṭṭhamaṃ.
