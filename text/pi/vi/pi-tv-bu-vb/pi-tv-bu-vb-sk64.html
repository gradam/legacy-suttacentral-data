Theravāda Vinaya

Mahāvibhaṅga

Sekhiyakaṇḍa

Pādukavagga

Sekhiyā 64: 4 Sayana­sikkhā­pada

Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anātha­piṇḍi­kassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū sayanagatassa dhammaṃ desenti … pe ….

“Na sayanagatassa agilānassa dhammaṃ desessāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (64:209)

Na sayanagatassa agilānassa dhammo desetabbo. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca antamaso chamāyampi nipannassa sayanagatassa agilānassa dhammaṃ deseti, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

Anāpatti—asañcicca … pe … ādi­kammi­kas­sāti.

Catut­tha­sikkhā­padaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
