Theravāda Vinaya

Mahāvibhaṅga

Sekhiyakaṇḍa

Surusuruvagga

Sekhiyā 59: Sattha­pāṇi­sikkhā­pada

Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anātha­piṇḍi­kassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū satthapāṇissa dhammaṃ desenti … pe ….

“Na satthapāṇissa agilānassa dhammaṃ desessāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (59:204)

Satthaṃ nāma ekatodhāraṃ ubhatodhāraṃ paharaṇaṃ.

Na satthapāṇissa agilānassa dhammo desetabbo. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca satthapāṇissa agilānassa dhammaṃ deseti, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

Anāpatti—asañcicca … pe … ādi­kammi­kas­sāti.

Navama­sikkhā­padaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
