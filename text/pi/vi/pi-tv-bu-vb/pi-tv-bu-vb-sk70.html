Theravāda Vinaya

Mahāvibhaṅga

Sekhiyakaṇḍa

Pādukavagga

Sekhiyā 70: 10 Ṭhita­sikkhā­pada

Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anātha­piṇḍi­kassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū ṭhitā nisinnassa dhammaṃ desenti … pe ….

“Na ṭhito nisinnassa agilānassa dhammaṃ desessāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā”ti. (70:215)

Na ṭhitena nisinnassa agilānassa dhammo desetabbo. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca ṭhito nisinnassa agilānassa dhammaṃ deseti, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

Anāpatti—asañcicca … pe … ādi­kammi­kas­sāti.

Dasama­sikkhā­padaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
