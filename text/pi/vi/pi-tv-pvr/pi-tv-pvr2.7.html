Parivāra

Bhik­khu­ni­vibhaṅga

Paṭhamabhāga

7 Samathavāra

Avassutāya bhikkhuniyā avassutassa purisa­pugga­lassa kāyasaṃsaggaṃ sādiyantiyā āpattiyo sattannaṃ samathānaṃ katihi samathehi sammanti? Avassutāya bhikkhuniyā avassutassa purisa­pugga­lassa kāyasaṃsaggaṃ sādiyantiyā āpattiyo sattannaṃ samathānaṃ tīhi samathehi sammanti—siyā sam­mukhā­vina­yena ca paṭiñ­ñā­ta­kara­ṇena ca, siyā sam­mukhā­vina­yena ca tiṇa­vatthā­ra­kena ca … pe …. (1)

Dadhiṃ viññāpetvā bhuñjantiyā āpattiyo sattannaṃ samathānaṃ katihi samathehi sammanti? Dadhiṃ viññāpetvā bhuñjantiyā āpattiyo sattannaṃ samathānaṃ tīhi samathehi sammanti—siyā sam­mukhā­vina­yena ca paṭiñ­ñā­ta­kara­ṇena ca, siyā sam­mukhā­vina­yena ca tiṇa­vatthā­ra­kena ca. (2)

Samathavāro niṭṭhito sattamo.
