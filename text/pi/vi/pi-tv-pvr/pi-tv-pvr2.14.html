Bhik­khu­ni­vibhaṅga

Dutiyabhāga

6. Adhikaraṇavāra

Kāyasaṃsaggaṃ ­sādiya­na­pac­cayā āpattiyo catunnaṃ adhikaraṇānaṃ katamaṃ adhikaraṇaṃ? Kāyasaṃsaggaṃ ­sādiya­na­pac­cayā āpattiyo catunnaṃ adhikaraṇānaṃ, āpat­tādhika­ra­ṇaṃ … pe …. (1)

Dadhiṃ viññāpetvā bhuñ­jana­pac­cayā āpattiyo catunnaṃ adhikaraṇānaṃ katamaṃ adhikaraṇaṃ? Dadhiṃ viññāpetvā bhuñ­jana­pac­cayā āpattiyo catunnaṃ adhikaraṇānaṃ—āpat­tādhika­ra­ṇaṃ. (2)

Adhikaraṇavāro niṭṭhito chaṭṭho.
