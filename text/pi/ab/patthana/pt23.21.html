Dhamma­pac­canīyā­nuloma (4), Tika­tika­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (5)

23.21. Vipākattika, Kusalattika

Navipākaṃ nakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā vipā­ka­dham­ma­dhammo kusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

Navipākaṃ naakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā vipā­ka­dham­ma­dhammo akusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

Navipākaṃ naabyākataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca neva­vipāka­navi­pā­ka­dham­ma­dhammo abyākato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.
