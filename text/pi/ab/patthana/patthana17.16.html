Dham­mānu­loma­pac­canīya (3), Tika­tika­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (5)

17.16. Kusalattika, Uppannattika

Kusalo anuppanno dhammo nakusalassa naanuppannassa dhammassa āramma­ṇa­pac­ca­yena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

Ārammaṇe aṭṭhārasa, adhipatiyā tīṇi, upanissaye aṭṭhārasa. (Uppādī anup­panna­sadisaṃ.)
