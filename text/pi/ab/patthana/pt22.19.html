Dhamma­pac­canīyā­nuloma (4), Tika­duka­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (4)

22.19. Upādinnattika, Hetuduka

Naupādin­nu­pādā­niyaṃ nahetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca anupā­dinnu­pādā­niyo hetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā nava, ārammaṇe nava, adhipatiyā cha … pe … āsevane cha … pe … vipāke cha … pe … avigate nava.

Naupādin­nu­pādā­niyaṃ nanahetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca anupā­dinnu­pādā­niyo nahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā aṭṭhārasa, ārammaṇe nava, adhipatiyā ekādasa … pe … āsevane cha … pe … vipāke pannarasa … pe … avigate aṭṭhārasa.
