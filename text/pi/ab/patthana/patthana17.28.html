Paṭṭhānapakaraṇa

Dham­mānu­loma­pac­canīya (3), Tika­tika­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (5)

17.28. Sani­dassa­nat­tika, Vedanāttika

Ani­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­ghaṃ sukhāya vedanāya sampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naa­ni­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­gho nasukhāya vedanāya sampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, ārammaṇe tīṇi … pe … avigate cha.

Ani­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­ghaṃ dukkhāya vedanāya sampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naa­ni­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­gho nadukkhāya vedanāya sampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, ārammaṇe tīṇi … pe … vipāke tīṇi … pe … avigate cha.

Ani­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­ghaṃ aduk­kha­ma­su­khāya vedanāya sampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naa­ni­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­gho naa­duk­kha­ma­su­khāya vedanāya sampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, ārammaṇe tīṇi … pe … avigate cha.
