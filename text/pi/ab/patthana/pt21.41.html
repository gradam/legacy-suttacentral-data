Paṭṭhānapakaraṇa

Dhamma­pac­canīyā­nuloma (4), Duka­tika­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (3)

21.41. Sani­dassa­na­duka, Sani­dassa­nat­tika

Nasanidassanaṃ ­nasani­dassa­na­sappa­ṭi­ghaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca sanidassano sani­dassa­na­sappa­ṭi­gho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ, adhipatiyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.

Nasanidassanaṃ naani­dassa­na­sappa­ṭi­ghaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca anidassano ani­dassa­na­sappa­ṭi­gho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ, adhipatiyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.

Nasanidassanaṃ naani­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­ghaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca anidassano ani­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­gho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ, adhipatiyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.
