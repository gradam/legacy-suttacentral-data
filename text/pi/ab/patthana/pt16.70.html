Dham­mānu­loma­pac­canīya (3), Tika­duka­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (4)

16.70. Sani­dassa­nat­tika, Citta­sa­muṭṭhā­na­duka

Ani­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­ghaṃ citta­sa­muṭṭhā­naṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naa­ni­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­gho nocitta­sa­muṭṭhāno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

Hetuyā cha, ārammaṇe tīṇi … pe … avigate cha.
