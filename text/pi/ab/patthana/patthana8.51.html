Dhamma­pac­canīya (2), Duka­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (2)

8.51. Saṃ­kiliṭ­ṭha­duka

Nasaṃkiliṭṭhaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaṃkiliṭṭho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (1)

Naasaṃ­kiliṭ­ṭhaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naasaṃkiliṭṭho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Naasaṃ­kiliṭ­ṭhaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaṃkiliṭṭho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Naasaṃ­kiliṭ­ṭhaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaṃkiliṭṭho ca naasaṃkiliṭṭho ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (3)

Nasaṃ­kiliṭ­ṭhañca naasaṃ­kiliṭ­ṭhañca dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaṃkiliṭṭho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (1) (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

Hetuyā pañca, ārammaṇe dve … pe … vipāke ekaṃ … pe … avigate pañca. (Sabbattha vitthāro.)
