Dhamma­pac­canīyā­nuloma (4), Duka­tika­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (3)

21.32. Hetuduka, Uppannattika

Nahetu nauppanno dhammo hetussa uppannassa dhammassa āramma­ṇa­pac­ca­yena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

Ārammaṇe nava, adhipatiyā upanissaye nava.
