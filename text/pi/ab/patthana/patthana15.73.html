Paṭṭhānapakaraṇa

Dham­mānu­loma­pac­canīya (3), Duka­tika­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (3)

15.73. Saraṇaduka, Upādinnattika

Araṇaṃ upādin­nu­pādā­niyaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo naupādin­nu­pādā­niyo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.

Araṇaṃ anupādin­na­a­nu­pādā­niyaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo naa­nupādin­na­anupā­dā­niyo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.
