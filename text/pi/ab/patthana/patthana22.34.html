Dhamma­pac­canīyā­nuloma (4), Tika­duka­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (4)

22.34. Sani­dassa­nat­tika, Kilesadukādi

­Nasani­dassa­na­sappa­ṭi­ghaṃ nokilesaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca ani­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­gho kileso dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.
