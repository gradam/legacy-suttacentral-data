Paṭṭhānapakaraṇa

Dham­mānu­loma­pac­canīya (3), Duka­duka­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (6)

18.26 Saraṇaduka, Cūḷantaraduka

Araṇo appaccayo dhammo nasaraṇassa naappaccayassa dhammassa āramma­ṇa­pac­ca­yena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

Ārammaṇe adhipatiyā upanissaye ekaṃ.

Araṇo asaṅkhato dhammo nasaraṇassa naasaṅkhatassa dhammassa āramma­ṇa­pac­ca­yena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

Ārammaṇe adhipatiyā upanissaye ekaṃ.

Araṇo sanidassano dhammo naaraṇassa nasa­ni­dassa­nassa dhammassa āramma­ṇa­pac­ca­yena paccayo. Araṇo sanidassano dhammo nasaraṇassa nasa­ni­dassa­nassa dhammassa āramma­ṇa­pac­ca­yena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

Ārammaṇe dve, adhipatiyā ekaṃ, upanissaye purejāte atthiyā avigate dve.
