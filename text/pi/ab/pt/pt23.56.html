Dhamma­pac­canīyā­nuloma (4), Tika­tika­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (5)

23.56. Sani­dassa­nat­tika, Atītattika

Nasa­ni­dassa­na­sappa­ṭi­gho napaccuppanno dhammo ani­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­ghassa paccuppannassa dhammassa āramma­ṇa­pac­ca­yena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

Ārammaṇe cha, adhipatiyā cha, anantare samanantare tīṇi, upanissaye cha, natthiyā vigate tīṇi.
