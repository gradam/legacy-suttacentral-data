Dham­mānu­loma­pac­canīya (3), Duka­tika­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (3)

41 Citta­sam­payut­ta­duka, Kusalattika

Citta­sam­payut­taṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nacitta­sam­payutto nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.

Citta­sam­payut­taṃ akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nacitta­sam­payutto naakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ. (Abyākatamūlaṃ tīṇiyeva paccayavasena.)
