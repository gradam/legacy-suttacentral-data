Dham­mānu­loma­pac­canīya (3), Tika­duka­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (4)

16.94 Sani­dassa­nat­tika, Sauttaraduka

Ani­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­ghaṃ sauttaraṃ dhammaṃ paccayā nasa­ni­dassa­na­sappa­ṭi­gho nasauttaro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

Ani­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­ghaṃ anuttaraṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naa­ni­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­gho naanuttaro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha … pe … avigate cha.
