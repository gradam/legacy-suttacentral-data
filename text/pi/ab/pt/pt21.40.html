Dhamma­pac­canīyā­nuloma (4), Duka­tika­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (3)

40 Sappac­caya­dukādi, Sani­dassa­nat­tika

Naappaccayaṃ ­nasani­dassa­na­sappa­ṭi­ghaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca sappaccayo sani­dassa­na­sappa­ṭi­gho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ, adhipatiyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.

Naappaccayaṃ naani­dassa­na­sappa­ṭi­ghaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca sappaccayo ani­dassa­na­sappa­ṭi­gho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ, adhipatiyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.

(Naappac­caya­naa­ni­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­gha­mūlepi ekameva.) Hetuyā ekaṃ, adhipatiyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.

(Nasaṅkhataṃ nasappac­caya­sadisaṃ.)
