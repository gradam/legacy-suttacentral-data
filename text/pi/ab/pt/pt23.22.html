Dhamma­pac­canīyā­nuloma (4), Tika­tika­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (5)

22 Upādinnattika, Kusalattika

Naanupā­din­nu­pādā­niyaṃ nakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā anupā­dinnu­pādā­niyo kusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha … pe … avigate cha.

Naanupā­din­nu­pādā­niyaṃ naakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā anupā­dinnu­pādā­niyo akusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

Naupādin­nu­pādā­niyaṃ naabyākataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca anupā­dinnu­pādā­niyo abyākato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pañca, adhipatiyā pañca … pe … avigate pañca.
