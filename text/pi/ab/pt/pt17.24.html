Paṭṭhānapakaraṇa

Dham­mānu­loma­pac­canīya (3), Tika­tika­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (5)

17.24 Saṃ­kiliṭ­ṭhat­tika, Kusalattika

Asaṃ­kiliṭ­ṭha­saṃki­lesikaṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaṃ­kiliṭ­ṭha­saṃki­lesiko nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi.

Asaṃ­kiliṭ­ṭha­asaṃ­ki­lesikaṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naasaṃ­kiliṭ­ṭha­asaṃ­ki­lesiko nakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha … pe … avigate cha.

Saṃ­kiliṭ­ṭha­saṃki­lesikaṃ akusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaṃ­kiliṭ­ṭha­saṃki­lesiko naakusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā adhipatiyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

Asaṃ­kiliṭ­ṭha­saṃki­lesikaṃ abyākataṃ dhammaṃ paccayā naasaṃ­kiliṭ­ṭha­saṃki­lesiko naabyākato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha … pe … avigate cha.
