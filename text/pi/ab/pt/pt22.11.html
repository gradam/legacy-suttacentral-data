Dhamma­pac­canīyā­nuloma (4), Tika­duka­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (4)

22.11 Kusalattika, Arūpā­vacara­duka

Nakusalaṃ naarūpāvacaraṃ dhammaṃ paccayā kusalo arūpāvacaro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha … pe … avigate cha.

Nakusalaṃ nana­arūpā­vacaraṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato naarūpāvacaro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pañca, adhipatiyā pañca … pe … avigate pañca.
