Dhamma­pac­canīyā­nuloma (4), Tika­tika­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (5)

33 Micchat­ta­niyatat­tika, Kusalattika

Namicchat­ta­niyataṃ nakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā sammattaniyato kusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha … pe … avigate cha.

Namicchat­ta­niyataṃ naakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā micchat­ta­niyato akusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha … pe … avigate cha.

Namicchat­ta­niyataṃ naabyākataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca aniyato abyākato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha … pe … avigate cha.
