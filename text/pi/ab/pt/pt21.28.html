Dhamma­pac­canīyā­nuloma (4), Duka­tika­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (3)

28 Hetuduka, Parit­tā­ramma­ṇat­tika

Nahetuṃ naparit­tā­ramma­ṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu parittārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

Nahetuṃ nama­hagga­tā­rammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā hetu mahagga­tā­rammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

Nahetuṃ naappa­māṇā­rammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā hetu appa­māṇā­rammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.
