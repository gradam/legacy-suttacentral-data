Dham­mānu­loma­pac­canīya (3), Tika­duka­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (4)

16.81. Sani­dassa­nat­tika, Bhāva­nā­yapa­hātab­ba­hetu­ka­duka

Ani­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­ghaṃ bhāvanāya pahātab­ba­hetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naa­ni­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­gho nabhāvanāya pahātab­ba­hetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, ārammaṇe tīṇi … pe … avigate cha.

Ani­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­ghaṃ nabhāvanāya pahātab­ba­hetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasa­ni­dassa­na­sappa­ṭi­gho nanabhāvanāya pahātab­ba­hetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.
