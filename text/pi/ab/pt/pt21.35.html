Dhamma­pac­canīyā­nuloma (4), Duka­tika­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (3)

35 Hetuduka, Ajjhattattika

Nahetu naajjhatto dhammo hetussa ajjhattassa dhammassa āramma­ṇa­pac­ca­yena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Ārammaṇe nava, adhipatiyā upanissaye nava, purejāte atthiyā avigate tīṇi.

Nahetu nabahiddhā dhammo hetussa bahiddhā dhammassa āramma­ṇa­pac­ca­yena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Ārammaṇe nava, adhipatiyā upanissaye nava, purejāte atthiyā avigate tīṇi.

(Ajjhattattiko na labbhati paṭic­ca­vārā­dīsu.)
