Dham­mānu­loma­pac­canīya (3), Tika­duka­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (4)

60 Sani­dassa­nat­tika, Āsava­sam­payut­ta­duka

Ani­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­ghaṃ āsava­sam­payut­taṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naa­ni­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­gho naāsava­sam­payutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Ani­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­ghaṃ āsava­sam­payut­taṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasa­ni­dassa­na­sappa­ṭi­gho naāsava­sam­payutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … pe … ani­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­ghaṃ āsava­sam­payut­taṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasa­ni­dassa­na­sappa­ṭi­gho naāsava­sam­payutto ca naa­ni­dassa­na­sappa­ṭi­gho naāsava­sam­payutto ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, ārammaṇe tīṇi … pe … avigate cha.

Ani­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­ghaṃ āsava­vippa­yuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasa­ni­dassa­na­sappa­ṭi­gho naāsava­vippa­yutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.
