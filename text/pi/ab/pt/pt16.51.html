Dham­mānu­loma­pac­canīya (3), Tika­duka­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (4)

52 Sani­dassa­nat­tika, Sappaccayaduka

Ani­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­gho appaccayo dhammo ­nasani­dassa­na­sappa­ṭi­ghassa naappaccayassa dhammassa āramma­ṇa­pac­ca­yena paccayo.

Ārammaṇe tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi, upanissaye tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
