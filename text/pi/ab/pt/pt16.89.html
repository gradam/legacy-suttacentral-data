Dham­mānu­loma­pac­canīya (3), Tika­duka­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (4)

16.89 Sani­dassa­nat­tika, Rūpāvacaraduka

Ani­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­ghaṃ rūpāvacaraṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naa­ni­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­gho narūpāvacaro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha … pe … ārammaṇe tīṇi … pe … avigate cha.

Ani­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­ghaṃ narūpāvacaraṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasa­ni­dassa­na­sappa­ṭi­gho nanarūpāvacaro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.
