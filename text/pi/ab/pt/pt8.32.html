Paṭṭhānapakaraṇa

Dhamma­pac­canīya (2), Duka­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (2)

8.32. Oghadukādi

Nooghaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca … pe … noyogaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca … pe …. (Ogha­goc­cha­ka­yoga­goc­cha­kesu āmasanaṃ āsavagocchake āmasanasadisaṃ.)

Nonīvaraṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca … pe …. (Nīvara­ṇa­goc­chake āmasanaṃ sañño­jana­goc­chake āmasanasadisaṃ.)

Nīvara­ṇa­goc­cha­kaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
