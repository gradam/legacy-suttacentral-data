Dhammapaccanīya (2), Dukapaṭṭhānapāḷi (2)

32 Oghadukādi

Nooghaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca…pe…  noyogaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca…pe… . (Oghagocchakayogagocchakesu āmasanaṃ āsavagocchake āmasanasadisaṃ.)

Nīvaraṇagocchakaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
