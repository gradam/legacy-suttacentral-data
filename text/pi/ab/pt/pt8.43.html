Paṭṭhānapakaraṇa

Dhamma­pac­canīya (2), Duka­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (2)

8.43 Citta­saṃsaṭ­ṭha­sa­muṭṭhā­na­saha­bhū­duka

Nacitta­saṃsaṭ­ṭha­sa­muṭṭhā­na­saha­bhuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nacitta­saṃsaṭ­ṭha­sa­muṭṭhā­na­saha­bhū dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā …. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

Hetuyā nava … pe … avigate nava.
