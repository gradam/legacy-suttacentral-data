Dhammapaccanīyānuloma (4), Tikapaṭṭhānapāḷi (1)

9 Dassanahetuttika

9.1–⁠7 Paṭiccādivāra

Nadassanena pahātabbahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca dassanena pahātabbahetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
