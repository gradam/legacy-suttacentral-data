Dhamma­pac­canīyā­nuloma (4), Tika­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (1)

9 ­Dassa­na­hetut­tika

Nadassanena pahātab­ba­hetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca dassanena pahātab­ba­hetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

Hetuyā chabbīsa, ārammaṇe ekavīsa, adhipatiyā aṭṭhārasa … pe … vipāke tīṇi … pe … avigate chabbīsa.
