Dham­mānu­loma­pac­canīya (3), Tika­tika­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (5)

12 Kusalattika, Parit­tā­ramma­ṇat­tika

Kusalaṃ parit­tā­ramma­ṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nakusalo naparit­tā­rammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā nava, adhipatiyā nava … pe … aññamaññe tīṇi … pe … vipāke tīṇi … pe … avigate nava.

Kusalaṃ mahagga­tā­rammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nakusalo nama­hagga­tā­rammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā nava, adhipatiyā nava … pe … avigate nava.

Kusalaṃ appa­māṇā­rammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nakusalo naappa­māṇā­rammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha … pe … vipāke tīṇi … pe … avigate cha.
