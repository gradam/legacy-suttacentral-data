Dhammānulomapaccanīya (3), Dukatikapaṭṭhānapāḷi (3)

21 Hetuduka, Ajjhattārammaṇattika

Hetuṃ ajjhattārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu naajjhattārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Sabbattha tīṇi.)
