Dhamma­pac­canīyā­nuloma (4), Tika­tika­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (5)

29 Sekkhattika, Kusalattika

Nasekkhaṃ nakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā sekkho kusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha … pe … avigate cha.

Nasekkhaṃ naakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā neva­sekkha­nā­sekkho akusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

Nasekkhaṃ naabyākataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca neva­sekkha­nā­sekkho abyākato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pañca, adhipatiyā pañca … pe … avigate pañca.
