Dhamma­pac­canīya (2), Duka­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (2)

48 Upādā­na­goc­chaka

Naupādānaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naupādāno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

Hetuyā nava, vipāke ekaṃ … pe … avigate nava.
