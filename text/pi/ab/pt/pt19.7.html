Dhamma­pac­canīyā­nuloma (4), Tika­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (1)

7 Pītittika

Napīti­saha­gataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca pītisahagato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … cattāri.

Nasu­kha­saha­gataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca sukhasahagato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … cattāri.

Naupekkhā­saha­gataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca upekkhā­saha­gato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Naupekkhā­saha­gataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca pītisahagato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … cattāri. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

Hetuyā aṭṭhavīsa … pe … avigate aṭṭhavīsa.
