Dham­mānu­loma­pac­canīya (3), Tika­tika­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (5)

17.14 Kusalattika, Micchat­ta­niyatat­tika

Akusalaṃ micchat­ta­niyataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naakusalo namicchat­ta­niyato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

Kusalaṃ sam­mat­tani­ya­taṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nakusalo nasammat­ta­niyato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

Abyākataṃ aniyataṃ dhammaṃ paccayā naabyākato naaniyato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pañca … pe … avigate pañca.
