Dhamma­pac­canīyā­nuloma (4), Duka­tika­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (3)

21.54 Kilesadukādi, Sani­dassa­nat­tika

Nokilesaṃ ­nasani­dassa­na­sappa­ṭi­ghaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nokileso sani­dassa­na­sappa­ṭi­gho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi … pe … vipāke ekaṃ … pe … avigate tīṇi.
