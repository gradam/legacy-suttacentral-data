Dham­mānu­loma­pac­canīya (3), Tika­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (1)

13.9. ­Dassa­na­hetut­tika

Dassanena pahātab­ba­hetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nadassanena pahātab­ba­hetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

Hetuyā chabbīsa, ārammaṇe ekavīsa, adhipatiyā ekūnavīsa … pe … vipāke tīṇi … pe … avigate chabbīsa. (Vitthāretabbaṃ.)
