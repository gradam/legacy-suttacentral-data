Dhammapaccanīya (2), Tikapaṭṭhānapāḷi (1)

16 Maggārammaṇattika

16.1--7 Paṭiccādivāra

Namaggārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca…pe…  namaggahetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca…pe… namaggādhipatiṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca… . (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
