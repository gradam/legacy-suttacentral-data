Dham­mānu­loma­pac­canīya (3), Duka­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (2)

14.7 Sappac­caya­dukādi

Sappaccayaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naappaccayo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

Hetuyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.

Sappaccayo dhammo naappaccayassa dhammassa āramma­ṇa­pac­ca­yena paccayo.

Appaccayo dhammo naappaccayassa dhammassa āramma­ṇa­pac­ca­yena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

Hetuyā ekaṃ, ārammaṇe dve, adhipatiyā dve … pe … upanissaye dve … pe … avigate ekaṃ. (Saṅkhataṃ sappac­caya­sadisaṃ.)
