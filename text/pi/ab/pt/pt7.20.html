Dhammapaccanīya (2), Tikapaṭṭhānapāḷi (1)

20 Ajjhattattika

20.1–⁠7 Paṭiccādivāra

Naajjhattaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naajjhatto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā.

Hetuyā dve…pe…  avigate dve.
