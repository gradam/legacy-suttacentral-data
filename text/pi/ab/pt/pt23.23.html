Dhamma­pac­canīyā­nuloma (4), Tika­tika­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (5)

23 Saṃ­kiliṭ­ṭhat­tika, Kusalattika

Nasaṃ­kiliṭ­ṭha­saṃki­lesikaṃ nakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā asaṃ­kiliṭ­ṭha­saṃki­lesiko kusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha … pe … avigate cha.

Nasaṃ­kiliṭ­ṭha­saṃki­lesikaṃ naakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā saṃ­kiliṭ­ṭha­saṃki­lesiko akusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

Nasaṃ­kiliṭ­ṭha­saṃki­lesikaṃ naabyākataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca asaṃ­kiliṭ­ṭha­saṃki­lesiko abyākato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha … pe … avigate cha.
