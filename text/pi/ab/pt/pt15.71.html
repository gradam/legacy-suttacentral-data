Dham­mānu­loma­pac­canīya (3), Duka­tika­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (3)

72 Saraṇaduka, Vipākattika

Araṇaṃ vipākaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo navipāko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.

Saraṇaṃ vipā­ka­dham­ma­dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo ­navi­pā­ka­dham­ma­dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Araṇaṃ vipā­ka­dham­ma­dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo ­navi­pā­ka­dham­ma­dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā dve … pe … avigate dve.

Araṇaṃ neva­vipāka­navi­pā­ka­dham­ma­dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo naneva­vipāka­navi­pā­ka­dham­ma­dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.
