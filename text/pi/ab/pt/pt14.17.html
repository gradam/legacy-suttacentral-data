Dham­mānu­loma­pac­canīya (3), Duka­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (2)

17 Āsava­āsava­sam­payut­ta­duka

Āsavañceva āsava­sam­payut­tañca dhammaṃ paṭicca naāsavo ceva naāsava­vippa­yutto ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi.

Āsava­sam­payut­tañ­ceva no ca āsavaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naāsava­vippa­yutto ceva nano ca āsavo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi.

Āsavañceva āsava­sam­payut­tañca āsava­vippa­yuttañ­ceva no ca āsavañca dhammaṃ paṭicca noāsavo ceva naāsava­vippa­yutto ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

Hetuyā nava. (Sabbattha nava.)
