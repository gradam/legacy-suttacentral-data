Dham­mānu­loma­pac­canīya (3), Duka­tika­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (3)

78 Saraṇaduka, ­Dassa­na­hetut­tika

Saraṇaṃ dassanena pahātab­ba­hetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo nadassanena pahātab­ba­hetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṃ, ārammaṇe ekaṃ … pe … sahajāte tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

Saraṇaṃ bhāvanāya pahātab­ba­hetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasaraṇo nabhāvanāya pahātab­ba­hetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṃ, ārammaṇe ekaṃ … pe … sahajāte tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

Araṇaṃ nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātab­ba­hetukaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naaraṇo naneva­dassa­nena nabhāvanāya pahātab­ba­hetuko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Hetuyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.
