Dhamma­pac­canīya (2), Duka­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (2)

44 Cit­ta­saṃsaṭ­ṭha­sa­muṭṭhā­nānupa­rivat­ti­duka

Nacit­ta­saṃsaṭ­ṭha­sa­muṭṭhā­nānupa­rivat­tiṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nacitta­saṃsaṭ­ṭha­sa­muṭṭhā­nā­nupari­vatti dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā …. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

Hetuyā nava … pe … purejāte āsevane pañca … pe … avigate nava. (Sabbattha vitthāro.)
