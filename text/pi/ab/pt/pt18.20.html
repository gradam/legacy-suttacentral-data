Paṭṭhānapakaraṇa

Dham­mānu­loma­pac­canīya (3), Duka­duka­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (6)

18.20. Āsava­vippa­yutta­sāsava­duka, Hetuduka

Āsava­vippa­yuttaṃ sāsavaṃ hetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca āsava­vippa­yutto naanāsavo nahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … ekaṃ.

Āsava­vippa­yuttaṃ anāsavaṃ hetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca āsava­vippa­yutto naanāsavo nahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cattāri, ārammaṇe dve … pe … avigate cattāri.

Āsava­vippa­yuttaṃ sāsavaṃ nahetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca āsava­vippa­yutto naanāsavo nanahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Āsava­vippa­yuttaṃ anāsavaṃ nahetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca āsava­vippa­yutto nasāsavo nanahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā dve … pe … avigate dve.
