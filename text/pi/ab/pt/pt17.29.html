Dham­mānu­loma­pac­canīya (3), Tika­tika­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (5)

29 Sani­dassa­nat­tika, Vipākattika

Ani­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­ghaṃ vipākaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naa­ni­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­gho navipāko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha … pe … aññamaññe tīṇi … pe … avigate cha.

Ani­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­ghaṃ vipā­ka­dham­ma­dhammaṃ paṭicca naa­ni­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­gho ­navi­pā­ka­dham­ma­dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha … pe … avigate cha.

Ani­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­ghaṃ neva­vipāka­navi­pā­ka­dham­ma­dhammaṃ paṭicca nasa­ni­dassa­na­sappa­ṭi­gho naneva­vipāka­navi­pā­ka­dham­ma­dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.
