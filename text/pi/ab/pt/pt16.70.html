Dham­mānu­loma­pac­canīya (3), Tika­duka­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (4)

71 Sani­dassa­nat­tika, Citta­saha­bhū­duka

Ani­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­ghaṃ cittasahabhuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naa­ni­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­gho nocittasahabhū dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

Hetuyā cha, ārammaṇe tīṇi … pe … avigate cha.
