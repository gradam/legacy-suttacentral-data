Paṭṭhānapakaraṇa

Dhamma­pac­canīyā­nuloma (4), Duka­tika­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (3)

21.21 Hetuduka, Vitakkattika

Nahetuṃ nasavitak­ka­sa­vicāraṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu savitak­ka­savi­cāro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

Nahetuṃ naa­vitak­ka­vicāra­mattaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu avitak­ka­vicāra­matto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

Nahetuṃ naa­vitak­ka­a­vicāraṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu avitak­ka­avi­cāro dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.
