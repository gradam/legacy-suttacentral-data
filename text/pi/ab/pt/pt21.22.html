Paṭṭhānapakaraṇa

Dhamma­pac­canīyā­nuloma (4), Duka­tika­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (3)

21.22 Hetuduka, Pītittika

Nahetuṃ napīti­saha­gataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu pītisahagato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

Nahetuṃ nasu­kha­saha­gataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu sukhasahagato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

Nahetuṃ naupekkhā­saha­gataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu upekkhā­saha­gato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.
