Dham­mānu­loma­pac­canīya (3), Tika­duka­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (4)

66 Sani­dassa­nat­tika, Cittaduka

Ani­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­ghaṃ cittaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naa­ni­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­gho nocitto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Ani­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­ghaṃ cittaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasa­ni­dassa­na­sappa­ṭi­gho nocitto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … pe … ani­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­ghaṃ cittaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasa­ni­dassa­na­sappa­ṭi­gho nocitto ca naa­ni­dassa­na­sappa­ṭi­gho nocitto ca dhammā uppajjanti hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, ārammaṇe tīṇi … pe … avigate cha.

Ani­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­ghaṃ nocittaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasa­ni­dassa­na­sappa­ṭi­gho nanocitto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.
