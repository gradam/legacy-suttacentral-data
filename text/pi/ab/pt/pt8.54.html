Dhamma­pac­canīya (2), Duka­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (2)

54 Kilesa­saṃ­kiliṭ­ṭha­duka

Nakilesañceva naasaṃ­kiliṭ­ṭhañca dhammaṃ paṭicca nakileso ceva naasaṃkiliṭṭho ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi.

Naasaṃ­kiliṭ­ṭhañ­ceva nano ca kilesaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naasaṃkiliṭṭho ceva nano ca kileso dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi.

Nakilesañceva naasaṃ­kiliṭ­ṭhañca naasaṃ­kiliṭ­ṭhañ­ceva nano ca kilesañca dhammaṃ paṭicca nakileso ceva naasaṃkiliṭṭho ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … tīṇi. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

Hetuyā nava … pe … avigate nava.
