Dham­mānu­loma­pac­canīya (3), Tika­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (1)

21 Ajjhat­tā­ramma­ṇat­tika

Ajjhat­tā­ramma­ṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naaj­jhat­tā­rammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā.

Hetuyā cha, ārammaṇe dve … pe … avigate cha.
