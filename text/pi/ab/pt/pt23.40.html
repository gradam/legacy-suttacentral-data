Paṭṭhānapakaraṇa

Dhamma­pac­canīyā­nuloma (4), Tika­tika­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (5)

23.40 Sani­dassa­nat­tika, Vedanāttika

­Nasani­dassa­na­sappa­ṭi­ghaṃ nasukhāya vedanāya sampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca ani­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­gho sukhāya vedanāya sampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

­Nasani­dassa­na­sappa­ṭi­ghaṃ nadukkhāya vedanāya sampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca ani­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­gho dukkhāya vedanāya sampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

­Nasani­dassa­na­sappa­ṭi­ghaṃ naa­duk­kha­ma­su­khāya vedanāya sampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca ani­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­gho aduk­kha­ma­su­khāya vedanāya sampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.
