Dhamma­pac­canīya (2), Duka­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (2)

38 Cit­ta­saṃsaṭ­ṭha­duka

Nacit­ta­saṃsaṭ­ṭhaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nacitta­saṃsaṭ­ṭho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā … (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

Hetuyā nava … pe … avigate nava.
