Dhamma­pac­canīyā­nuloma (4), Duka­tika­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (3)

33 Hetuduka, Atītattika

Nahetu napaccuppanno dhammo hetussa paccuppannassa dhammassa āramma­ṇa­pac­ca­yena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

Ārammaṇe nava, adhipatiyā nava, anantare samanantare nava, upanissaye nava, natthiyā vigate nava.
