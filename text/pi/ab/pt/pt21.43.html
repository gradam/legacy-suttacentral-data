Paṭṭhānapakaraṇa

Dhamma­pac­canīyā­nuloma (4), Duka­tika­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (3)

21.43 Rūpīduka, Sani­dassa­nat­tika

Narūpiṃ ­nasani­dassa­na­sappa­ṭi­ghaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca rūpī sani­dassa­na­sappa­ṭi­gho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

Narūpiṃ naani­dassa­na­sappa­ṭi­ghaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca rūpī ani­dassa­na­sappa­ṭi­gho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi … pe … aññamaññe ekaṃ … pe … avigate tīṇi.

Naarūpiṃ naani­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­ghaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca rūpī ani­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­gho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekaṃ, adhipatiyā ekaṃ … pe … avigate ekaṃ.
