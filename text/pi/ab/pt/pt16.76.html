Dham­mānu­loma­pac­canīya (3), Tika­duka­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (4)

77 Sani­dassa­nat­tika, Upādānadukādi

Ani­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­ghaṃ upādānaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naa­ni­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­gho noupādāno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

Hetuyā cha, ārammaṇe tīṇi … pe … avigate cha.

Ani­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­ghaṃ kilesaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naa­ni­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­gho nokileso dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

Hetuyā cha, ārammaṇe tīṇi … pe … avigate cha.
