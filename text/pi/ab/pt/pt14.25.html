Dhammānulomapaccanīya (3), Dukapaṭṭhānapāḷi (2)

25 Kilesagocchaka

Kilesaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nokileso dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

Hetuyā nava … pe … vipāke ekaṃ … pe … avigate nava.
