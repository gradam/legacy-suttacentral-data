Paṭṭhānapakaraṇa

Dham­mānu­loma­pac­canīya (3), Tika­duka­paṭṭhā­na­pāḷi (4)

16.50 Sani­dassa­nat­tika, Hetu­hetu­sam­payut­ta­duka

Ani­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­ghaṃ hetuñceva hetu­sam­payut­tañca dhammaṃ paṭicca nasa­ni­dassa­na­sappa­ṭi­gho nahetu ceva nahetu­vippa­yutto ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.

Ani­dassa­na­ap­paṭi­ghaṃ hetu­sam­payut­tañ­ceva na ca hetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nasa­ni­dassa­na­sappa­ṭi­gho nahetu­vippa­yutto ceva nanahetu ca dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi … pe … avigate tīṇi.
