7 Kilesagocchaka

Nakilesaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca kileso dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

Hetuyā nava … pe … vipāke ekaṃ … pe … avigate nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
