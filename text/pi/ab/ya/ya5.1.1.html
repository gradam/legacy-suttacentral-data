Saccayamaka

1 Paṇṇat­ti­vāra­uddesa

Namo tassa Bhagavato Arahato Sammā­sambud­dhassa.

Cattāri saccāni—dukkhasaccaṃ, samudayasaccaṃ nirodhasaccaṃ, maggasaccaṃ.
