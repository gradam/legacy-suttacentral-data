Khandhayamaka

1 Paṇṇattivārauddesa

Namo tassa Bhagavato Arahato Sammāsambuddhassa.

Pañcakkhandhā—  rūpakkhandho, vedanākkhandho, saññākkhandho, saṅkhārakkhandho, viññāṇakkhandho.
