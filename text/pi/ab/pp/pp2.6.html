Niddesa

6 Chakka­pugga­la­paññatti

Tatra yvāyaṃ puggalo pubbe ananussutesu dhammesu sāmaṃ saccāni abhisam­buj­jhati, tattha ca sabbaññutaṃ pāpuṇāti balesu ca vasībhāvaṃ, sammāsambuddho tena daṭṭhabbo. (1)

Tatra yvāyaṃ puggalo pubbe ananussutesu dhammesu sāmaṃ saccāni abhisam­buj­jhati, na ca tattha sabbaññutaṃ pāpuṇāti na ca balesu vasībhāvaṃ, pac­ce­ka­sambud­dho tena daṭṭhabbo. (2)

Tatra yvāyaṃ puggalo pubbe ananussutesu dhammesu sāmaṃ saccāni anabhi­sambuj­jhati, diṭṭheva dhamme duk­khas­santa­karo hoti, sāvaka­pāramiñca pāpuṇāti, sāri­putta­mog­gallānā tena daṭṭhabbā. (3)

Tatra yvāyaṃ puggalo pubbe ananussutesu dhammesu sāmaṃ saccāni anabhi­sambuj­jhati, diṭṭheva dhamme duk­khas­santa­karo hoti, na ca sāvakapāramiṃ pāpuṇāti, avasesā arahantā tena daṭṭhabbā. (4)

Tatra yvāyaṃ puggalo pubbe ananussutesu dhammesu sāmaṃ saccāni anabhi­sambuj­jhati, na ca diṭṭheva dhamme duk­khas­santa­karo hoti, anāgāmī hoti anāgantā itthattaṃ, anāgāmī tena daṭṭhabbo. (5)

Tatra yvāyaṃ puggalo pubbe ananussutesu dhammesu sāmaṃ saccāni anabhi­sambuj­jhati, na ca diṭṭheva dhamme duk­khas­santa­karo hoti, āgantā itthattaṃ, sotā­panna­sa­kadā­gāmino tena daṭṭhabbā. (6)

Chakkaniddeso.
