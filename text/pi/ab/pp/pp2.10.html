­Pugga­la­paññatti

Niddesa

10 Dasaka­pugga­la­paññatti

Katamesaṃ pañcannaṃ idha niṭṭhā? Sattak­khat­tu­paramassa, kolaṃkolassa, ekabījissa, sakadāgāmissa, yo ca diṭṭheva dhamme arahā—imesaṃ pañcannaṃ idha niṭṭhā. (1–‍5)

Katamesaṃ pañcannaṃ idha vihāya niṭṭhā? Antarā­pari­nib­bā­yissa, upahac­ca­pari­nib­bā­yissa, asaṅ­khā­ra­pari­nib­bā­yissa, sasaṅ­khā­ra­pari­nib­bā­yissa, uddhaṃsotassa akaniṭ­ṭha­gāmino—imesaṃ pañcannaṃ idha vihāya niṭṭhāti. (6–‍10)

Ettāvatā puggalānaṃ ­pugga­la­paññat­tīti.

Dasakaniddeso.

­Pugga­la­paññat­ti­pakara­ṇaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
