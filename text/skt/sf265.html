Satyasūtra

śrāvastyāṃ nidānam |

ye kecid bhikṣavaḥ samyag eva śraddhayā agārād anagārikāṃ pravrajaṃti sarve te caturṇāṃ āryasatyānāṃ jñānāya |

katameṣāṃ caturṇām |

duḥkham āryasatyasya duḥkhasamudayasya duḥkhanirodhasya duḥkhanirodhagāminī pratipad āryasatyasya jñānāya |

tasmāt tarhi caturṇām āryasatyānām anabhisamitānām abhisamayāya atimātraṃ tīvracchando vyāyāmaś cotsudhiś cāprativāṇiś ca smṛtyā saṃprajanyena ca yogaḥ karaṇīyaḥ |

katameṣāṃ caturṇām |

duḥkham āryasatyasya duḥkhasamudayasya duḥkhanirodhagāminī pratipad āryasatyasya iti |

… o … ai … r … satya sūtraṃ samāptaḥ |

rakṣaṃ bhavatu || … syasya svāhā ||

Waldschmidt, Ernst 1959a. Kleine Brahmi-Schriftrolle. NAWG 1959/1: 1–25. Reprinted in VCBT, 371–395.
