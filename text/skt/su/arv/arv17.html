Arthaviniścayasūtram

17. Pañca Balāni

Tatra bhikṣavaḥ katamāni pañca balāni?

Yad-uta:

  1. Śraddhā-balam |
  2. vīrya-balam |
  3. smṛti-balam |
  4. samādhi-balam |
  5. prajñā-balam ||

Imāni bhikṣavaḥ pañca balāni ||
