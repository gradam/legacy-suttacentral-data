24: Four Realizations of a Tathāgata

katamāstathāgatasya catasraḥ pratisaṃvidaḥ? yaduta arthapratisaṃvit dharmapratisaṃvit niruktipratisaṃvit pratibhānapratisaṃvit | imā bhikṣavaścatasraḥ pratisaṃvidaḥ ||
