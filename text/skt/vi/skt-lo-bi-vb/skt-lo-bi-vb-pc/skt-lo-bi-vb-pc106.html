pācattika-dharma 106

anuvarṣam

Origin story

bhagavān śrāvastīyam viharati | tā dāni bhikṣuṇīyo anuvarṣam upasthāpayanti | tāsāṁ dāni kaukṛtyaṁ | kin nu khalv etaṁ labhyaṁ kartuṁ | utāho na labhyaṁ | peyālaṁ | yāvad bhagavān āha | tena hi na kṣamati anu-varṣam upasthāpayituṁ | atha khalu vīciḥ kartavyā |

Ruling

yā puna bhikṣuṇī anu-varṣam upasthāpayet pācattikaṁ |

Definitions

  • yā puna bhikṣuṇī ti upasampannā |
  • anu-varṣan ti anu-samvatsaraṁ |
  • upasthāpayed iti upasthāpayet

pācattikaṁ | yāvat prajñaptiḥ |

Permutations

sā eṣā bhikṣuṇī anu-varṣam upasthāpayeti pācattikaṁ | ekaṁ varṣam upasthāpayitavyaṁ | apara-varṣe vīciḥ kartavyā | atha dāni bhikṣuṇī kṛtapuṇyā bhavati ekaṁ varṣaṁ śikṣā deśayitavyā | aparaṁ varṣam upasthāpayitavyā | kiñcāpi bhikṣuḥ anu-varṣam upasthāpayed an-āpattiḥ | tena bhagavān āha |

Restatement of rule

yā puna bhikṣuṇī anu-varṣam upasthāpayet pācattikam || 6 ||
