uddānam |
na eka ekena na dvau na saṃbahulāḥ kṛtāḥ |
na gaṇo gaṇasya karmāṇi paṃca saṃghakarmaṇāṃ svāminaḥ ||

api tu bhikṣavaḥ paṃca saṃghakarmaṇāṃ svāminaḥ | katame paṃca | catvāro bhikṣavaḥ saṃghaḥ | pāṃcāpi bhikṣavaḥ saṃghaḥ | daśa bhikṣavaḥ saṃ­ghaḥ | viṃśatir bhikṣavaḥ saṃghaḥ uttare ca | paṃca saṃghāḥ |

caturvargakaraṇīyaṃ pudgala ūnakaḥ kṛtaḥ |
