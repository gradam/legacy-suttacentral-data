5. Analysis Of The Faculties

5.1. Analysis According To Abhidhamma

The twenty-two faculties are: The faculty of eye, faculty of ear, faculty of nose, faculty of tongue, faculty of body, faculty of mind, faculty of femininity, faculty of masculinity, faculty of vital principle, faculty of pleasure (physical), faculty of pain (physical), faculty of mental pleasure, faculty of mental pain, faculty of indifference, faculty of confidence, faculty of energy, faculty of mindfulness, faculty of samādhi, faculty of wisdom, faculty of “I am knowing the unknown”, faculty of knowing, faculty of one who has known fully.

Therein what is faculty of eye? That eye which, deriving from the four great essentials, is sensitive surface, See section 156. this is an empty village. This is called faculty of eye. (1)

Therein what is faculty of ear. Complete appropriately. Faculty of nose. Complete as first example Faculty of tongue. Complete as first example Faculty of body? That body which, deriving from the four great essentials, is sensitive surface, See section 160. this is an empty village. This is called faculty of body. (5)

Therein what is faculty of mind? Controlling faculty of mind by way of singlefold division: Is associated with contact. Faculty of mind by way of twofold division: Is accompanied by root; is not accompanied by root. Faculty of mind by way of threefold division: Is skilful; is unskilful; is neither-skilful-nor-unskilful. Faculty of mind by way of fourfold division: Is characteristic of the plane of desire; is characteristic of the plane of form; is characteristic of the formless plane; is not included (i.e. is supramundane). Faculty of mind by way of fivefold division: Is associated with the faculty of pleasure (physical); is associated with the faculty of pain (physical); is associated with the faculty of mental pleasure; is associated with the faculty of mental pain; is associated with the faculty of indifference. Faculty of mind by way of sixfold division: Eye consciousness, Intermediate senses. mind consciousness. Thus is faculty of mind by way of sixfold division.

Faculty of mind by way of sevenfold division: Eye consciousness; Intermediate senses. body consciousness; mind element; mind-consciousness-element. Thus is faculty of mind by way of sevenfold division.

Faculty of mind by way of eightfold division: Eye consciousness; Intermediate senses. body consciousness that is accompanied by pleasure, that is accompanied by pain; mind element; mind-consciousness-element. Thus is faculty of mind by way of eightfold division.

Faculty of mind by way of ninefold division: Eye consciousness; Intermediate senses. body consciousness; mind element; mind-consciousness-element that is skilful, that is unskilful, that is neither-skilful-nor-unskilful. Thus is faculty of mind by way of ninefold division.

Faculty of mind by way of tenfold division: Eye consciousness; Intermediate senses. body consciousness that is accompanied by pleasure, that is accompanied by pain; mind element; mind-consciousness-element that is skilful, that is unskilful, that is neither-skilful-nor-unskilful. Thus is faculty of mind by way of tenfold division, See sections 122–149 inclusive. Thus is faculty of mind by way of manifold division. This is called faculty of mind. (6)

Therein what is faculty of femininity? That which in woman is feminine appearance, feminine characteristic, feminine behaviour, feminine deportment, femininity, being feminine. This is called faculty of femininity. (7)

Therein what is faculty of masculinity? That which in man is masculine appearance, masculine characteristic, masculine behaviour, masculine deportment, masculinity, being masculine. This is called faculty of masculinity. (8)

Therein what is faculty of vital principle? Faculty of vital principle by way of twofold division: Is faculty of material vital principle; is faculty of non-material vital principle.

Therein what is faculty of material vital principle? That which in these material things is life, stability, going on, sustaining, progressing, continuance, preservation, vital principle, faculty of vital principle. This is called faculty of material vital principle.

Therein what is faculty of non-material vital principle? That which in these non-material dhammas is life, stability, going on, sustaining, progressing, continuance, preservation, vital principle, faculty of vital principle. This is called faculty of non-material vital principle. This is called faculty of vital principle. (9)

Therein what is faculty of pleasure? That which is bodily ease; bodily pleasure; easeful pleasant experience born of bodily contact; easeful pleasant feeling born of bodily contact. This is called faculty of pleasure. (10)

Therein what is faculty of pain? That which is bodily uneasiness; bodily pain; uneasy painful experience born of bodily contact; uneasy painful feeling born of bodily contact. This is called faculty of pain. (11)

Therein what is faculty of mental pleasure? That which is mental ease; mental pleasure; easeful pleasant experience born of mental contact; easeful pleasant feeling born of mental contact. This is called faculty of mental pleasure. (12)

Therein what is faculty of mental pain? That which is mental uneasiness; mental pain; uneasy painful experience born of mental contact; uneasy painful feeling born of mental contact. This is called faculty of mental pain. (13)

Therein what is faculty of indifference? That which is neither mental ease nor uneasiness; neither painful nor pleasant experience born of mental contact; neither painful nor pleasant feeling born of mental contact. This is called faculty of indifference. (14)

Therein what is faculty of confidence? That which is confidence, being confident, trust, implicit faith, confidence, faculty of confidence, power of confidence. This is called faculty of confidence. (15)

Therein what is faculty of energy? That which is the arousing of mental energy, toiling, endeavour, aspiring, effort, zeal, perseverance, vigour, stability, unfaltering endeavour, not relinquishing wish, not relinquishing the task, firm hold of the task, energy, faculty of energy, power of energy. This is called faculty of energy. (16)

Therein what is faculty of mindfulness? That which is mindfulness, constant mindfulness, recollection, mindfulness, act of remembering, bearing in mind, non-superficiality, non-forgetfulness, mindfulness, faculty of mindfulness, power of mindfulness, right mindfulness. This is called faculty of mindfulness. (17)

Therein what is faculty of samādhi? That which is stability, steadfastness, firmness of consciousness; non-distraction, non-wavering, mental imperturbability, calmness, faculty of samādhi, power of samādhi, right samādhi. This is called faculty of samādhi. (18)

Therein what is faculty of wisdom? That which is wisdom, understanding, See section 525. absence of delusion, truth investigation, right view. This is called faculty of wisdom. (19)

Therein what is faculty of initial awakening? That which for the realization of those dhammas that are not known, not comprehended, not attained, not discerned, not realized, is wisdom, understanding, See section 525. absence of delusion, truth investigation, right view, truth investigation-awakening-factor, path constituent, included in the path. This is called faculty of initial awakening. (20)

Therein what is faculty of intermediate awakening? That which for the realization of those dhammas that are known, comprehended, attained, discerned, realized, is wisdom, understanding, See section 525. absence of delusion, truth investigation, right view, truth investigation-awakening-factor, path constituent, included in the path. This is called faculty of intermediate awakening. (21)

Therein what is faculty of final awakening? That which for the realization of those dhammas that are known fully, comprehended, attained, discerned, realized, is wisdom, understanding, See section 525. absence of delusion, truth investigation, right view, truth investigation-awakening-factor, path constituent, included in the path. This is called faculty of final awakening. (22)
