Theravāda Vinayapiṭaka

Parivāra

2.13. Nuns’ Analysis: on Origins

Because of consenting to physical contact of the six origins of offences by how many origins do the offences … originate? see Prv.2.5 … Because of partaking of curds, having had them asked for … and by mind.

Concluded is the Fifth Chapter: on Origins
