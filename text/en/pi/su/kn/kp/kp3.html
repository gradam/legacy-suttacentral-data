Short Readings 3

The Thirty Two Fold Nature

There are in this body: hairs of the head, body hairs, nails, teeth, skin; flesh, sinews, bones, bone-marrow, kidneys; heart, liver, pleura, spleen, lungs; intestines, mesentery, undigested food, excrement; bile, phlegm, pus, blood, sweat, fat; tears, grease, spit, mucus, synovial fluid, urine; and the brain in the head.

Translated by Bhikkhu Ānandajoti.

The text and translation, together with detailed notes and discussions, may be found in multiple formats on the translator’s website, Ancient Buddhist Texts.
