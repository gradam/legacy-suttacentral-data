Stories of the Buddha’s Former Births

Book 1 Ekanipāta

111. Gadrabha-Panha

“Thou think’st thyself a swan.”—This Question as to the Ass will also be set out at length in the Ummagga-jataka. This is the end of the Question as to the Ass.
