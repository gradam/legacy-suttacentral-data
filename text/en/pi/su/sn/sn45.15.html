Saṃyutta Nikāya 45

Connected Discourses on the Path

15. Arising (2)

At Savatthī. “Bhikkhus, these eight things, developed and cultivated, if unarisen do not arise apart from the Discipline of a Fortunate One. What eight? Right view ... right concentration. These eight things….”
