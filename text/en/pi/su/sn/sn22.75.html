Saṃyutta Nikāya 22

Connected Discourses on the Aggregates

75. Origin (2)

At Savatthi. “Bhikkhus, the instructed noble disciple understands as it really is the origin and the passing away, the gratification, the danger, and the escape in the case of form, feeling, perception, volitional formations, and consciousness.”
